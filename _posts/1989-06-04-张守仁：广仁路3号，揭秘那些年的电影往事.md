<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">

<p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>

<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   烟台山下。从滨海广场走到广仁路步行街，有幢两层高的小楼，我从两扇斑驳沧桑的铁门缝隙里，看到里面一片狼藉。这里是我融入芝罘这座城市的起点。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   小楼昨夜又东风
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
    ，只是不见昔日的辉煌。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   这座小楼是广仁路
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    3
   </span>
   号，前身是德国海员俱乐部。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1951
   </span>
   年
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    2
   </span>
   月，莱阳专署电影队（站）在莱阳城东关吴格庄村专署院里诞生，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1958
   </span>
   年
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    8
   </span>
   月，它跟随莱阳专署迁至烟台市（芝罘）的脚步，在四马路
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    2
   </span>
   号落脚，后搬至朝阳街
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    54
   </span>
   号。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1965
   </span>
   年
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    5
   </span>
   月
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    8
   </span>
   日，在这座小楼里定居，挂着
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   山东省烟台专署电影发行放映公司
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   的牌子。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我于
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1971
   </span>
   年初在县城参加电影工作，经常到小楼里开会或取送影片，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1978
   </span>
   年
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    10
   </span>
   月更成为小楼里的一员。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   小楼是胶东半岛的电影中心，就像蝴蝶效应，有时这里的一个举动，就会首先在芝罘，继而在烟台地区
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    18
   </span>
   个县市的观众中引起轰动。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
   1
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   几回回梦回广仁路，几回回在小楼的院子里徘徊，却怎么也走不出去。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   小楼过道的东两间屋是电影发行调度科，这里像渤海湾，时常
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   风起浪涌
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1965
   </span>
   年
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    5
   </span>
   月，接上面紧急通知，发行科连夜调配《夺印》、《箭杆河边》等
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    10
   </span>
   部影片，配合莱阳、荣成等
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    5
   </span>
   县的
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   四清
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   运动。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1966
   </span>
   年至
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1967
   </span>
   年，这里不断接到上面查封
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   毒草
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   影片的电报，近百部影片片盒被贴上封条。但有时还得按照上面的指令，将《燎原》、《清宫秘史》等
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    17
   </span>
   部
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   反动
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   影片，分批在新中国、大光明影院举行
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   示众
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   批判专场，片头打出
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   内部观看，专供批判
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   的字样，观众潮水般涌来。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1966
   </span>
   年底，小楼的职工载歌载舞迎来《毛主席和百万文化大军在一起》等纪录片，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    30
   </span>
   多部裹着红布的拷贝被各地
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   请
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   到影院，芝罘
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    3
   </span>
   家影院昼夜连轴转，买票的长龙还是伸向马路。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   在这之后的
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    10
   </span>
   年间，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    8
   </span>
   部样板戏影片以汇映周（月）或展映季等形式，在各地轮番上映。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1975
   </span>
   年，发行科收到一封观众匿名来信，抱怨
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   国产片新闻简报，朝鲜片又哭又笑，越南片飞机大炮，罗马（尼亚）片搂搂抱抱
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   。小楼的领导肖经理把信丢进了火炉里。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1977
   </span>
   年，发行小屋又开始接到封存影片
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   解冻
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   的电报、电话，一批又一批的解禁片、内部片和香港特供片堆满了片库，各地发行员、经理和芝罘区几十家厂矿俱乐部放映员，每日都把于调度员里三层外三层地围起来。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   还有让于调度更上火的事儿：这天刚上班就接到肖经理的电话，命《简
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ·
   </span>
   爱》影片停映。于辩白了几句，肖丢下一句话：
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   简单的爱也不行！这是领导说的话！
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">

</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   《简
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ·
   </span>
   爱》从芝罘下线后，于调度又接到即墨影院经理的电话，说：
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   青岛上映《神秘的大佛》，我们县为什么不能？
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   最后还说：
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   还我
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ‘
   </span>
   大佛
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ’
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
    ！
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   不用说，这部影片在烟台地区被
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   封
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
    杀
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   了。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1979
   </span>
   年春，中影公司在交际处宾馆召开全国会议，总经理在讲话中说：
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   山东的票房，济南市第一吧？
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   肖经理举手示意：
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   不是！
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”“
   </span>
   青岛？
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   肖站起来回答：
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   烟台！
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   这个时期，烟台地区的票房收入七八百万元，上缴国家税金近四百万元。小楼的主人成为全国电影发行业的一面红旗。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
   2
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   发行屋的东南角坐的是与我同宿舍的李哥，负责农村影片发行，人称大先生。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   这天，肖经理会前向全区农村发行科长大讲李哥的故事：挨饿那年，他在东县的饭店里买了一盘包子，还未下口，一位叫
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   彪弟
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   的大姑娘盯着他流口水，他把一盘包子全给了她，姑娘边吃边哭着说：
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   大叔，你不
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ……”
   </span>
   肖经理还没讲完，李哥一步闯进来，全场哄堂大笑。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我从这亦哭亦笑的故事里看到了李哥的热心肠，他也从此成为我这位乡下人立足芝罘城的第一个良师益友。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   李哥参加工作时在农村电影队赶马车，深知农民渴求电影的心情，他一生用心为农民服务。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   上世纪五六十年代，他一人负责全区
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    100
   </span>
   多个农村电影队的影片供应，到了七八十年代，还是他一个人，这时农村电影队猛增到
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    3000
   </span>
   多个，其中村办队
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    2600
   </span>
   多个。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   乡间的夜晚，集镇影院像一颗颗明珠闪烁在千里胶东，而星罗棋布的村电影队，又似天上的繁星跌落人间。李哥每月为这些农村电影院（队）调度数百部影片节目和拷贝，从未有过差错。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<img alt="113.jpg" border="0" height="333" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5743/113.jpg" width="500"/>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1976
   </span>
   年春，他在奇山宾馆主持召开农村电影工作座谈会，有一个发行员说，他们县有一个队，腊月天飘着小清雪在街头放映钢琴伴奏的《红灯记》，观众廖落，最后只剩下一位老翁。李哥上前紧紧抓住那位发行员的手说：
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   我们要向这位农民兄弟致敬！会后领我去见见他！
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   发行员说：
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   他等着拿他家的桌子呢。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   李哥的脸
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   刷
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   地一下红了。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   他从此不断改进排片方法，每日放映双片，最后放映的必是一部农民喜爱的影片。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   李哥创造了第一等的工作成绩，省内外有一百多家地市公司的同行来小楼取经学习。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
   3
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   小楼二楼西面的
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    6
   </span>
   间屋是看片室，只要院中的电铃连响
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    30
   </span>
   秒钟，各屋里的人都会到这里
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   业务看片
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
    ，有时是宣传场，请新闻单位看片。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s3" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    对风传很久的新片，就会请行署大院里的分管领导
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s3" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    审片
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   ，有时领导会当场指示宣传、组织等部门联合下发通知，组织单位观看。偶尔因影片有
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s3" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    副作用
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   ，领导也会说
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s3" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    不要宣传
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s3" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    、
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s3" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    控制放映
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   ，这时公司经理的脸就成了
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s3" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    老阴天
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s3" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1979
   </span>
   年春节前，公司周六晚上组织职工家属看片专场，每人发了
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    8
