  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    六十一个叛徒集团案
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    早已尘埃落定，但一些历史的细节尚不清楚。我父亲王林在文革中有三份交代谈及此事，提供了此案最初的情况。三份交代分别写于
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1967
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年、
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1968
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年和
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1969
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，内容基本相同，为阅读方便，我选择
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1968
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年的为范本，所缺之处在注释里加以补充。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1968
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年的交代：
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    关于李楚离的情况
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    李楚离，原叫李通良，后来在狱中叫李即吾。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我认识他是一九三○年春，介绍人是我的今是中学的同学过家和（又名过一民，李大钊时代的共产党员），当时我只是个赤色群众。过家和给我介绍李楚离，是叫我听革命故事，提高政治觉悟。过家和说李参加过北伐战争和广州暴动。当时我和李的关系只是朋友关系。在他住的公寓里见过他几面之后，因为他搬了家没有告诉我新搬住处的地址，我也因为在这一年五月初，由李续纲（文化大革命前在北京市人委工作）介绍参加了共青团，工作忙了，就跟李楚离断绝了朋友间的来往。后来又听说李楚离被捕入狱了，怎么被捕的，在哪个监狱扣押着也不知道。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    一九三六年五六月间，叛徒李素若（抗日期间因托匪关系被我八路军处决）叛变自首，从北海西草岚子胡同伪
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    军人反省院
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    出来，见到过家和说：
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    李通良（李楚离原名）也在军人反省院里扣押着，他已经成神经病了，谁跟他说王明召开四中全会引起党内斗争、党内分裂的问题，他就说：
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ‘
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我们党不会分裂，你是造谣！你是叛徒！
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ’
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    疯狂地骂人。我看李通良完了，疯了，神经病了，不可救药了！
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ……”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    过家和把这件事告诉我，我和过家和都认为李楚离在狱中的那种表现，是一个党员因长期关在狱中，不了解外边的情况，而对党的起码的忠贞。所以很同情。于是过家和出钱，我跑腿，给李楚离往狱中送过一次或两次吃的东西。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    后来，过家和向我说：
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    北海西草岚子胡同军人反省院的狱长（具体名称忘了），还是我本家的一个叔祖父。
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    因此他倡议说：
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    咱们想法把李通良弄出来吗？
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我说：
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    那太好啦！你有人、有势，全看你啦！
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    他说：
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    好，我奔走试一试。
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    过家和是国民党老西北军冯玉祥的财政干将过之瀚的侄子（过之瀚的儿子过家芳也是我的今是中学的同学，李大钊时代的党员，一九四八年淮海战役时给国民党冯治安部当师长，在火线上起义，文化大革命前在安徽军区当副司令员），过家和本人也是西北军某干将的门婿，这时他在北平宋哲元二十九军当什么差事（详情不知），但是他这时还没有恢复党的关系，据说后来不久就恢复了党籍。（在淮海战役中，他所联系的过家芳部在火线上起义成功后，他被国民党刺杀而死。）
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    一九三六年夏，过家和利用他的社会关系为李楚离奔走一些日子以后，见到我说：
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    李通良出狱的事不好办，因为国民党有规定，刑期不满提前出狱，必须办理自首手续。
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    过家和觉得自己不能劝同志叛变自首，因此他就不再奔走此事了。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我这时与黄敬几乎天天见面，就把上述的情况随时告诉了黄敬（黄敬此时是中共北平市委书记）。起初他对此事没有表示过任何态度。后来大约在六月底或七月初，黄敬忽然很严肃地向我说道：
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    有件工作，需要你去做一做。你不是跟你的姓过的同学要救一个押在狱里的同志吗？
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我说：
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    是啊。
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    他说：
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    党中央很关心这些在狱里押着的老同志们，考虑到华北的局势，随时都可能有变化，有突然被日寇占领的可能，像沈阳
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ‘
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    九一八事变
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ’
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    一样，一晚上成了日本的。如果华北沦陷在日本手里了，这些同志们可就出不来啦。同时外边又非常缺少干部，急需这些老同志们出来工作。因此组织上决定叫他办手续赶快出来工作。
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我一听这件事非同小可，不免一惊愣。黄敬见我一惊愣，更加严肃地加重语气申明道：
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    这是组织上的决定，而不是他个人的动摇变节！你要利用你那姓过的同学的特殊关系，找个方便，把组织上这个决定告诉李楚离，叫他赶快办手续出来工作！
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我接受了这个任务以后，先找过家和（在他叔父家中）要他向他叔祖父要求在狱中规定的一般犯人家属探亲时间以外，允许我单独接见一次李楚离，过答应了。下一次我找到他时，他说：
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    说好了，你到那个军人反省院的收发室找某某（姓名忘了），一说你叫什么，他就知道你是来干什么的了。
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我记下那个姓名，后来就到伪军人反省院去了。到门口收发室一提找某某，正好就是他本人值班。他叫我到那个接见犯人的大铁栅栏前等候。不久，李楚离趟着铁镣出来了，后边还有个警察跟来监视。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
