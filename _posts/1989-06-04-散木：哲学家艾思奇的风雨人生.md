<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
<b>
<font size="5">
      哲学家艾思奇的风雨人生
     </font>
</b>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>

<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
<b style="">
<font size="4">
      －－作者：散木
     </font>
</b>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    大众热读《大众哲学》
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    艾思奇（
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1910
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    －
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1966
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ），原名李生萱，云南腾冲人。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    艾思奇的父亲李曰垓是一个激进民主主义思想的学者，还曾是蔡锷的一位幕僚，担任过云南省民政厅长等职；其兄长李生庄则是五四时期云南新文化运动的传播者之一，曾任《腾越日报》社长，创立有省立腾越简易师范学校，并著有《云南边区问题》等。在这种家庭的熏陶下，艾思奇很早就接触到新思想新思潮的影响，在云南省立第一中学读书时期参与了由共产党员李国柱秘密领导下的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    读书会
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    、
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    青年努力会
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的活动，并开办义务夜校。当时他还与云南人的音乐家聂耳结为友好。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1928
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，艾思奇留学日本，在福冈工业大学冶金系学习。当时正是中国革命处于低潮之际，大革命的失败使许多人热心于探讨马克思主义的理论与中国具体实际的问题，而日本更是马克思主义向中国传播的一个中转渠道。在日本有大量的马克思主义研究者和翻译过来的马克思主义著作，于是，艾思奇一边认真学习大学的功课，一边大量接触马克思、恩格斯以及西方众多哲学流派的理论著作。为了能直接阅读马克思、恩格斯的著作，他还努力学习日语、德语，最终掌握了这两种外语，为他后来深入研究马克思主义哲学打下了坚实的基础。难能可贵的是，当年艾思奇除了刻苦地学习马克思主义，研究理论，还特别注意将理论与实践结合，他和当时中共东京支部所联系的许多进步的留日同学，共同学习和探讨问题，并参与进行社会调查。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1931
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年九一八事变爆发后，全国抗日浪潮风起云涌，艾思奇和许多中国留学生遂毅然弃学回国。在上海，他开始从事哲学研究，而
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    艾思奇
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    这个名字就是他当年使用的一个笔名，意思是
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    爱好思考奇异的事物
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。不久，艾思奇参加了上海
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    反帝大同盟
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，随后又在杜国庠的介绍下加入了中共领导的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    社会科学家联盟
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    （简称
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    社联
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ）。从此，艾思奇正式走上了研究和宣传马克思主义哲学的人生道路。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    当时
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    社联
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    出版有一份综合性的月刊《正路》，艾思奇相继发表了一些哲学文章。由于文章写得相当深刻，便引起了广大读者的注意。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1934
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，艾思奇又加入《申报》
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    流通图书馆
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的读书指导部工作。当时这家图书馆努力与读者产生互动关系，艾思奇也将自己的许多文章发表在《申报》的《读书问答》专栏里。后来为了满足读者的要求，这个专栏从《申报》中分出，发展成为独立半月刊的《读书生活》杂志，由李公朴主编，艾思奇参与编辑，艾思奇的成名作《大众哲学》一书，就是先以《哲学讲话》为题相继发表在《读书生活》上的（
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1935
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年结集出版，初版书名为《哲学讲话》，至第四版时易名为《大众哲学》）。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    《大众哲学》给艾思奇带来的巨大的声誉，此书在
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1949
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年前已印行了
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    32
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    版，在宣传马克思主义的启蒙运动中起到了巨大的作用，不少青年读者在它的影响下相继走上了革命道路。当年延安的诗人贺敬之曾在一首诗中这样吟诵道：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    传递着，传递着，我们的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ‘
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    火炬
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ’
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    －－啊！我们的《新华日报》，我们的《大众哲学》，我们的《解放》周刊，我们的《活跃的肤施》。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”(
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    肤施即延安－－笔者注
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    )
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    可见当时这本哲学通俗著作是与《新华日报》、《解放》以及一篇著名的报告文学《活跃的肤施》并称，产生了共同的影响的。当然，对此艾思奇自己却十分谦虚，后来每当有人称赞这本书时，他总是说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    当时以※※※为首的党中央领导着工农红军取得了长征的胜利，正在进行着伟大的革命实践。广大革命群众向往光明，迫切追求真理，在这样的时代潮流下，这本小册子才能起一点作用。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    这也的确是《大众哲学》所以能够
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    走红
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的一个历史背景。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    延安的红色哲学家
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1935
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1945
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，艾思奇出席了中共七大，随即继杨松、陆定一之后任《解放日报》总编。七大确立了※※※思想是中共指导思想的地位，艾思奇也为此作出了贡献。他在延安整风运动中，曾针对当时许多人误解
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    实事求是
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，写了《不要误解
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    实事求是
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    》一文，其中写道：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我们可以从※※※同志那里学习到许多东西。我们只要举出他在
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1929
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年红军第四军第九次党代表会议所作的决定来看
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ……
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    就可以看出，他怎样善于在分析事实的时候，就同时考察出正确地对症下药的办法。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    七大之后，针对国民党蒋介石掀起的反共高潮，艾思奇撰写了《论〈中国之命运〉－－极端唯心论的愚民哲学》等文章，驳斥反共思潮是张扬
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    中国式的买办封建性的法西斯主义的政治学和反对科学唯物主义、提倡迷信盲从的法西斯主义的唯心论哲学
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    延安时期的艾思奇除具体负责延安新哲学会等活动外，还在边区文协工作，并曾担任中央研究院文化思想研究室主任、中央宣传部文化工作委员会秘书长、《解放日报》副总编辑和总编辑、青年艺术剧院理事、延安诗会理事、鲁迅研究会理事等。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    在延安，艾思奇除了研究马克思主义哲学之外，还学习了一些自然科学的基本知识，他还阅读了牛顿、达尔文、海克尔、赫胥黎、汤姆生、艾丁顿等西方科学家的著作，并热心提倡研究自然科学和推动自然科学的普及工作，为此他先后写了《怎样研究自然科学》以及许多
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    读者问答
   </span>

    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    、
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    名词浅释
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    、
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    科学小品
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    李崇基

<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    意为崇拜高尔基
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，与会的有章汉夫、于光远等
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    20
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    多人。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    在延安，艾思奇勤勉工作，他不以哲学家自居，从来不摆架子，待人热情、诚恳。至于他的讲课，更是从不以声取胜，而是侃侃而谈，旁征博引，因此他的讲课具有敏锐和深刻的内涵、严密的逻辑和深入浅出的表达方法，被人广为称颂，人们也习惯称他为
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    艾教员
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。于光远回忆说：当年艾思奇留下了许多
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    笑话
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，如
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    有一个晚上，艾思奇开完会从窑洞里出来，不知道窑前有人挖了一条沟，不小心跌了进去，但他并不忙着站起来，而是躺在沟里想，
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    这次我跌进去究竟是必然性的呢，还是偶然性的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ……’
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    大家笑，他不笑，也不生气。艾思奇就是这样随和，没有一点儿官架子的人。同事们都很喜欢他。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    据说※※※也曾说过这样一句话：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    老艾同志不是天下第一好人，也是第二好人。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    这是对他极好的一个评价。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    风云变幻中的哲学家
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1949
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年新中国成立后，艾思奇历任中共中央党校哲学教研室主任及副校长、中国哲学学会副会长、中国科学院哲学社会科学学部委员等，他还是中国共产党第七、八次全国代表大会代表，中华人民共和国第一、二、三届全国人民代表大会代表。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    在新中国的哲学界，艾思奇以其声望和影响，几乎成为能够坐头把交椅的领军人物，而在新中国大规模的经济建设开始后，艾思奇也十分重视哲学和自然科学的结合，他认为哲学的产生和发展要依赖自然科学，反之，自然科学的发展也要受哲学的影响，正确的哲学能推动科学的前进。但是在具体的实践过程中，伴随着新中国建设的风风雨雨，艾思奇的经历可谓跌宕起伏。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    这一时期，艾思奇既反对忽视马克思主义哲学的倾向，又反对无限夸大哲学的作用，反对把哲学庸俗化和简单化处理，反对以哲学代替自然科学的做法。在他的主持下，中央党校开设了自然辩证法班、逻辑班等，并在他的指导下编写了比较系统的自然辩证法等教材。为了贯彻理论联系实际的方法论原则，艾思奇还多次深入农村，参加实际的工作，在实际工作中发现问题，并将之提高到辩证唯物论的理论高度加以分析。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1958
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    9
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    月到
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1959
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    7
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    月，艾思奇在河南调研了
   </span>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    大跃进

<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    中的

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>

<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
