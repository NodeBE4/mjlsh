   01
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   一个秋日，我来到井冈山下的茶陵县。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   在茶陵县城的东南郊，在蜿蜒曲折的洣江河畔，有一个洣江茶场。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<img alt="23.jpg" border="0" height="448" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5755/23.jpg" width="350"/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   走过这个牌楼，就进了洣江茶场
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   这个茶场，曾经另有一个名称：湖南省第三劳改管教队。至今，茶陵监狱仍设于此。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   在监狱围墙的西侧，有一个平缓的山坡，坡上建有很多红砖平房，至今仍是职工宿舍区。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   在这个山坡的最下方，有一栋小小的红砖平房。平房坐北朝南，墙上挂了一块木牌：
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   潘汉年旧居
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<img alt="24.jpg" border="0" height="375" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5755/24.jpg" width="500"/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   潘汉年旧居外景
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   这里曾是职工澡堂，室内约有
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    20
   </span>
   余平米。被改造为潘汉年住房后，隔成两间，其中一间为灶屋。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   如今门窗紧锁，室内空空。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   在这栋平房的左前方，有一间小房，挂牌为
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   四方水塔
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<img alt="25.jpg" border="0" height="466" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5755/25.jpg" width="350"/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   四方水塔
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   在这栋平房的右前方，与
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   四方水塔
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   相对的小房子，也挂了
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   职工澡堂
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   的牌子。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<img alt="26.jpg" border="0" height="375" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5755/26.jpg" width="500"/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   职工澡堂
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   在这片建筑的前面，是一片普普通通的菜地和田野。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   潘汉年就在这里度过了人生的最后两年。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<span class="Apple-converted-space">
</span>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
   02
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   在中共党史上，潘汉年是一个公认的传奇。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   他是江苏宜兴的望族子弟，本是妥妥的一枚文青，却在
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    19
   </span>
   岁那年（
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1925
   </span>
   年）加入中共，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    20
   </span>
   岁担任了国民革命军总政治部《革命军日报》的总编辑；
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    24
   </span>
   岁时，他就是
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   左联
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   创始人、领导人之一；
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    26
   </span>
   岁时，他成为神秘的中共
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   特科
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   下辖的情报科科长，参与处理了顾顺章、向忠发叛变事件；
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    29
   </span>
   岁那年，他作为中共代表，与广东军阀陈济棠谈判，为中央红军顺利通过粤赣、湘粤边界创造了条件；遵义会议后，他又和陈云奉命离开长征队伍，赴苏联向共产国际汇报请示工作；
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    30
   </span>
   岁那年，他从莫斯科归来，代表中共与南京政府进行多次谈判，对促成国共第二次合作发挥了重要作用；
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    他常年西装革履，说话温文尔雅，是上海滩上风度翩翩的
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    小开
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   ，却长期担任中共在上海、香港地下组织的负责人，与蒋、汪、日、英、美、苏等多种势力周旋，多次传送出重要情报；
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   上海解放后，他是上海市常务副市长、市委副书记，却于
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1955
   </span>
   年
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    4
   </span>
   月
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    3
   </span>
   日在北京被捕，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    21
   </span>
   年后病逝于羁押期间。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   潘汉年的功绩无需赘述。吸引我目光的，是他身后的一位女性。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
   03
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1938
   </span>
   年
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    9
   </span>
   月，潘汉年在延安向组织提出：希望配备一位在香港、上海等地具有广泛社会关系的助手。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   不久，组织就从延安马列学院物色到了一位
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    20
   </span>
   岁的女学员，她就是董慧。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   董慧，原名董奉然，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1918
   </span>
   年出生，祖籍广东，父亲董仲维是香港道亨银行董事长，在香港商界威信很高，担任过香港总商会会长。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   董慧原本在北京上大学，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1935
   </span>
   年
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    12
   </span>
   月参加了
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   一二九运动
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   。七七事变后，董慧和许多热血青年一样奔向延安，进入抗日军政大学学习，第二年
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1
   </span>
   月就入了党，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    7
   </span>
   月进入延安马列学院学习。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1939
   </span>
   年
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    4
   </span>
   月，董慧随潘汉年回到香港，在父亲的道亨银行做一名普通职员，实际是在潘汉年领导下从事情报工作，负责保管党的情报工作经费，同时负责香港与上海之间的情报传递。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1940
   </span>
   年，他们结为伴侣。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<img alt="27.jpg" border="0" height="276" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5755/27.jpg" width="450"/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   潘汉年和董慧（网络图片）
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   此前，潘汉年有过一次由母亲包办的婚姻，前妻名叫许玉文，文化不高，两人感情不和。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   此后，潘汉年和董慧辗转香港、上海等地，在隐蔽战线上出生入死，甘苦与共。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   上海解放后，董慧也从香港到上海，担任市委统战部秘书科科长，后来提任为工商处副处长。他们的家，是位于武康路上的一座花园式小洋房。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   据老战友刘人寿回忆：
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   她对一般同志也十分热情、诚恳。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s3" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
    （他们的家里）
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   经常高朋满座，好友如云；平时总是客人不断，到了节假日时，更是热闹得很，欢声笑语常常不绝于耳。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   但厄运说来就来。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1955
   </span>
   年
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    4
   </span>
   月，潘汉年被捕的消息传到上海，董慧坚信丈夫无罪。为了促成问题解决，她主动来到北京，找战友、领导反映情况。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    5
   </span>
   月
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    19
   </span>
   日，董慧也在北京被捕，能够成立的理由只有一条：她是潘汉年的战友和妻子。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
   04
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   入狱之后，文人出身的潘汉年写过多首诗歌思念董慧。这里试选两首：
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   怀妻
  </p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   不知生死第五年，纵活人间亦可怜；
  </p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   莫谓夫妻不到老，三生石上订良缘。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<span class="Apple-converted-space">
</span>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   七律
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
</span>
   给董慧
  </p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   千里驰书一片心，巫山隔绝白云深。
  </p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   朝思暮念夜成梦，月暗花愁空断魂。
  </p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   纵死不辞称所爱，此生何时复相亲？
  </p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   天摇地动倒流水，但愿冬寒化早春。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   字里行间，都是思念。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   潘汉年没有想到：妻子紧随自己也进了班房，而且关在同一个地方，先是北京功德林监狱，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1960
   </span>
   年又第一批入住秦城监狱。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   在关押审查期间，董慧依然正直、朴实、善解人意，审讯者也认为她态度很好。王朝柱在《功臣的悲剧》一文中写道：
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   她受审查期间的态度真是好极了！事后，没有一个奉命办案的执法者不交口称赞。而在这种交口称赞的背后，董慧那纯朴的心灵又遭受到了何等大的摧残啊！她心甘情愿地接受这种非人的心灵摧残，除去那个特定的年代留给她的烙印外，她更是为了思念和钟爱中的潘汉年！
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">

    ”
   </span>
   文革被判刑十年，也关在洣江茶场。后来，他回忆第一次见到潘汉年：
  </p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我一眼望去，是一个身材矮小、面容清癯、头发白多于青而且非常稀朗、穿着一件旧灰色派力司干部服的老头，手里提着一只小竹篮子。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   在洣江茶场，他们有特殊的待遇：带了不少书，有钱买鱼和蛋吃，可以抽
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   牡丹牌
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   一级的好烟，可以订购公开报刊，享受公费医疗。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<img alt="29.jpg" border="0" height="375" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5755/29.jpg" width="500"/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   潘汉年旧居外景
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   董慧被明确是人民内部矛盾，还有通信的自由。当然，她给香港的亲人写信，必须经过茶场领导检查。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   他们夫妇俩同住在由浴室改成的小平房里，被允许在茶场范围内
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   自由活动
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   。在初来的头一年里，潘汉年同志总是每天五点多钟起床打太极拳，接着就打扫屋子周围的卫生。到六队买柴火，到邮电所取报纸，都是这一年里的事情。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   钟叔河反复提到，那时候潘汉年神情安祥、恬静。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   钟叔河主动与之搭话，悄悄地说了不少，潘汉年只轻轻回过两句，一句是
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   相信人民
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s3" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
    ，一句是
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   你还年轻
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   潘汉年的肝功能本来就有毛病，虽然已被排除癌症，但由于得不到很好的治疗，身体一直不太好。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   与潘汉年相比，董慧的身体更差：初到茶场时，她的身体虚弱，说话无力，精神也不太正常；加上腿有残疾，行走不便，高血压也越来越重。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   经过一段时间的休养、治疗，董慧的身体状况有所好转。后来，她的弟弟从香港给她送来了一把轮椅，她经常由潘汉年推着，在场部范围内转转。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   茶场领导为了照顾他们，特地派了一个名叫王芝兰的职工家属，每天照料董慧的日常生活。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   但是，湘东山区气候潮湿，夏暑逼人，冬寒刺骨，毕竟不适宜老人居住。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<img alt="30.jpg" border="0" height="375" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5755/30.jpg" width="500"/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   潘汉年旧居后侧的职工宿舍
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
   07
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1976
   </span>
   年
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    10
   </span>
   月，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   四人帮
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   垮台。消息传来，潘汉年欣喜万分。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    12
   </span>
   月
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    26
   </span>
   日，为了纪念毛泽东诞辰
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    83
   </span>
   周年，《人民日报》、《湖南日报》等报纸在头版头条刊登了毛泽东长篇文章《论十大关系》，其中有这样一句：
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    胡风、潘汉年、饶漱石这样的人不
   </span>
   杀。不杀他们不是没有可杀之罪，而是杀了不利。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   这篇文章彻底击垮了潘汉年，他自感沉冤难雪，旋即病倒。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   到了
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1977
   </span>
   年
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    2
   </span>
   月，潘汉年病情严重，而茶场医院的医疗条件实在太差。经茶场请示省公安厅，省公安厅先后请示公安部、省委，终于批准送他去长沙治病。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1977
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    年
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    3
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    月
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    24
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    日，潘汉年化名
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    萧叔安
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   ，住进了湖南省医学院附属第二医院（今中南大学湘雅附二医院）
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    14
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    病室，这是一个干部病室。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   经过检查，才发现潘汉年不仅早已患了慢性支气管炎、类风湿性关节炎、贫血，还患有多囊肝合并消化道出血、肺部感染等病症。虽然采取了一些治疗措施，但为时已晚。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    4
   </span>
   月
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    6
   </span>
   日，潘汉年病情恶化，神志不清，全身浮肿，大小便失禁，呕吐。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   董慧没有陪同和看望的权利，只能在茶陵苦盼消息，度日如年。直至潘汉年病危，有关方面才通知董慧去长沙探望。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    4
   </span>
   月
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    14
   </span>
   日傍晚，董慧终于来到病室。潘汉年睁大眼睛看着妻子，却一句话也说不出来（一说是抱头痛哭）。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">

</p>

<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">

<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>

<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">

<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">

<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">

<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">

</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>

   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">

<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">

<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">

<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">

<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">

   时过境迁。此时复查，已能看到许多档案，包括
