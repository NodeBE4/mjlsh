</b>
</p>
<p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<b>
    －－吴小伟讲述
    <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
</span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
     团团撰稿
    </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
</span>
</b>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">

<img alt="34.jpg" border="0" height="455" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5758/34.jpg" width="350"/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   吴小伟，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1947
   </span>
   年生于徐州，浙江东阳人。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    2
   </span>
   岁时，爸妈去台湾，他被留在老家，由姑妈抚养长大。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    01
   </span>
   与妈妈分别
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    39
   </span>
   年，她还认得我吗？
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1988
   </span>
   年
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    8
   </span>
   月
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    6
   </span>
   日，酷热。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我和太太、儿子、女儿，四人一起在笕桥机场候机厅等待。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   就要见到妈妈了，每一日，我都想着团聚的这一刻。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我两岁时，爸爸妈妈去了台湾，他们说很快回来接我。没想到，这一别，就是三十九年。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<img alt="35.jpg" border="0" height="502" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5758/35.jpg" width="320"/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   爸妈离开时，我（左）刚
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    2
   </span>
   岁
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   爸爸很早去世了，我连最后一面也没见到。妈妈一个人经历了很多，最苦最难的时候，我却不在她身边。弟弟和两个妹妹，已成家生子，他们人生中最重要的时刻，我都错过了。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   这些年，我只在信中看见过妈妈的样子，熟悉，又陌生。两岁后，我没再感受过妈妈的拥抱，没有牵过她的手，甚至连当面喊她一声
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   妈妈
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   的机会也没有。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    42
   </span>
   岁了，不再是那个哭喊着要妈妈的孩子。我很少流泪，想念都埋在心底。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   妈妈在信中说，她日日夜夜都想着我这个儿子。这一生中最大的事，就是回大陆和儿子团聚。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我从没怨过父母，让我们一家人分离的，是时代，是战争。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我告诉自己：总有一天，爸妈会回来的，在那之前，我要好好活着。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   为了我，妈妈提前办完退休手续，定下
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1988
   </span>
   年夏天带小妹一起回来。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   得知这个消息，我太高兴了，高兴得有些不知所措。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我不再是那个东阳乡下的穷小子了，我有幸福的家庭，在杭州有自己的事业。我想让妈妈看到我的成长，她的儿子在大陆生活得很好，有出息了。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   为了迎接妈妈的到来，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1987
   </span>
   年初，我在老家盖了四层小楼，这是东阳第一栋公寓式楼房。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我的车坏了，我特意找同事借了一辆小轿车去机场接妈妈。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我想让妈妈坐我开的车，载着她看杭州的美丽风景。这是我生活打拼的地方。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    8
   </span>
   月
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    6
   </span>
   日早上，我穿着纯白色的短袖衬衫，前后都熨烫得没有一道褶子，搭配浅灰色长裤，棕色皮凉鞋，腰间系着黑色皮带，左手带着上海牌手表，短发梳得一丝不苟。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我想把自己最精神的样子，给妈妈看。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我开车载着太太、儿子女儿一起去笕桥机场。这里的每条路，每个大厅我都太熟悉了。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1971
   </span>
   年为迎接尼克松访华，周总理于
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    11
   </span>
   月
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    18
   </span>
   日亲自签署命令，在笕桥机场新建设一个候机楼，项目名为
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “1118
   </span>
   工程
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<img alt="36.jpg" border="0" height="189" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5758/36.jpg" width="550"/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我曾参与建造的笕桥机场候机楼
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   政府部门调动了整个浙江省建筑业的优秀人才，我也在其中。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    24
   </span>
   小时不停作业，换人不换机。我们
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    12
   </span>
   个人一个班，同住一个帐篷，每天加班到凌晨。候机楼如期完成。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    等待了两个多小时，飞机
   </span>
   迟迟未来。我不时看看手表上的指针，频频抬头向出口张望。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   喜悦、兴奋、期盼、焦急，各种情绪混作一团。心跳很快，掌心都是汗，我搓了搓手，来回走着，一刻也坐不下来。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   等会见到妈妈，我应该做什么呢？我该拥抱她，还是握握她的手呢？
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   脑子里的念头一个个蹦出来，我盯着候机厅的天花板，想让自己平静下来。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   突然，出口处的门打开了，我伸长了脖子朝里面探望。一个红衣服的年轻女孩儿挽着一位老人，徐徐走来，我一眼就认出来，这是妈妈和小妹。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<img alt="37.jpg" border="0" height="502" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5758/37.jpg" width="350"/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   妈妈和小妹在杭州
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   妈妈留着一头乌黑的中长卷发，带着黑框眼镜，身型偏瘦，但步子很
   <span class="s3" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
    稳，眼神坚定。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我迎上前去，妈妈和小妹也朝着我快步走来。妈妈张开了双臂，将我紧紧抱在怀里。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   这个拥抱，我盼了三十九年。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我们都没有哭，此刻的妈妈，眼里满是笑意。思念、痛苦、期盼，这一刻的喜悦与幸福，全都融化在了这个温暖的拥抱里。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   妈妈，你知道吗，这个候机楼是我参与建设的。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   妈妈看着我，露出惊讶的表情。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我带着妈妈和小妹入住望湖宾馆，又陪她们逛西湖，去灵隐寺拜佛。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我搂着妈妈的肩膀，拍下三十九年来的第一张合照。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">

<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    39
   </span>
   年后母子才相聚
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   妈妈转过头对我说：
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   你那么小，把你一个人留在东阳，是我最内疚的事。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">

   </span>
   妈妈，这是历史造成的，不是我们自愿的，是无可奈何。你看我现在不是好好的。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">

   </span>
   我还有一个心愿，这也是你爸爸的遗愿。去东阳老家看看你的姑妈，我要当面感谢她。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   驱车五个多小时，从杭州回到东阳老家。看见老家新建的房子，妈妈对我说：
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">

   </span>
   这儿比我在台湾的别墅更大，更漂亮。你过得好，我也放心了。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">

  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">

</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   姑妈年纪大了，得了癌症，躺在床上不能起身。妈妈在病床前握着姑妈的手，在她耳边说了很多话。我坐在床边陪着两位老人。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">

</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   妈妈是生我的母亲，姑妈是将我养大成人的母亲，她们都是我最爱的人。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">

</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   两个月后，姑妈去世。我捧着姑妈的遗像走在出殡队伍的最前面。我人生中最遗憾的事，就是没能多为姑妈尽孝，她
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">

   </span>

  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   姑妈是个很善良的人，她经常关心帮助贫困的邻居，家里有好吃的都要送给别人。她对长辈很孝顺，言传不如身教，她的为人处事，我都看在眼里，记在心里。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   姑妈将我养大成人，将她的爱毫无保留的给了我，这个恩情，我一辈子也还不完。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">

   是姑妈将我养大成人
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">

  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">

<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>

<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>

   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我问姑妈，爸爸妈妈呢？她总说，他们很快就回来接你了，你要听话。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   后来妈妈告诉我，在她与爸爸结婚前，有个算命的追着爸爸要给他看相。他说爸爸未来会娶到一个很贤惠的太太，但头胎不能生男孩，而且孩子不能跟在身边，否则养不大。如果在别处长大，将来会有很出息。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<img alt="41.jpg" border="0" height="387" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5758/41.jpg" width="550"/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   爸爸和妈妈在台湾
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   没想到，这些话，竟真的应验了我的一生。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我从小体弱，瘦小，肺炎留下的病根一直没好。崔先生是姑妈的租客，他在印刷厂当技术员，还懂一些中医。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   崔先生为我把脉，开了七贴方子，让姑妈为我抓药。我喝了以后，竟再没生过病。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   七岁时，我还没读小学。姑妈姑父刚出门，去田里劳作，我和小姐姐在家里玩耍，床前面是一张方桌。我们从床跳到桌子上，再从桌子跳回床上，来来回回。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我一脚踩空，后脑勺重重磕在桌子的棱角上。血瞬间喷涌而出，姐姐吓得大哭，边跑边喊，将出门的姑妈姑父叫了回来。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   姑妈一看，我的脑壳裂开了一条缝，便用一把面粉糊在后脑勺上，再用纱布缠上。她用菜油煎了两个鸡蛋，跟我说，吃下去就好了。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   没过多久，裂开的脑壳真的自己长好了，一点儿事没有。现在我的后脑勺还有道凹凸不平的疤。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   姑父姑妈待我就像亲儿子一般，姐姐们也很疼我。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   最小的姐姐比我大两岁，小时候穿的布鞋，用桐油刷过。每逢雨天，小姐姐都会背着我去上学。下课后，等在我教室门口，我们一起回家。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   每天放学后，我背个草篮子去地里拔草，喂兔子。去汽车修理厂捡垃圾，从一堆废弃物里捡些铜丝、铅丝、螺丝帽，拿到供销社去卖，能赚几毛钱。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我从小就很懂事，从不跟人争辩，不计较。我是家里唯一的儿子，总想为家里多分担一点事情。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我爱读书，成绩一直名列前茅。在同龄人中我个子最小，坐在教室第一排。上政治课，老师喊了十几个同学背提纲，没有一个人背得出，都被罚站。老师火了，说从最前排的人开始背，一个个往下轮，背出来才能坐下。我记性很好，长长的提纲，背得一字不差。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   三年自然灾害开始了，生活条件越来越艰苦。玉米糊、番薯成了主要口粮，我还上山挖一些葛根、金刚刺充饥。缺乏油水，几天无法排便，有时不得不用铁丝勾。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   姑父很孝顺，用粮票买的米，都留给奶奶吃，自己吃豆腐渣。肚子越胀越大，没多久，姑父去世了。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   姐姐们去外地工作读书，三姐姐从卫校毕业，分配到杭州当护士，每个月会寄二十块钱回来，这是我和姑妈的全部生活费。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   每月一到日子，邮差就在门口喊姑妈：
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   卢秀翠，快拿印章来
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s3" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
    ，我拿着印章高兴地飞跑出去。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   姑父走了，家里没有男人，只有我一个小孩，还是异姓的，许多人欺负、嘲笑我。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   很想念爸爸妈妈。五十年代，他们曾信回来，但姑父不敢回信。初中后，我懂事了，自己给爸妈写信，在信里写了很多思念的话，但寄出去前，每一个字都要被检查。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<img alt="42.jpg" border="0" height="404" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5758/42.jpg" width="550"/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   没有我的全家福
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   爸妈也给我寄照片，他们，还有我没有见过面的弟弟妹妹们。弟弟妹妹穿得整整齐齐，很洋气，屋前停着一辆摩托车。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我连自行车都没有，好羡慕他们啊
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ……
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    03
   </span>
   爸爸娶了一位大家闺秀
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   妈妈叫闵光慧，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1927
   </span>
   年出生在武昌，是独生女，也是家族中的大小姐。她有一个美丽的小名：芙蓉。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   外公名前栋，经营一个钱庄，兼在外地买货回武昌做生意，家境小康。闵家在武昌是出名的旺族，族内四十余人，二嫂当家，如王熙凤般精明能干。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   妈妈从小娇生惯养，六岁入私塾，上课书包要丫鬟背着。书背不熟被先生打手心，她哭闹不肯上学，非要外婆将束脩要回，此后便改上公立小学。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   有次生眼疾，郎中嘱咐需日日以盐水淸洗，外公便亲自照顾，直到妈妈痊愈。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   十岁时，外公去世了，妈妈快乐的童年从此结束。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   不久后，妈妈的姑丈强占了外公的钱庄，吸鸭片的三叔败尽家产，外婆为维持家计发愁，妈妈都看在眼里。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1938
   </span>
   年，日军占领武汉，对整个武汉推行奴化教育。妈妈跟着沦陷区一百多名孩子一起去了香港。外婆收拾了妈妈的衣物，舍不得独生女离家，却终究奈何不了，只能让妈妈独自上路。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<img alt="43.jpg" border="0" height="315" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5758/43.jpg" width="550"/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1938
   </span>
   年，武汉沦陷
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   在香港读书三年，妈妈的英文和广东话都是在这期间学会的。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   战事吃紧后，妈妈与同学们也去过湖南、四川、贵州等地念中学。虽然长期物资缺乏，但妈妈依然坚持读书学习。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   妈妈的五叔曾寄过一本《范氏大代数》给她，老师拿去刻钢板给全体同学练习，妈妈的堂兄闵汉英也常寄钱接济。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1945
   </span>
   年抗战胜利，妈妈在离家八年后回到老家武昌，堂兄闵汉英介绍妈妈与爸爸相识了。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   爸爸名叫卢海澄，又名卢松竹，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1917
   </span>
   年出生在东阳卢宅。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   爸爸从小聪明好学，努力上进，但家里穷，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    16
   </span>
   岁便和亲戚一同离家从军。后来他经过自己努力考上中央军校（即黄埔军校），学习无线电专业。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   毕业后，爸爸负责美国援华无线电器材的押运，从缅甸仰光到云南昆明到的空中运输。蒋介石只有两个团是负责无线电通讯，这是一个特殊兵种，爸爸年纪轻轻就成为了中校。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1946
   </span>
   年，抗战胜利，国共和谈。爸爸刚
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    29
   </span>
   岁，正是风华正茂的年纪。他和一帮同学在公园玩耍，黄埔军校教官闵汉英向爸爸介绍了一位姑娘，他的堂妹，闵光慧。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<img alt="44.jpg" border="0" height="494" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5758/44.jpg" width="350"/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   妈妈是个大家闺秀
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   两人第一次见面，爸爸想在妈妈面前想卖弄一下自己的英文，便问妈妈有没有读过《新世界》，哪知妈妈随即便背了一段给爸爸听，自此，爸爸就败倒在妈妈的石榴裙下。很快坠入爱河，第二年便成婚了。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1947
   </span>
   年，爸爸带着妈妈第一次回东阳老家省亲，很风光。妈妈穿着旗袍，踩着高跟鞋，十分时髦。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   乡亲们得知爸爸娶了一位大城市来的大家闺秀做太太，都出来看热闹。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    04
   </span>
   妈妈花两百个银元买了青霉素
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   农历十月，淮海战役前夕，我在徐州基督教会医院出生了，是美国传教士医生接生的。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1948
   </span>
   年年初，爸爸接到调令，到南京任职。他开着吉普车，载着我和妈妈，从徐州来到南京，花了
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    20
   </span>
   个金条，在南京中央路大街
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    333
   </span>
   号建了一幢三开间的二层小楼。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   爸爸爱打麻将，我很不喜欢他总是出门打麻将。为了表示生气，我就躲起来，不让爸妈找到。有一回，妈妈找了半天，才在沙发背后找到我。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   生下来就体弱多病，我得百日咳时，咳嗽咳得嘴里吐出了蛔虫。我拿着咳出的小白条说：
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   妈妈，妈妈，小豆芽，小豆芽。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   百日咳引起肺炎，我高烧不退。妈妈急得去找西医买青霉素。这种药刚上市不久，很稀有，两百个银元才买回几支。我退烧了，但身子仍旧病弱。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1949
   </span>
   年初，南京解放前，爸爸接到通知立即撤防去广州。妈妈把家门一锁，只拎了一只皮箱就走了。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<img alt="45.jpg" border="0" height="448" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5758/45.jpg" width="350"/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   国民党撤离南京
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   没多久，广州也解放了，只好撤到重庆，最后实在没地方去了，决定一起回东阳老家。此时，弟弟已经出生，还不到一周岁。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   三月，爸爸带妈妈和我回东阳，这次是落难回去的，往日的风光不再。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   妈妈不会说东阳话，语言不通，和当地人说不上几句话。她生长在都市，五谷不分，在卢宅居住时想去后园割韭菜炒鸡蛋，结果割了一把长相差不多的麦子回来。邻居发现后，把这事当笑话传开了。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<img alt="46.jpg" border="0" height="350" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5758/46.jpg" width="550"/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我的老家东阳卢宅
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我们一家生活在乡下卢宅，姑妈家在县城开店，店里黄酒、年糕，物资充裕。隔三差五，妈妈就带着我到姑妈家打打牙祭。姑父很喜欢我，抱着我到处玩。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   姑妈有四个女儿，没有儿子。姑妈和姑父对我非常疼爱。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   半年不到，妈妈受不了这里的生活，喊着要回武汉。爸爸和姑妈说，他们先回武汉，安顿好之后就来接我。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   姑妈抱着我，站在门口。看着爸妈离去的背影，我大哭不止。姑妈说：
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   听话，你爸妈很快就回来接你了。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   这一场看似普通的离别，让我和家人至此分隔两地，一别三十九年。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   妈妈离开东阳老家后，回了武汉，但那儿也局势紧张。爸爸是黄埔军校
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    14
   </span>
   期的学生，托关系随战友一起去了香港。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   到香港后，爸爸和战友们合计着怎么生活，找不到工作，他们只能去工地上背黄沙水泥，当小工。终于安顿下来，爸爸写信让妈妈带着弟弟过来，但形势更加严峻了，没有介绍信和印章，无法去香港。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   妈妈找人写了一封假的介绍信，她的小叔子用肥皂刻了块假印章，印在信上。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   妈妈带着弟弟赶到广州，住进一家小饭店里，老板娘看妈妈抱着孩子，很同情她，便问她去哪里，妈妈说去香港。老板娘说，这里每天晚上要查夜的，如果被查到，你们就走不了了，她安排妈妈和弟弟住到自己的房间里。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   第二天一早，妈妈带着弟弟乘上广州到罗湖的火车，顺利过关进入香港。这也是罗湖口岸最后一趟末班车。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<img alt="47.jpg" border="0" height="366" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5758/47.jpg" width="550"/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   横架深圳河两岸的罗湖桥
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   爸爸在工厂做工，收入低微，妈妈到香港后，刚巧一个小学校长要找会广东话、国语，又会注音符号的老师，妈妈毛遂自荐拿到教职。这年，妈妈
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    22
   </span>
   岁。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   好景不常，先是工厂裁员，接着妈妈因怀孕，学校不再续聘，生活陷入困境。爸爸去修过马路，妈妈替工人洗过衣服，也推着车卖过面条。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   在香港熬了三年，经由爸爸昔日长官做保，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1953
   </span>
   年爸妈去了台湾。爸爸降级为少校入军职，妈妈自修教育概论，教材教法，以第三名的成绩考进空军子弟小学当教师。此后两人双薪，生活不再拮据。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   两个妹妹陆续在眷村出生，大妹生于
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1953
   </span>
   年，小妹
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1957
   </span>
   年
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<img alt="48.jpg" border="0" height="369" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5758/48.jpg" width="500"/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   两个妹妹都在眷村出生
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    05
   </span>
   我自己做主，改
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   卢
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   姓
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   吴
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1960
   </span>
   年，我
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    14
   </span>
   岁，在东阳二中念初中。我是班里读书最好的，年年是三好学生，每门功课都是优秀。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    17
   </span>
   岁初中毕业，我因父母在台湾，无法继续念书。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   家里隔三差五有人上门来查，爸爸的证件和照片原本藏在小姑妈家，他们害怕，把所有东西一股脑儿扔进了池塘。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1963
   </span>
   年，三个姐姐陆续嫁人，四姐姐高中毕业，升不了大学，只得回家
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   修地球
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   没有书读了，我白天在生产队干农活，晚上躺在床上暗暗流泪。难道我这一生就只能当农民吗？
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   干农活太苦太累了，我暗自立下志向，绝不能当一辈子农民，一定要走出东阳这个小地方。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   经朋友介绍，我花了
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    15
   </span>
   块钱跟一个上海师傅学裁缝。家里有台上海牌缝纫机，是用爸爸寄回来的华侨票买的。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   半个多月，所有服装设计我都学会了，开始帮亲戚朋友做衣服。裁缝是吃百家饭的，得挨家挨户上门给人量身做衣服，我不喜欢。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1967
   </span>
   年，我从市场买了木料回到东阳。姑妈家的老房子五十多年了，一直没有铺楼板。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我问邻居木匠师傅借来工具，在他的指导下，我边学边做，居然铺得一点不差。从此我有了学木匠的想法。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   刚学半年，一天，生产队长问我：
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   你会不会造房子？我们需要造三间牛棚。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   我想都没想就答：
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   会啊。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我从来没造过房子，凭着自己在初中学习的平面几何知识画建筑图纸。三间牛棚很快造完，扎实
   <span class="s3" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
    稳固。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<img alt="49.jpg" border="0" height="375" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5758/49.jpg" width="500"/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   牛棚的结构我现在还能画出
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   不久后，工程队承接了南乡铜矿铺木楼板的活，我比做了十多年的老师傅们铺得还快。后来在杭州被评为四级技工。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   也许命运早就注定了我要学一门和建筑相关的手艺。当初人人都觉得做家具的才叫
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   木工
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s3" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
    ，但在我眼里，做建筑也需要木工，建筑做好了，是能走遍四海的。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1970
   </span>
   年，我
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    23
   </span>
   岁，经人介绍，认识了现在的太太。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<img alt="50.jpg" border="0" height="522" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5758/50.jpg" width="350"/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   我和太太
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1973
   </span>
   年结婚
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    然而我姑父家姓
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    吴
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   ，我姓
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    卢
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   ，这样谈婚论嫁，名不正言不顺。于是我自己做主，将
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    卢
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    姓改为
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    吴
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   ，叫
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    吴小伟
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   ，正式向大家宣告，我是过继到姑妈家的儿子。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1973
   </span>
   年爸爸去世后，妈妈通过香港寄信回来。我回信告诉妈妈，我未经父母同意，就改姓吴了。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   妈妈回信说：
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   儿子你做得很对，你了却了你爸爸未了的心愿。你爸爸临终前，一直说起，要把你正式过继给姑妈家。他们把你养大成人，很不容易，你要好好孝顺姑妈。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   吴小伟
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   这个名字，跟随我至今。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    06
   </span>
   一生只做一件事
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1970
   </span>
   年我跟着一批老乡来到杭州，应该算最早的农民工了。一干就是五十年，我亲眼见证了杭州的发展，自己半辈子的汗水都在这儿了。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   在浙建二处（现省建工总公司的前身）干了六年，我学会了很多建筑技术。不怕吃苦，不怕吃亏，任何事我都抢着去做，老工人们很愿意和我搭活。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   生产队长照顾我们，介绍大家去工地附近做
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   过煤工
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   赚零花钱。两百斤一筐的媒，我们搬起倒出过称，一天过上百次，能拿三块工钱。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   四姐姐比我晚一年结婚，姐夫入赘。我
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1973
   </span>
   年结婚，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1976
   </span>
   年分家。姑妈家的产业，我让姐姐先挑。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   大年初一，我自己亲自督建新房，初三破土，正月十一上梁，正月十五打灶头。半个月时间，工程如期完成，我将老屋拆迁后新建了四间房，正月十六，就回杭州上班了。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   在杭州搞工程，我是两个工地的承包负责人。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1977
   </span>
   年的一天，我骑着借来的新自行车从工厂回家，路过杭州缝纫机厂，一辆大卡车开过来。我靠边躲在工厂大门后，卡车撞到了大门，两吨重的铁门直直倒下来。我拖着自行车拼命往后撤，还是被铁门砸到了脚背，顿时血流如注。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   缝纫机厂的工人把我送到文二路的新华医院，脚部缝了
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    36
   </span>
   针，第一个大拇指粉碎性骨折，打上石膏，我住了六个月的院。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   太太和三岁的儿子在东阳老家，她想来看我，我写信回去，说自己没事，很快就好了，有同事在医院照顾我。其实我心里挺后怕的，差一点，就没命了。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<img alt="51.jpg" border="0" height="375" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5758/51.jpg" width="500"/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    2017
   </span>
   年，四兄妹聚会
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   还有一次，在生产队干活，一不下心划破了脚踝，缝了三针。拆线后，我就回去干活了，没想到伤口又崩开，往外滋滋冒血，我也没管它。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   坐
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    8
   </span>
   路公交车时，我头刚上车司机就关了门，头被重重一撞，眉骨裂开了，又缝三针。同事们都笑我，说你脚还没好，头又伤了，真是运气背。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1981
   </span>
   年，公司派我去富阳施工队负责施工管理，工资加奖金每月
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    212
   </span>
   元，是全公司工资最高。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1984
   </span>
   年公司领导班子组阁，我被任命为公司副总兼杭州办事处主任，负责杭州建筑业务的拓展。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   经过两年时间的努力，杭州市场份额已经占到公司规模的百分之六十。这一年，东阳市建管局举行管理者技能比赛，总共三项，我参与了两项，都获得第一名。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">

</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    2001
   </span>
   年杭州红楼饭店封顶仪式，我讲话
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
