<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    林风眠
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    26
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    岁时就已经是北京国立艺专的校长了，而在他
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    38
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    岁以后，却经历了流亡逃难，妻女别离，孤独和困顿。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    他曾对外孙说：等我的画卖得好了，就给你们买个自己的房子
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ……
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    他的一生做过最重要的事情是
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    调和了中西方艺术文化，推翻了国画以水墨为上的宗旨，撼动了中国传统绘画千年不变的程式。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
<img alt="74.jpg" border="0" height="458" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5683/74.jpg" width="320"/>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    林风眠
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1900
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年出生。在他
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    19
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    岁时，受蔡元培的影响赴法求学。在学习了
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    3
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    个月的法文后，他考取了法国国立第戎美术学院。后来，院长将他介绍到了巴黎美术学院，拜著名油画家哥罗孟为师。两年后，林风眠游学德国，看到了塞尚、毕加索、马蒂斯的绘画，这些变形的人体，浓烈的色彩使林风眠受到了很大的震动，他创作了《柏林咖啡》《平静》等作品，画风单纯粗犷，明显是受到了后印象派和表现主义的影响。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
<img alt="75.jpg" border="0" height="374" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5683/75.jpg" width="550"/>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    《平静》
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1923
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，林风眠恋爱了，姑娘是柏林大学化学系的奥地利人爱丽丝
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ·
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    冯
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ·
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    罗达。在这一年的冬天，他们的婚礼在巴黎举行。婚后第二年，林风眠带着
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    42
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    幅作品参加了当时在斯特拉斯堡举办的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    中国古代与现代美术展
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，巨幅油画《摸索》也在这次展出中。可就在这时，妻子罗达分娩时感染了产褥热，两周后母子夭折。有人说他很爱罗达，因为即便到了晚年，他也常常
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    拿着罗达的照片端详。也有人说他并不那么爱罗达，因为在转年的春天，他就娶了法国姑娘爱丽丝
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ·
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    华丹。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
<img alt="76.jpg" border="0" height="329" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5683/76.jpg" width="550"/>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    《摸索》
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1926
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    26
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    岁的林风眠被聘为
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    北京国立艺专
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的校长。那时有学生闹学潮被开除，他在到任后以考试成绩重新决定去留。他还不顾反对，屡次登门邀请齐白石先生。齐白石说：这样恳切的心意，着实使我不好意思再推辞了。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
<img alt="77.jpg" border="0" height="405" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5683/77.jpg" width="500"/>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    林风眠一直反对抄袭，他还请了新文化运动的作家：周作人、谢冰心、郁达夫等人为艺专教文学。从法国聘请克罗多教西画。此时林风眠做的已经不是简单的艺术教育了，而是艺术革命。但是，上任没几天，三一八惨案就发生了，第二年，张作霖在北京逮捕杀害了李大钊，四月十二日，蒋介石在上海屠杀共产党，四月十五日血洗广州，与林风眠一同留法的好友熊君锐也在中山大学遇害了。这件事对林风眠的影响非常大，他创作了油画《人道》《人类的痛苦》。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
<img alt="78.jpg" border="0" height="369" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5683/78.jpg" width="500"/>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    林风眠一直主张艺术家要从画室走到民间，了解社会实际情况，纾解人民的苦难，他经常组织学生画模特，去校外写生。他觉得艺术离不开老百姓，不能由少数的贵族独享。但他这种大胆的改革方式遭到了抨击，教育部长教训林风眠用模特写生有伤风化，认为他的教育主张是和李大钊一样的赤色分子。迫于无奈，林风眠辞职了。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
<img alt="79.jpg" border="0" height="428" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5683/79.jpg" width="450"/>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1928
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，林风眠出任杭州国立艺专的校长。他教育学生用心去表达自己的思想，而不是简单的模仿和传承。在每次课堂上，他经过学生们的身后时总会说：放松一些，随便一点儿，多去看一些文艺、哲学、历史方面的书。也许正是因为这样，他培养出的学生撑起了中国现代美术的半壁江山，吴冠中，李可染，赵无极，董希文，席德进，苏天赐都是他一手带出来的学生，可见他在现代艺术中的地位。
   </span>
</p>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1937
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，艺专筹备十周年校庆，但在这时，战争也全面爆发了。国难当头，林风眠沉痛的告别了他亲手创建的杭州国立艺专，带着两百多名师生流亡。一路跋山涉水，走了半年才到达湖南沅陵，奉命与南迁的北平艺专合并。林风眠任校务主任委员。但杭州与北平的教育理念存在很多的矛盾，两个派系的学生针锋相对，甚至有斗殴的现象。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
<img alt="81.jpg" border="0" height="381" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5683/81.jpg" width="500"/>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1938
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    4
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    月，林风眠离开了沅陵。这一别他再也没有见过对他有着知遇之恩的蔡元培。直到
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1980
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年他移居香港，才由人搀扶着来到蔡元培的墓前献上了一束花。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
<img alt="82.jpg" border="0" height="491" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5683/82.jpg" width="500"/>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1939
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，林风眠在嘉陵江边找到了一个废弃的仓库，就在这里，他一直生活到抗战胜利。他自己买菜、做饭、洗衣、打扫。那些曾经当校长、住洋房、乘坐私人汽车的日子都已经远去。他说：人味耗尽，体验着中国几万万人的生活，才教我真正变成了
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    人
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。也是在这时，因为没有油画纸，林风眠在画油画时改用了四川宣纸，结果，这竟成了独特的林风眠风格的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    方纸布阵
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。他在仓库中不停的画，从早到晚，有时一天可以画九十多张。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
<img alt="83.jpg" border="0" height="423" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5683/83.jpg" width="500"/>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1944
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，潘天寿执掌艺专，第一件事就是去这个仓库请林风眠回校任教。在此后的抗战岁月里，林风眠每周徒步几十里山路到艺专，连续授课三天，再走回嘉陵江边。抗战胜利后，国立艺专重新分家，杭州与北平两校各自复员，可当他回到杭州踏进家门后，却发现他没有带走的所有油画，包括《摸索》《人道》《人类的痛苦》都被日本兵割的七零八落。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
<img alt="84.jpg" border="0" height="800" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5683/84.jpg" width="251"/>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1949
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，林风眠被复聘为教授，国立杭州艺专更名为
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    中央美术学院华东分院
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。有领导在大会上讲话：文艺复兴滚出去。显然，林风眠的中西融合不合时宜，他的那些花花草草和革命现实主义思想明显不同。他遭到了严重的排挤，走廊中有学生见他走来，会把身子贴在墙边，就像躲避瘟疫一样。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
<img alt="85.jpg" border="0" height="499" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5683/85.jpg" width="500"/>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1951
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，他托病辞职返回了上海家中，远离了艺术教育，他开始埋头作画，由太太卖给那些喜欢他画的外国人。最便宜的时候大概十块钱一张，还要卖一张送一张。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
<img alt="86.jpg" border="0" height="451" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5683/86.jpg" width="450"/>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1956
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，林风眠
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    56
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    岁时，妻女离开了上海去投奔巴西的亲戚。他把南昌路的房子退掉了一居，独自住在二楼。他想起了苦难的母亲，又想起了死于产褥热的罗达。他讲所有的情感全都渗透在了画布上，他画了很多关于救母情节的画。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
<img alt="87.jpg" border="0" height="463" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5683/87.jpg" width="400"/>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1966
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，文革来了。在听闻好友傅雷夫妇双双自尽后，他将自己数十年的画作撕碎，浸在浴缸中，等待画纸湿透再捣成纸浆。最后一勺一勺地舀入马桶中冲走，他的神情严肃又漠然。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
<img alt="88.jpg" border="0" height="458" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5683/88.jpg" width="500"/>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1968
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年夏天，有人举报林风眠是日本特务，他刚从单位里批斗回来就被抓了进去。他们轮流地、不停的审问他，甚至有时早上
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    9
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    点出去，要到深夜十一二点钟才回来。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
<img alt="89.jpg" border="0" height="337" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5683/89.jpg" width="500"/>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1972
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，关押了四年半的林风眠终于无罪释放，回到了南昌路的家。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
<img alt="90.jpg" border="0" height="449" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5683/90.jpg" width="450"/>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1978
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    已经
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    78
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    岁的老人林风眠转机四次，飞了四十多个小时来到了里约热内卢，为的就是看上一眼已经分别了
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    22
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
