<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    苏步青老校长在复旦学生活动中心二楼舞蹈房参加李源潮的婚礼茶话会。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 22px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1982
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年春秋季毕业的复旦数学系三百多名大学生中，
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    30
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年之后有的成为世界最著名的哈佛、斯坦福、剑桥等大学的终身教授、系主任，国内著名大学复旦、交大、北大等院校的院长、教授也不乏其人。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 22px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    78
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    级数学二班的孟晓犁被选为哈佛大学研究院院长，学术论文誉满全球。有的成为华尔街高盛、摩根斯坦利、德意志银行的执行董事。首席经济学家，
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    78
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    级数学三班的徐幼于在
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    2008
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年美国金融危机中成为
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    做空
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    老大，操控
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    50
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    亿美元大盘
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    做空
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，一举成名为新生代
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    金融大鳄
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 22px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    有的担当政府官员做了省长、市长、部长。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    77
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    级数学一班的李源潮在十八大后当选国家副主席。有的成为少将院士，如黄民强同学；也有的成为企业家、职业经理人、教师、工程师、研究员等等。总之，这一人群成为了杰出的精英人群。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 22px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    当网络上疯传这些同学名单的时候，我们还应该想到他们成功背后的启蒙老师，感恩这些培养出教授、专家、精英的教授们、班主任和辅导员，其中有七位院士教授，他们是苏步青、谷超豪、胡和生、李大潜、夏道行、洪家兴、陈恕行等。他们就是被网友称为
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    史上最牛班级
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的老师们。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 22px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
<img alt="121.jpg" border="0" height="374" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5667/121.jpg" width="550"/>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    苏步青先生给
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1978
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年全国中学生数学竞赛优胜者李骏、徐幼于、张琪等颁奖。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 22px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    苏步青老校长是最疼爱
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    78
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    级数学系学生的，他亲自推动
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1978
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年全国中学生数学竞赛，并且向教育部申请特殊政策，对前
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    20
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    名优胜者可以免试上复旦数学系。当时的前三名李骏、徐幼于、张琪等都是这样进入复旦的。在我们入学的第一天，苏老给我们做欢迎词，还豪迈地说，我的名字叫苏步青，所以我的学生
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    数不清
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    呀。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 22px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    记得新入学不久，我们这批学生中有许多是跳级上大学的，最小的几名学生像孟晓犁、李世康、张坚等只有十五十六岁，还很有少年稚气也贪玩。课余后有几个同学在三号楼男生宿舍前平台上玩
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    打弹子
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，正好被巡视的苏校长看到，大家慌得不得了，苏校长走过却安慰大家，要劳逸结合，发愤图强，为四化作贡献。此后，大家再也不玩这些东西了。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 22px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    大学毕业时，天气特别热，苏老已经是年过八旬老人，但他还是坚持站在烈日下，与每个班级拍完年级集体照和班级集体照。而这些照片都是我们最珍贵的记忆。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 22px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
<img alt="122.jpg" border="0" height="369" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5667/122.jpg" width="500"/>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    谷超豪先生和胡和生先生与
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    7818
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    级数学三班同学座谈。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 22px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    谷超豪先生和胡和生先生是一对恩爱伉俪、院士夫妇，是苏老最得意的两位弟子。他们德高望重，学术造诣深厚，解题思路清晰敏捷，给高材生们出题讲题，都是一点就通。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 22px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
<img alt="123.jpg" border="0" height="400" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5667/123.jpg" width="300"/>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    严绍宗教授。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 22px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    严绍宗教授是一位大高个、中气十足的人，在
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    3208
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    教室给我们整个年级
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    200
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    多名学生上《实变函数》。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    30
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年后，严克任同学讲起严老师时非常生动，他说，函数是有可微的，可积的，可没有听说函数是可操的，但严老师总说
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    硬操起一个函数
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
