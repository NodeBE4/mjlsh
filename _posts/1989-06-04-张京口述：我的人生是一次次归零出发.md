   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>

  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    在异国屋檐下回忆过去，往事洞若烛照。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我是
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    68
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年下乡的老知青，我一直认为我的人生经验大都来自那个时期。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

  </p>

   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    从初中没毕业到上山下乡，从工厂做工到考上大学，从外企辞职到美国继续求学，一路走来最后做到世界
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    60
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    岁退休以后的生活和改变。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>

   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    那是一个理想主义的年代。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    身为将门之后，我血液里流淌着浓厚的英雄主义色彩，我为父辈的业绩感到自豪，自己也热烈地向往着辉煌的业绩，从小受母亲影响，我很想成为那种能做开颅手术的脑外科医生，所以我不像一般的女孩子喜欢玩布偶娃娃，我最喜欢的事情是摆弄父亲的军功章，听父亲讲他的战斗故事。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS";'>

  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    父母忙，我
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    5
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    岁就上小学。后来
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    60
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年全家随父亲的调动迁至吉林。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1965
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，我刚上初一，文革开始，失控的校园再也听不到朗朗的读书声。在航空学院当政委的父亲被批斗游行，接着被关进牛棚。母亲工作保姆离开，做为家中四个孩子的老大，
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    13
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    岁的我突然肩负重任，一边照顾弟弟妹妹，一边把做好的饭给父亲送去。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1968
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，我得到了这张初中毕业证。其实整个初中，我们只读了一年书，其它两年，学校全都在停课闹革命：参加全国大串连，进京接受毛主席的接见。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    当时，早熟的我喜欢和更有见识的高年级同学玩，我有机会读到了被禁的外国小说：《基督山伯爵》、《娜拉》
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    …
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    书页朽了，书中的故事却不朽，一种人生观早早在我心里扎下了根：人要掌握自己的命运，任何时候都要清楚自己想要什么。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

  </p>

   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>

    我们头顶

   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的桂冠，却带着一颗精神世界一贫如洗的头脑和一个没有完全发育成熟的身体，被逐出了城市，抛向农村。差不多在同一时间，一个人类的使者在全人类的目光注视下登上了月球，而我们却唱着苏联老歌
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    再见吧，亲爱的故乡，胜利的星会照耀我们
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    …”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    奔向了荒芜的土地。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我和另外六个女孩三个男孩一路坐卡车，换马车，颠簸着到了吉林省舒兰县西崴子公社。当我跳下马车，看到简陋破败的农屋，我知道生活这才开始。这是
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年下乡前和同学的合影。前排右一是我。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    老三届
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
