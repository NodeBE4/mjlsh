<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC"; font-kerning: none;'>
    今天网上热传一张照片，因为是国内最权威的官方报纸刊登，所以引起人们的热议，社交平台
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC"; font-kerning: none;'>
    刷屏
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC"; font-kerning: none;'>
    了。今年
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    2
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC"; font-kerning: none;'>
    月
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    22
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC"; font-kerning: none;'>
    日，浙江绍兴中心医院一位
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    3
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC"; font-kerning: none;'>
    岁的小患者（阴性）治愈出院，他向护士阿姨鞠躬致敬。一百多年前，杭州广济医院（今浙大医学院附属第二医院的前身）创始人梅藤更先生（官方媒体写的是
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC"; font-kerning: none;'>
    梅藤根
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，音相似字不同，以前约定俗成叫梅藤
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC"; font-kerning: none;'>
    更
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ）也有和小患者的鞠躬照。两张照片穿越百年的照片放在一起，情景相似动作一致，医者和患者相互尊重，确实非常和谐。一年前我曾专文写过梅藤更医生的一些事迹，尤其梅藤更的夫人梅福孙，知道的人还是不多。所以今天找出旧文再补充一些材料，希望读者朋友可以更多了解梅藤更夫妇其人其事。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">

<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    百年前的老照片
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">

<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    曾几何时，医者和患者的关系绝对不是对立的。梅藤更是晚清民国时代的人，这张照片拍的是一位病孩和他相互作揖的场景。不少朋友感叹：为什么当年能彼此尊重，如今在一些地方却有了变化？到底出了什么问题？医患的问题不是我可以作解释的，但我可以讲讲老照片，还有一些历史。图中这位向病孩拱手作揖的医生梅藤更，来自苏格兰，他是杭州浙医二院前身广济医院的创始人，中文名梅藤更，英文叫
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    David Duncan Main
   </span>
<span class="s4" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ，戴维
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ·
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    邓肯
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ·
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    缅因。其实这张拱手作揖的照片还有后续，若干年以后孩子长大了，和梅藤更医生又见了面。他们再次合影，这次梅藤更医生站着，当年的小孩子坐着，梅医生俏皮地作势要打小孩子，摄影者不失时机地抓住了这个极富生活情趣的瞬间。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    梅藤更和长大后的病孩合影

<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>

<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">

<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    梅藤更
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1856
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年出生于苏格兰，他夫人是位护士，中文名梅福孙，英文是弗洛伦斯
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ·
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    南丁格尔
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ·
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    史密斯（
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    Florence Nightingale Smith
   </span>
<span class="s4" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ），因为弗洛伦斯的母亲崇拜
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    白衣天使
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    南丁格尔，所以给女儿起了个一模一样的名字。她一直陪伴梅藤更，也活得像自己的名字一样，是位白衣天使。梅藤更在杭州生活了
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    45
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，非常热爱中国，他一生遵循的座右铭是
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “Live and Work”
   </span>
<span class="s4" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ，他说：
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    生活里的乐趣和欢笑，比药更能让生病的人觉得活着之振奋
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。梅藤更
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1926
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年回国，当时已经
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    70
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    岁了。他在苏格兰又生活了
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    8
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，死于
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1934
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年。在离开中国之前，他宴请杭州地方人士，用汉语杭州话说了这样一段话：
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我知道中国是有前途的，后一代的青年更是了不起！可惜我老了，来不及看到中国的复兴。
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>

<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    可惜我老了，来不及看到中国的复兴
   </span>
<span class="s3" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”

<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
