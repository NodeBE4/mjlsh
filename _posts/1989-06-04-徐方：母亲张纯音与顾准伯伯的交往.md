</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
<img alt="25.jpg" border="0" height="399" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5602/25.jpg" width="300"/>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    顾准（
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1915.7.1
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    －
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1974.12.3
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ），上海人，字哲云。中国当代学者、思想家、经济学家、会计学家、历史学家。中国最早提出社会主义市场经济理论的第一人。文集有《顾准文集》《顾准日记》《顾准自述》等。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    母亲张纯音出身于书香门第。我的外曾祖父程树德是近代著名中国法律史学家，上世纪初由清廷公派赴日本法政大学留学，归国后任翰林院编修。其代表作《九朝律考》被翻译成十几国外文，是研究中国法律史不可或缺的重要文献。我的外祖父张耀翔早年考取
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    庚款
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    留学，由清华学堂保送至美国哥伦比亚大学，
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1920
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年获心理学硕士学位后归国，开创了中国心理学专业，并亲任中国心理学会首任会长。是国内外公认的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    中国心理学第一人
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我的外祖母程俊英是中国第一批女大学生，是李大钊、胡适的亲灸弟子。她深受新思潮影响，积极投身五四运动，与庐隐、王世瑛、陈定秀等三位女性并称为
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    五四四公子
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。正是在这种家庭环境的熏陶下，母亲很早就具有知识分子的独立人格，对各种问题都有独到见解，从不苟同俗见、随波逐流。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1943
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，母亲考取上海交通大学财务管理系。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1956
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年经郑振铎推荐，到中国科学院经济研究所工作，时年
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    29
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    岁。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
<img alt="26.jpg" border="0" height="437" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5602/26.jpg" width="323"/>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    张纯音
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    初相识
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    母亲与顾准伯伯相识于
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1962
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年。顾于
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1958
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年被下放到农村劳改，
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    4
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年后摘掉右派帽子，再次来到经济所。当时经济所的办公地点在三里河经委大楼，管理得相当好，整洁而有序。母亲所在的办公室里有一张白色木制单人床，上面铺着雪白的床单，供研究人员午休用。顾准伯伯回到所里第一天上班，在后勤人员的陪同下，到各办公室转转，跟大家见见面。他走进母亲的办公室，环视了一下房间，高兴地往床上一躺，说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    哈，没想到我这个穷小子一跤跌到了青云里！
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    当别人介绍说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    这位是张纯音同志
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    时，他说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    哦，你就是张纯音啊。告诉你，我还是坚持我那篇文章的观点
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    !”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    母亲年轻好胜，回了一句：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我也坚持我那篇文章的观点
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    原来事情始于
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1956
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，当时的科学院副院长张劲夫安排顾准去资源综合考察委员会（简称综考会）任副主任，并根据顾准自己的要求兼做经济所的研究工作。而顾准在经济所的研究工作，深入探讨了当时的一些理论禁区，并撰写了《试论社会主义制度下的商品生产和价值规律》一文，发表在《经济研究》
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1957
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年第
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    2
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    期上。该文主张通过经济核算的手段来达到最大的经济效果，提高效率，以市场价格、市场规律调节生产。文章指出
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    市场经济是社会主义经济的必由之路
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，在此之前中国经济学界还从未有人提出过这样的观点。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    正是这篇具有划时代意义的文章，使顾准逝世二十年后，获得了
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    中国市场经济第一人
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的美誉，而在当时却被视为离经叛道的邪恶异端。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1957
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年开展的反右运动中，《试论》一文中的观点被定性为严重的右派言论，成了顾准的罪状之一；另一项罪名是
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    反苏
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。他在综考会时，参加了中苏联合考察队，赴黑龙江探讨两国合作利用水力资源。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    那时正值
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    一边倒
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    时期，别人见到苏联专家一般都唯唯诺诺，唯命是从。可顾准为了维护祖国利益，在坝址的选择上与苏方针锋相对，争执得很厉害，还对苏联专家傲慢无礼的态度表示不满。他的这些言行被人打了小报告。当时兼任文教系统领导的康生，在看到顾准的材料后，当众恶狠狠地骂道：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    顾准这种人不是右派，谁是右派？！
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    康生这一表态，把顾准划成右派就成了不可更改的组织决定。①
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1957
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年母亲刚到经济所不久，顾准就被划成了右派。经济所要对他的右派言论进行
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    消毒
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，上方给母亲布置了一项任务
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    :
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    写文章批判顾准的《试论社会主义制度下的商品生产和价值规律》。当时母亲很年轻，刚三十出头，属于进步青年一类。很快，由她主笔写出了一篇奉命文字，标题为《驳斥顾准关于价值规律的修正主义观点》，刊登在《经济研究》
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1957
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年第
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    6
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    期上。多年后，当母亲和顾准伯伯一起回忆起母亲写的那篇批判文章，顾伯伯哈哈大笑说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    咱们真是不打不相识啊！
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    顾准
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1962
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年回到经济所后，被安排到政治经济学组工作，恰好与母亲同在一组。通过接触，母亲逐渐认识到顾准这个人非同凡响
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    :
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    他头脑清楚、看问题敏锐、知识渊博。特别是他在治学方面刻苦认真的态度，给母亲留下了深刻的印象。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    文革
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    前顾准在翻译西方经济学名著时，常碰到一些数学公式。为了更好地理解西方经济学理论，他借了一本很厚的高等数学，不厌其烦地一道道习题进行演算。这种刻苦治学精神在老干部中殊不多见。②
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    给母亲印象特别深的还有顾准那铮铮铁骨。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    文革
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    初期，顾准受到更大的冲击，红卫兵勒令他们这些
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    牛鬼蛇神
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    交代自己的罪行。别人都小心翼翼把写好的交代材料贴到布告栏上；唯独顾准只在一张稿纸上写了两个大大的字：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    读史
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，便贴了上去。造反派将他痛打一顿，责问为何要这么写。他说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    因为最近什么都没干，只读了些史书。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    事后他对母亲讲：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    眼见那些红卫兵到处打、砸、抢、抄家，这些将来都得写进历史。我倒要看看中国会变成什么样，中国向何处去
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ……”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    母亲认为顾准在文革初期写下
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    读史
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    这两个字，说明他当时就已经理性地看待文革。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    据母亲讲：顾准做事非常讲原则，从不畏惧强权。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    文革
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    中他给前来外调的人和给本所的人先后写了几十万字的交代材料。在这些材料中，每句话都实事求是，从不为了讨好某人，或夹杂个人恩怨而写不负责任的话。从
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1967
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    3
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    月起，找顾准外调的人逐渐增多，
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1968
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年冬达到高潮，最多时一天就要应对三、四拨前来外调的人。当时如果不顺着他们的口径来讲，就要吃苦头。一次，有几个红卫兵来到经济所，要求顾准写一份材料，说明上海某人过去曾和国民党有瓜葛。可他说从来不知道这件事。当时红卫兵打了他一个耳光。他干脆把脸送过去，让红卫兵一连串打了十几个耳光。红卫兵见顾准怒目而视，也就打不下去了。后来顾准给他们的材料仍然坚持写不知道这件事。③
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    母亲是个仗义豪侠的人，她交友只看重两条：一是德，二是才。绝不因朋友的荣辱升降而有所改变。文革期间，孙冶方、骆耕漠等德高望重的学者挨整，她曾多次暗中帮助过他们。顾准那刚直不阿的品质与渊博的学识令母亲折服，跟他成为好友，直到他去世。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    文革初期，顾准被隔离审查，存款冻结，每月只发给二十元生活费。母亲想到他既要吃饭，又要抽烟，经济上一定非常拮据。于是趁没人的时候，偷偷在他办公桌上放了八十元钱。当时八十元不是一个小数目，接近她一个月的工资。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1972
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年他们从干校回到北京后，顾准的经济状况略有好转。一次他拿出八十元，说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    这钱还给你。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    母亲觉得奇怪：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    你怎么知道那钱是我放的？
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    他说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我当然知道，除了你不会有第二个人。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    他还说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    这钱真是及时雨，当时我都险些没钱吃饭了，几次想找你借，只是不好意思开口
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ……”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1969
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年春节过后，顾准随同其他监管对象，被押送到位于建国门外的学部大院接受隔离审查。他虽然在此之前已被迫与夫人汪碧离婚，子女又都跟他断绝了关系，可内心深处无时无刻不惦记着他深爱着的妻子、儿女，幻想着有朝一日能与妻子破镜重圆。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1969
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    11
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    月初，学部正式宣布要下放到河南息县五七干校。经济所作为
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    先遣队
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，将于
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    11
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    月
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    16
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    日出发。此时，顾准并不知道妻子已于一年多前自杀身亡，仍一再写申请要求在走之前与妻子见上一面。对于所领导、工宣队的支吾、搪塞，他突然有一种不祥的预感，意识到他的秀（汪碧）或许已不在人世。他急迫地对连指导员杨清华保证：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    无论她死了、疯了，病重了，都一不影响下去，二不影响改造。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    领导这才对他说了实话。汪碧的死讯，对顾准犹如晴天霹雳，悲伤欲绝。他在当天的日记中凄惨地写到：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    闻噩耗，既觉意外，也不觉意外
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ……
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我此时只想知道她死时的情况。赵说，他们帮我找找，我就去打饭来吃。吃了几口饭，悲从中来，脸伏在饭盆上失声大号。但我还是抑制住，努力把饭吃完。我要活下去
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ……”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ④
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    顾准接下来恳求军宣队帮他找一下大女儿稖头（顾淑林），希望在走之前跟她见一面，一起在外面吃顿饭，顺便了解一下亲人的消息。军宣队领导同意了，派人去和稖头联系，可她竟拒绝会见。⑤孩子的绝情，对他来说更是雪上加霜。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    经历干校
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    学部下干校，母亲带上了我。那年我只有
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    15
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    岁，还是个稚气未脱的少年。母亲为了让我了解顾准伯伯，同她一道照顾这位老朋友，跟我讲述了许多关于伯伯的事
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    :
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    他的人品、他的学问，以及他所遭遇的种种不幸。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    母亲说她第一次知道顾准这个名字，是在解放初期的上海。那时顾准任上海税务局长，三天两头签发关于税收的公告，满大街贴的都是署名
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    顾准
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的告示，他被人们戏称为
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    布告局长
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。没想到这位
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    布告局长
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    不久之后就因莫须有的罪名遭到撤职，落难后两度来到经济所，再后来竟成为她的好朋友。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    对于母亲描述顾准这个右派是
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    第一流的德和才
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，我一点儿也不觉得奇怪。她以前就曾跟我说过，右派分子往往是一些耿直且德才兼备的好人。你姨姥姥和舅公就是右派，他们不都是人品好、学问高的好人吗？对于母亲帮助落难学者，我更不感到意外。她的善良和乐于助人在亲友、同事当中是有名的。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    记得刚下干校那会儿，一天夜晚下着小雨，母亲开完会往宿舍走。这时，她看到老经济学家骆耕漠被困在遍布积水的泥路上。骆老是个知识分子老干部，某些方面很像顾准伯伯：早年参加革命，没上过大学，是自学成才的经济学家。他解放后曾任国家计委第一副主任，
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1958
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年因潘、杨事件受牵连，被削去职务，贬到经济所。文革期间更被打成
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    走资派、反动资产阶级学术权威
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，冠以
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    叛徒、特务
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    嫌疑，一直挨整，没人敢跟他沾边儿。他患有严重的青光眼，几近失明。东岳是粘土地，天一下雨，道路就变得异常泥泞。正所谓：下雨一泡脓，天晴一片铜。骆伯伯穿的是雨鞋，一脚踩到泥水里，抬腿时脚出来了，鞋却粘在地上。如果换了别人，自己可以把鞋子拔出来。可骆伯伯眼睛看不见，根本找不到鞋，在泥水中不知已经站了多久，却无人相助。母亲见状，赶紧上前，帮他把鞋子从泥水中捞出，套在脚上。她知道骆老视力极差，只剩下微弱的光感，告诉他雨夜行走千万别往亮处踩。因为亮处都是水，之所以发亮，是因为反射了月光。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1990
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年代初，骆老在接受《顾准全传》作者高建国的采访时谈到这样一件事：干校期间，他因挨整被彻底孤立，没有一个人敢跟他说话，只有张纯音悄悄对他说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我知道你的为人，绝不可能是叛徒、特务，你可千万要挺住啊！
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ⑥几年后，高建国见到笔者，谈到那次采访。他说骆老讲那件事时老泪纵横、激动不已，说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    当时我被整得昏天黑地，甚至连自己都不相信自己是好人了。张纯音的那句话对我是多大的安慰啊！
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    刚下干校时，政治气氛左得出奇。顾准伯伯是监管对象，经常挨斗，处境极为恶劣。而母亲却冒着受牵连的危险，继续暗中与他来往。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    顾伯伯的胞弟陈敏之在他故去后撰文这样记述：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    五哥生前就曾告诉过我：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1969
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    11
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    月经济所从北京搬去河南息县时，他正是刚刚获悉五嫂（汪碧）去世已经一年多，稖头拒绝和五哥会见，不仅是精神上受到最严重打击的时候，也是生活上最艰苦的时候。就是在这种情况下，纯音同志和他的女儿咪咪给予五哥最难得的关心和照顾
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ……”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ⑦
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    干校初期伙食特别差。母亲考虑到顾伯伯身体不好，一定需要营养补充。而我们下来之前料到干校生活会很艰苦，带了不少奶粉、肉罐头等食品。于是母亲想到给顾伯伯送去一些。当时做这样的事是非常危险的，一旦被抓住，扣上一顶与监管对象勾勾搭搭的帽子。在那个
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阶级斗争一抓就灵
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的年代，轻则大小会点名批判，重则隔离审查，失去人身自由。于是母亲想让我去送，一个十几岁的孩子不会像大人那么惹眼。我那时已经懂事，对顾伯伯的种种遭遇深感同情，很愿意做这件事。记得我每次送的时候都紧张得要死，心都快从嗓子眼儿里蹦出来了，感觉像是地下党在做接头工作。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    刚下干校不久，顾伯伯来向母亲借布票。当时买布除了要花钱，还得用布票。可伯伯自己平时积攒的布票有限，还差一些。他在当天的日记中写道：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    借张纯音布票二尺，买维尼纶混纺布三十尺
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ……
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    用大量素白维尼纶混纺布，枕套被里都是。这次整理起来的被服，大体可以用到我长辞人世之日。服丧从白，自古礼也
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ……”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ⑧原来顾伯伯很想悼念亡妻汪碧。可在那个极左、高压的政治环境下，他既不可能穿孝服，也不可能戴黑纱，于是想到多买一些白布，做成白色被套、枕套，以这种方式来寄托哀思。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    母亲曾跟我提到这样一件事：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1971
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年林彪事件发生后，先以中央文件的形式向党内高级干部传达，然后是党内普通干部，最后到一般群众。但对
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    牛鬼蛇神
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    还是保密。一天，母亲要将一只箱子送到火车站托运。她自己弄不了，请所里派人帮忙，结果派来的人恰好是顾伯伯。他拉着一辆架子车，把箱子放在车上。一路上他们在边走边聊，母亲趁此机会把林彪事件跟他讲了。他说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我对党内派系斗争的来龙去脉很清楚，早就料到会有这一天。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    亦师亦友
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1972
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    10
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    月，学部从干校迁回北京。我家因下去之前退掉了房子，被安排住在学部大院八号楼二层。这是一座
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    U
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    字形两层的筒子楼，过去是招待所。顾伯伯有家不能归，也住在这一层楼上。本来跟他同住一室的还有经济所李学曾。李在北京大概还有其他住处，多数时间都不在，故那个
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    12
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    平方米的房间实际上是顾伯伯一个人住。自
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1966
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年文革开始他被隔离审查，到从干回到北京，伯伯第一次有了自己的私人空间。他的住室在八号楼二层西侧最南端，离我家非常近，出门沿过道往北走，经过几个房门往东一拐就是我家。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    位于八号楼南边的七号楼，是一座一字型两层的筒子楼，也用来安置从干校回来的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    无家可归者
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。钱钟书先生与夫人杨绛就住在七号楼一层。他们家的后窗正对着我家的前窗，夏天的时候他们常打开后窗通风。我弟弟是智障者，不懂事，有时在家里拉开喉咙放声高歌。每当这时，钱先生家的后窗很快就会关上，可他们从来不找我家提意见。一次我在大院里碰到钱钟书先生，他操着带有无锡口音的普通话，半开玩笑地对我说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    令弟的歌喉不错啊！
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    听得我差点儿憋不住笑出来。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    这时运动重点是清查
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    五一六
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，人们忙着打派仗，顾不上这些
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    死老虎
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。顾准伯伯便利用这难得的安宁，开始着手他那庞大的探索研究。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    这段时间是母亲与顾伯伯交往最多的阶段，几乎每天都与他长谈。伯伯白天去北京图书馆收集资料、写读书笔记，晚上跟母亲谈他的思想、他的写作计划、以及他对各种问题的看法。他说很喜欢这样的谈天，等于梳理思想。母亲常对他的一些精辟见解赞叹不已，称他是天才的思想家，每天都能
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    分泌
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    出精彩的思想。对他学问的评价：一是博，二是深。有时母亲不同意他的某些观点，于是两人争执不休，面红耳赤。甚至为某个字的读音也要辨清孰是孰非。不过，这些都只是学术之争，丝毫不会影响他们之间的友情。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    顾伯伯在思想上颇受基督教某些教义的影响，认为这个世界最终还是要实现大同，
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    四海之内，皆为兄弟
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。他所奉行的座右铭是：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    宁可天下人负我，勿让我负天下人
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。正是本着这一原则，他对所有过去整过他、害过他的人，一律宽恕。母亲对此颇不以为然，认为《圣经》中的那句话：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    别人打了你的左脸，伸出右脸也让他打
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    是奴隶主义哲学，不足取。她说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    顾伯伯则争辩道：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    人类社会正是因为有强烈的报复之心，你打我一拳，我踢你一脚，才总是争斗不已。如果大家都怀有宽容仁爱之心，这个世界会好得多。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    对于他们的讨论，我感到非常有意思。顾伯伯随即从书架上抽出一本中英文对照的《新约全书》（圣经），建议我读一读。这本书是他于
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1961
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年在北京王府井东安市场旧书摊上掏到的，上面有他的签名。是他写《从理想主义到经验主义》等著作的重要参考书。他去世后，遵照他的遗嘱，作为纪念品留给了我，一直珍藏至今。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    一次，顾伯伯在谈话中用了
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    饮鸩止渴
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    这个成语。他把
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    鸩
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    字读成了
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    chén
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，母亲说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    你这个字读错了，应该读
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    zhèn
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，结果两人争执起来，互不相让。这时顾伯伯从书架上抽出刚刚在内部发行的《现代汉语词典》，查出
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    鸩
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    字果然读
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    zhèn

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

</span>

</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    那段时间，顾伯伯完全沉浸在学问当中，已经达到忘我的地步。他每天大量阅读，写读书笔记。每周读的书能开出一个长长的单子。不过，对于天才与勤奋的关系，他跟母亲的观点倒惊人地一致。谈到爱迪生，他说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    99%
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的汗水加
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1%
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的灵感，可是若没有那

<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1%
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，他也就什么都不是了。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    有人说顾准恃才傲物，这倒不冤枉他。一次谈到
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    虚心使人进步，骄傲使人落后
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我的看法反其道而行之：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

    ’”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    顾伯伯很注意跟上时代，站在学术最前沿。之所以能做到这一点，须提及当时经济所图书馆长宗井滔先生。宗先生过去在中央研究院（经济所前身）就搞图书管理，懂好几国外文。他是个有心人，文革期间，其他单位图书馆大多陷于瘫痪，他却敢于动用外汇，一直坚持购进国外最新图书、期刊，当中只断过一、两年。据我所知，顾伯伯最后两年读的书当中，历史学文献大部分来自北图；而经济学文献则大部分来自经济所图书馆。宗先生不断地进书，顾伯伯不断地读书，这种情景在文革期间极为罕见。可以这样说，顾伯伯当年能够及时了解并吸收上世纪六七十年代西方经济学，宗井滔功不可没。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1957
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年在反右运动中挨整，差点儿划成右派，他的代理所长职务也丢了。顾准对巫先生评价非常高，认为他很了解西方经济学流派，有真学问。当时经济所的人大多认为巫先生从不谈经济思想，可顾伯伯却跟吴敬琏叔叔说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    巫先生实际上一直跟踪着世界经济学的演变，对现代经济学的源流十分清楚。只是由于政治原因变得很谨慎，绝口不谈西方经济学，而只谈中国古代经济思想史，如管子什么的。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    JoanRobinson
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ）在经济学流派中的地位，跟巫宝三讨论了之后，了解到她那篇《经济学的第二次革命》意义之所在，并随后翻译了罗滨逊夫人的《经济论文集》。罗宾逊夫人是左翼凯恩斯主义学者，时称
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    新剑桥学派
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，她的学说与哈耶克的新自由主义、萨缪尔森为代表的美国凯恩斯主义为当时鼎足而立的一大学派，对于那个时候的中国学者来说，她的思想似乎更容易接受。⑨
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    顾准伯伯学术水平高，英文又好，所里一些勤奋好学的中年人在这期间常向他请教。他跟我们开玩笑说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    最近收了几个学生
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    牛鬼蛇神
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    收学生，在文革期间可是个稀罕事。就我所知，他的得意门生是吴敬琏，其他人还有赵人伟、周叔莲、张曙光、林青松等，当然也包括我母亲。他跟这些人讨论问题，指导他们读书、翻译西方经济学论文，对提高他们的学术水平起了重要的作用。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    当时经济所的这些研究人员由于与世界隔绝了几十年，对西方经济学几乎完全不了解。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1972
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年从干校回到北京后，在顾准伯伯的带领下，所里几位中年研究人员开始如饥似渴地研究和吸收西方经济学的最新理念。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    一次，顾伯伯从宗井滔先生那里拿来一本美国经济学会的高级学术刊物：《美国经济评论》（
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    AmericanEconomicReview
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ），从中选出几篇最新的探索性经济学论文分给吴敬琏叔叔、赵人伟叔叔和我母亲，让他们拿去翻译，翻好之后由他来批改。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    母亲中学时代上的是上海中西女中和工部局女中。这是两所教会学校，英语教学质量非常高，到了高中就开始读《傲慢与偏见》等原版小说。可解放后国家一边倒向苏联，同时政治运动不断，她二十多年不用英语，有点儿生疏了。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1971
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    7
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    月，基辛格秘密访华，之后中美双方发表了联合公告，宣布美国总统尼克松将于
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1972
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    5
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    月前访华。这是个爆炸性新闻。当时还在干校的母亲，马上意识到中美对峙了二十多年，关系终于出现了转机。为了跟上时代变化，她决定重拾英语，找了些书开始复习。可这事很快就引起了上方的注意，连长靳某某在全所大会上不点名批判：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    尼克松要访华，有人就闻风而动学起英语来，她到底想做什么？！
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    母亲有一次跟赵人伟叔叔谈到顾准伯伯给她布置的翻译作业。她说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    可我啃了一两个礼拜都啃不下来。勉强译了几段拿给他看，他看了之后说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ‘
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    程度还是不够
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ’
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    听赵人伟叔叔讲，有一天他把翻译好的论文交给顾伯伯。本来那天顾伯伯是要去北京图书馆查资料的，可为了帮赵叔叔改翻译稿，他放弃了去北图，边批改边讲解，整整花了七个小时，用红笔在稿纸的四周改得密密麻麻的。不但修改译文的措辞，还详细解释为什么用这个词而不用那个词，其内涵是什么，讲得非常透彻。如：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “mechanism”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    这个词要翻译成
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    机制
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    而不是
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    机构
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，因为它指的是机体的运作方式。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>

</p>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    顾伯伯说：

<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    不会的，我了解他，他是个有思想的人，有思想的人是不会发疯的。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    他还说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    冶方若放出来，他第一个要见的一定是我。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    据母亲讲，文革初期，孙、顾二人都被打成
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    黑帮
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。红卫兵
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    勒令
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    他们打扫楼道和厕所，他们每天故意去得特别早，趁楼里还没有人来，边干活边交换思想，讨论对
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    文革
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的看法。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    4
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    月，孙冶方以
   </span>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    里通外国

<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1939
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，顾准从江苏省职委调至文委，与孙冶方曾经在一起工作。五十年代中期，顾准到经济所搞研究工作，再一次与孙冶方共事。他们在探讨理论问题时，顾准认为：价值规律在社会主义经济中具有重要的作用。在五十年代能提出这样的看法是非常难得的。正是受这一观点的启发，孙冶方撰写了《把计划和统计放在价值规律的基础上》一文，发表在《经济研究》
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1956
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年第
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    6
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    期上。文中深刻阐述了价值规律对社会主义经济的重要意义，并对按照主观意志
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    不计盈亏
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    大办工业等做法，提出了尖锐批评。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1963
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，他在一次关于经济理论问题的辩论会
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    (
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    实为批判会
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    )
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    上
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ,
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    以语不惊人死不休的气慨冲口而出
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    :
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    在社会主义建设中
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ,“
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    千规律
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ,
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    万规律
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ,
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    价值规律第一条
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    孙冶方
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1975
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年出狱后，对价值规律进一步作了全面研究，确立了他在中国经济学界领袖的地位。然而他在临终前却多次对吴敬琏等学生明确指出：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我于
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年代提出价值规律，是受了顾准的启发。今后再整理出版文集时，一定要在后记中注明这一点。①孙冶方讲这话时顾准已去世多年，如果他自己不说出来别人并不知道这些事，这充分体现了孙冶方先生的学术道德之高尚。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    顾准伯伯为了节省时间，从不自己做饭，而是去食堂打饭来吃。每到吃饭时间，总会看到他拿个很大的白色搪瓷缸子，径直朝食堂走去。学部食堂的大锅饭营养倒是够了，可味道实在不敢恭维。母亲有时烧了比较可口的的菜，如红烧排骨什么的，就会分出一些让我给顾伯伯端过去。即便如此，她还是对顾伯伯的健康忧心忡忡。一次，她跟老友骆耕漠说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    从那以后，骆老、江明，还有我母亲，时不时会拉顾伯伯去附近的小饭馆儿吃饭，并借机陪他逛逛街，每次他都感到非常高兴。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    最后的夙愿

   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    请六弟选择一些纪念物品，代我送给张纯音同志和她的女儿咪咪。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    母亲后来对我讲，她感到非常安慰，说明顾准很看重他们之间的友情。她说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ……”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    她讲这话是在
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1975
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，当时中国还处于
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    文革
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    －－那个最黑暗的历史时期，世上真正理解顾准的人屈指可数。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    顾准伯伯去世后，一次他弟弟陈敏之来北京联系顾伯伯译著出版事宜，顺便到我家探望母亲。当他们谈起顾准的时候，我看到母亲流泪了。这是我生平第一次，也是唯一的一次见到她落泪。她是个内心极为刚强的女性，生活中无论遇到多大的艰难困苦，哪怕是得知自己患了绝症即将离世，都从未掉过一滴泪。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    就在顾准伯伯辞世的几个月后，母亲突患急性类风湿关节炎，大病了一场。身上多处关节变形，几乎不能行走。医生说这种病的一个可能病因是精神上受到强烈刺激。母亲哀伤地对我说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我知道为什么会得这个病，因为顾准死了
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ……”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    母亲与我
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    母亲于
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1989
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    5
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    月被发现患了子宫中胚叶肉瘤。这是一种恶性程度极高的肿瘤，早期很容易随血液转移扩散至全身。她很快住进医院，接受了手术。可几个月后，医生告诉我母亲的病已经到了晚期，癌肿已大面积转移至肝、肺、骨。他们所能做的只是尽可能为她延长生命、减少痛苦
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ……
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我惊呆了，完全无法接受这个事实，泪水止不住地往下流
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ……
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    对母亲，我没有隐瞒，将病情一五一十地讲了。她当然很震惊，却出乎意料地平静，说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    到底是我的好女儿，懂得我。谢谢你讲了实情，让我有比较充裕的时间安排身后之事。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我问能为她做什么？她说只希望在生命最后的这些日子里每天见到我。从那一刻起，我每天下班后直奔医院，守在她的床前。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    在我所认识的人当中，像我跟母亲这种关系并不多见。记得在兰州大学读书期间，她每天都给我写一封信。我功课忙，隔三差五地回一封。母亲为了提高我的写作能力，每次接到信，都用红笔在上面勾勾划划地批改，甚至指出个别错别字，然后再寄回来。班上有个负责开信箱的女同学，每天都会为我取回一封厚厚的信，信封上的落款儿为
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    北京张
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。她跟我开玩笑说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    老实坦白，这个
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ‘
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    北京张
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ’
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    是你在北京交的男朋友吧？
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    才不是呢，这是我妈。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    她说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    打死我也不信，哪有当妈的每天给孩子写一封信的？！
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我曾对一位
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    闺蜜
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    眼见母亲在癌病的折磨下苦苦挣扎，一天天萎顿下去，我心如刀割！我对她讲：不能想象这个世上没有她我还怎么活
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ……
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    可她却劝慰道：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    傻孩子，千万别这么想，这个世界离开谁地球都照样转。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    她还引用了刘禹锡的两句诗：沉舟侧畔千帆过，病树前头万木春。母亲说她感到很安慰，三十几年来按照她的理想塑造了一个人。这个人无论是外貌还是内心都很像她，身上有她的基因，血管里淌着她的血，是她生命的延续
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ……
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    在母亲最后的那些日子里，她头脑一直都十分清醒。我们每天长谈，前后持续了三个月，话题涉及方方面面。她多次提到顾伯伯，说感到非常遗憾，没能在有生之年看到顾准的那些真知灼见为世人所了解。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    今天，母亲在天之灵一定感到十分欣慰。顾准伯伯的遗著《从理想主义到经验主义》历尽坎坷，终于在他故去
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    18
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年后的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1992
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年出版。这部著作是他与胞弟陈敏之于
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1973~1974
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年间在通信中的学术讨论笔记，是顾伯伯冒着生命危险，撰写的一系列掷地有声的醒世篇章，凝聚了他思想的精华。学者王元化在该书的序言中说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    这是近年来我所读到的一本最好的著作：作者才气横溢，见解深邃，知识渊博，令人为之折服。许多问题一经作者提出，你就再也无法摆脱掉
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ……
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    它们促使你思考，促使你去反省并检验由于习惯惰性一直扎根在你头脑深处的既定看法。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
