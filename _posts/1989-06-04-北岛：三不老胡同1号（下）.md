  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    要想标明三不老胡同
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    号在北京社会版图中的位置，就得从
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    大院
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    与
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    胡同
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    说起。这是两种截然不同的政治文化。一般说来，
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    大院
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    是居庙堂之高的外来户，
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    胡同
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    是处江湖之远的原住民；
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    大院
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    代表权力，
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    胡同
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    贯穿历史。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    当然问题没那么简单，真正的高官宁可在胡同深居简出。比如，我们大院的住户就多是中下层干部，而民主党派的大佬们则跟着执政党隐身胡同，相濡以沫，即使削官革职，照样好吃好喝，可谓
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    最后的贵族
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    大院
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    分三六九等，往往与国家机器的零部件有关。虽说民主党派在个别历史时期地位有所提升，但基本属于残次品，故三不老
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    号的人贵有自知之明。这种等级意识体现在发声学上，特别在
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    文革
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    期间，人家自报家门时中气十足：
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    中直的！
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”“
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    计委的！
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”“
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    海军大院的！
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    轮到我们，就像含着个大枣似的含混不清：
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    三不老的
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ——”
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    那时候市内楼少，三不老胡同
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    号在当地是标志性建筑，方圆三五里抬头可见。我在弘善寺小学读书，同学多来自底层。到同学家去玩，家长问及住处，同学抢先回答：
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    人家三不老大楼的。
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    家长多半翻白眼打量我－－对国家机器的零部件及残次品，平民百姓并无鉴别力。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    胡同构筑的迷宫、雨后的水坑、初夏槐花的香味和昏暗的街灯，让我这个在楼里长大的孩子心向往之。与楼房的刻板结构相比，那儿有一种平民的野性与自由。夏天，公用水龙头旁，半裸的男女插科打诨，孩子追逐嬉戏。沿墙角拐进小院，房屋歪斜，角落堆满碎砖破瓦。那有另一种生活：祖孙三代挤在一起，骂骂咧咧，可粗粝的外表下是深深的依恋；还有左邻右舍那份真心的关切
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ……
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    从胡同深处回望，我竟会对大楼产生隐隐的敌意。这无疑和青春期的反抗有关：大楼代表着父权和秩序。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    大院的孩子深入胡同是要冒风险的，弄不好会遭辱骂甚至暴打，除非你有几个真正的胡同朋友。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    关铁林是我小学同学，一度与我来往甚密。他家住在附近一条死胡同中的小院里，大楼遮蔽了其中的阳光。他母亲因病早逝；父亲是救火队员，三班倒，很少在家。我印象最深的是他家那个旧铜脸盆，坑坑疤疤，好像传家宝。下了课，他点火生炉子，把烧好的热水倒进铜盆，用手指试试水温，慢慢把双手浸泡进去，惬意地闭上眼。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    有一次我跟他吹牛，说我父亲的字写得多么棒。他吃惊地看着我。关于他父亲呢？他沉默了。至少在现实层次，写字与救火是不对等的
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    —
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    在大火中爬高等于玩命。他不能再失去父亲了。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    另一个胡同朋友的名字我忘了。他是我小学同班同学，家住后海河沿。他父亲是街头小贩，摆摊卖糖果针线兼营小型赌博业。那是个分格木盒，糊上窗户纸，交两分钱用手指捅进小格，输赢几率各占百分之五十，奖品是糖果玻璃球之类小玩意儿。我每次志在必得，道理也简单：他儿子把秘密事先透露给我。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    8
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    文革
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    爆发那年我十七岁。我就读的北京四中处于风暴的中心。那正是我数理化告急的关坎－－期末考试在即。学校突然宣布全面停课，我跟着欢呼雀跃，为了资产阶级教育路线的失败，也为了自己跨越数理化障碍的胜利。
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    文化大革命
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    于我，最初是一场狂欢节。每天醒来，我都感到不踏实，担心毛主席改主意，直到他老人家最终下定决心，永远关上学校大门。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    造反运动出现分化：出身好的同学成为主力军，我们被排除在外。赋闲在家，难免有些郁闷，我转而帮弟弟妹妹写大字报，批判老师引导的
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    白专道路
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，但远不够刺激，在这史无前例的大风大浪中，老师只不过是小鱼小虾而已。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我成了孩子王，跟楼里几个比我小的男孩子分析局势，我们找到一条大鱼－－
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    8
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    号楼的陈咸池。据说他曾在国民党特务机关干过，解放后被关了几年，属于典型的
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    历史反革命
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我领着五六个男孩儿冲到他家。敲开门，先宣读毛主席语录：
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    凡是反动的东西，你不打，他就不倒，这也和扫地一样，扫帚不到，灰尘照例不会自己跑掉。
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    没动一指头，陈咸池自己就倒下了，手举选民证，表示他也是人民的一员。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    不由分说，我们连推带搡，把他押到
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    4
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    号楼门前，让他坐在凳子上。我回家取来理发推子，在伙伴们的簇拥下，按下他的头。一触到那油腻腻的头发，我竟有点儿晕眩，迟疑片刻，终于定下神儿，沿着他脑门正中纵向在乱发中开出道深沟。那推子不怎么好使，反复好几次，沟底才露出青色头皮。这就是当时流行的
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阴阳头
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。我发现，不是推子不好使，而是我右手出了问题－－颤抖不已，我不得不放下推子，用左手攥住右手，装成没事儿人似的，继续指挥。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    陈咸池低着头，扯平褪色的中山装，掸掉头发茬儿，从最初的慌乱中平静下来，看清这不过是一帮毛孩子的恶作剧。他的轻视激怒了我们，当场召开了小型批斗会，只有几个过路人和小孩看热闹。陈咸池并没坐
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    喷汽式
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，低头弯腰，一问三不知，我们高呼口号：
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    打倒陈咸池！
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”“
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    敌人不投降，就叫他灭亡！
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我们先把他关进锅炉房，又怕他搞破坏，于是转移到
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    8
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    号楼地下室。我们轮流看守，三班倒，除了按时送饭，还得陪他上厕所，既怕他逃跑，又怕他自杀。两天过去了，我们累得人仰马翻，哈欠连天，看来除了释放别无他途。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我们把他从地下室带出来，他好像被关了很久，脸色苍白，眯着眼睛抬头看太阳。我先宣读毛主席语录：
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    政策和策略是党的生命，各级领导同志务必充分注意，万万不可粗心大意。
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    然后严厉警告，不许他乱说乱动，必须定期报到。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    以后半路遇上他，我就跟见了鬼似的，尽量绕道走。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    多年后，我读到英国作家戈尔丁的《蝇王》：那大胆设想，对我们来说却曾是无情的现实。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    9
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    狂欢节很快转成血腥的悲剧：我们楼最高行政长官葛志成的夫人华锦，八中党支部书记，被关在学校，因忍受不了拷打和侮辱，
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    8
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    月
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    22
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    日凌晨自缢。紧接着，一凡的家被北航红卫兵抄了，他父亲被遣送回四川原籍。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    三不老胡同
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    号几乎成了北京抄家的首选目标，整天鸡犬不宁。
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    3
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    号楼的赵君迈，这位辽沈战役被俘的国民党长春市市长，每天早上在院里舞剑，飘飘然，好像在练习升天。那天红卫兵抄家，他试图反抗，差点儿被当场打死。看来他做好了升天的准备。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    各楼门口贴出告示，宣称全体居民都是反革命，订于某日某时全部抄家，无一幸免；同时勒令事先交出
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    四旧
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，否则格杀勿论。于是我们先自行抄家，把涉嫌
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    四旧
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的书籍物品送到居委会，包括一副象牙麻将，多年后父亲提起来还心疼。大限到了，扬言抄家的红卫兵却无影无踪，虚惊一场。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    一个夏日晚上，轮到我们家在传达室值夜班。看大门的伍大爷被扫地出门
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    —
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    据说是逃亡富农，被遣返回乡。他黧黑瘦高、秃头、背微驼，身着白粗布褂黑缅裆裤，如同收进布袋里的弓。他一口河北口音，嗓门特大，后来几个看门的即使用扩音器，都无法相比。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    就在那天深夜，一个住
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    2
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    号楼的少女向我哭诉。天一亮，她和家人就要被押上火车，永远不准再回北京。在红卫兵的通令下，近十万北京居民被遣返回原籍。在昏暗的灯光下，她嘤嘤哭泣，晶莹的泪水沿面颊滚滚而下。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    血雨腥风的夏天过去了。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    文革
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    给民主党派带来实践民主的机会。民进中央连同司机勤杂总共二十来号人，按民主的游戏规则分成两拨。父亲忙着写大字报，打笔仗，乐此不疲。他刷标语时从梯子上掉下来，摔断了右手，住进积水潭医院，医生护士也忙着打派仗，手腕骨好歹接上了，却是歪的。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    在
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    把革命进行到底
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的主旋律中，是老百姓日常生活的变奏：收集纪念章、打鸡血、甩手疗法、养热带鱼
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ……
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    平安里丁字路口有个毛主席纪念章的集市，以物换物。我怀揣几枚纪念章，混在人群中，想换个碗口那么大的，但人家根本不屑一顾。父亲从派系斗争中急流勇退，开始攒半导体收音机。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    当时主要燃料是蜂窝煤。原来由煤铺工人蹬平板三轮，挨家挨户送货上门，赶上
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    文革
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，工人造反了，不再为资产阶级服务，一筐筐蜂窝煤就卸在楼门口，各家自己想办法。一筐蜂窝煤六七十斤，无壮劳力的人家傻了眼，那阵子招女婿，估摸先得过搬蜂窝煤这一关。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    趁
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    文革
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    之乱，一家废品收购站连同各种破烂，悄么悄地侵占了大院东头的篮球场，后来证明是极有远见的：六十年代末的全民大迁徙带来无限商机。我和一凡去废品收购站，拦截顾客，筛选要当废纸卖掉的旧书；甚至用介绍信蒙混过关，直接钻进废纸堆里淘宝。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    在全民大迁徙的同时，北京开始挖防空洞。大院又大兴土木。首先遭殃的是那些钻天杨。全部被砍倒运走，光秃秃一片。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    10
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    三不老胡同
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    号楼去人空，门可罗雀。废品收购站也随之生意萧条，一度洪水般泛滥的破烂，变戏法般缩进几个箩筐中。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1969
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年春，我被分配到北京第六建筑公司，去河北蔚县开山放炮。一年多后，工地转移到北京房山的东方红炼油厂，每两周大休回家一次。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我家成了聚会的中心。拉上厚重的粗布窗帘，三五好友，读书、写作、饮酒、听音乐，当然还有爱情。我们的行踪，早在大楼居委会的监视中。一天夜里，一凡在家冲洗照片，红灯和放大机的闪光被当成特别信号，
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    小脚侦缉队
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    立即报告西城公安局，警察破门而入，一无所获，最后没收了一摞黑胶木的古典音乐唱片。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我们把男高音康健请到我家。他头大如斗，脸色红润，像一轮夜里的太阳，照亮坐满小屋的客人。他笑起来会震得玻璃哗哗响。待他高歌《伏尔加船夫曲》，满堂失色，据说三五里外都能听见那警世洪钟：
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    踏开世界的不平路
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ……”
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    几年后，楼里的男孩女孩，插队的、兵团的、参军的、劳改的，各色人等都陆陆续续回来了，我和济年也一同从沙河五七干校回到了北京，唯独珊珊没能回来
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ……
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    （摘自母亲的口述记录）
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    沙龙不得不转移阵地，我们用自制的假月票到荒郊野外聚会。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    七十年代初，振开刚二十出头，已开始动笔写诗写小说。他常常请病在家，把厨房作为书房，关起门埋头写作。有时我半夜起来上厕所，厨房里淡黄色灯光还亮着
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ……
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    （摘自父亲的笔记）
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    通过父亲，我结识了
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    号楼的冯亦代伯伯，再通过他结识了更多的书和人。我常到他家小坐。冯伯伯笑眯眯地握着烟斗，思路和烟缕一起上升。穿围裙戴套袖的冯妈妈，奔忙于炉灶与字典之间。她几乎失明，开门时从厚厚眼镜片上迷茫地看着我，然后手持放大镜，帮冯伯伯锁定某个词的含义。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1976
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    10
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    月初的一天晚上，我带来
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    四人帮
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    垮台的好消息，当时冯伯伯正在厨房用毛巾擦拭后背。于是他和历史一起转身。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1978
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年年底，我和朋友创办了《今天》杂志。部分装订工作是在我家进行的，一摞摞油印纸页从床铺到地上，散发着浓烈的油墨味。门庭若市，我手忙脚乱招呼客人，估摸居委会派出所也跟着加班加点。
