<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

  </span>
</p>

<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); min-height: 19px;'>

</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); min-height: 19px;'>

<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); min-height: 19px;'>

</p>

  </span>

<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   “
  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   我妈跟个木头人似的坐在我对面，第一句话就是你干什么来了？
  </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   ”
  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   陈祖莫回忆说。格兰娜已经无法认出儿子，她疯了。
  </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   六年未见，母亲从肉体到精神与过去判若两人，这给了陈祖莫当头一棒。陈祖莫后来多次探监，母亲始终对他很冷淡，直到他带着刚出生的儿子过去，母亲精神上才有些许变化。
  </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   1975
  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   年格兰娜被发配到芜湖，陈祖莫为了照顾她，带着家人也去了芜湖。
  </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   父亲究竟是怎样的人？
  </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   文革动荡结束后，
  </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   1980
  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   年，在芜湖的陈祖莫鼓起勇气，给胡耀邦写信，请求调回北京。
  </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   陈祖莫对当年见到胡耀邦的场景记忆犹新。
  </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   “
  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   胡伯伯他马上要开会了，坐在台阶上，当时跟我说，陈昌浩的儿子啊，陈昌浩好同志嘛
  </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   ”
  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   。就这样，十三年前的
  </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   “
  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   革命罪人
  </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   ”
  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   ，如今忽又成了
  </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   “
  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   好同志
  </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   ”
  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   。
  </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   不久中共中央为陈昌浩平反，召开追悼会，五百余人前往吊唁。此时陈祖莫愈发觉得父亲是一个迷，采石场工人、小职员、小干部再到罪人，这些复杂多变的身份，似乎都不是完整的父亲。他的人生中究竟还埋藏着哪些秘密，他究竟是怎样的人？
  </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);'>
<img alt="72.jpg" border="0" height="412" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5029/72.jpg" width="550"/>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   中央为陈昌浩召开追悼会
  </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   自此，陈祖莫开始了对父亲过往的追寻。他在所有人的这些记忆里寻找父亲，在所有父亲的旧日行迹里寻找自己不曾听闻触摸的事实。随着陈祖莫的追寻，陈昌浩猝然离去的生命中，这些消散在历史烟尘里的形象，又一点点的拼合起来。
  </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   2000
  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   年之后，有关西路军的探讨公开化，外界开始客观看待这段历史。事实上，
  </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   1983
  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   年初，李先念根据邓小平的指示和陈云的建议，写成《关于西路军历史上几个问题的说明》，西路军执行的任务是中央决定的，西路军自始至终都在中革军委领导之下，重要军事行动也是中国军委指示，或经中革军委同意的，不能说是执行张国焘路线。但李先念的《说明》，和中央常委的批示，当时只限存档，故不为公众知晓，直到后来《陈云年谱》公开出版，这段对西路军问题的重要结论才得以浮出水面。
  </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   “
  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   我现在才真正地体会，他真正为了党的利益可以牺牲一切，他牺牲了自己。
  </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   ”
  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   陈祖莫说，
  </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   “
  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   讲句更深的，他牺牲了儿子，牺牲了家庭。为了这个事业，他最后就选择了默默无闻，有好多话没说出来就离开了人世。
  </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   ”
  </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   在陈昌浩离世数十年之后，晚年的陈祖莫接纳了身为
  </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   “
  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   陈昌浩儿子
  </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   ”
  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   的命运，
  </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   “
  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   我也挺替我爸爸自豪，我以前就没有这种感觉。
  </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   ”
  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   他从心底原谅了父亲，
  </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   “
  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   如果今天我的话能传到阴间去，我父亲会对我有所责怪的话，我估计我就能放下了。
  </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   ”
  </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); min-height: 19px;'>

<p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);'>

<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   西路军烈士公墓墓碑
  </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   2008
  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   年深冬，塞外朔风呼号。已经移居澳大利亚的陈祖莫带着家人第一次来到甘肃张掖高台县的西路军烈士纪念馆，替父亲祭奠西路军阵亡将士。这片土地与他父亲一生荣辱，与他自己一生的颠沛，都有着无法割裂的联系。一到这里，陈祖莫就做了一个奇怪的梦。
  </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   我梦见我爸了。
  </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   ”
  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   陈祖莫的表情兴奋而微妙。在梦里，他觉得父亲似乎有很多话想说，但又只是看着他并没有说什么，
  </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   “
  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   因为我们之间确实有好多话没说完，也没机会说
  </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
   ”
  </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   。
  </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
</span>

<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); min-height: 19px;'>

<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); min-height: 19px;'>
