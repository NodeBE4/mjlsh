  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">

  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   原载于《百年潮》
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    2018
   </span>
   年第
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    3
   </span>
   期，原题《亲历十三陵水库建设工作》。此处略有删节。
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   新中国成立前，北京市区的地表水源仅有西郊玉泉山一处，平均年出水量只有
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    3000
   </span>
   万立方米，
   <span class="s3" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
    远远不能满足城市和农业用水。新中国成立后，决定在流经北京地区的永定河、潮白河等几条主要河道上修建水库。北京周边相继修建了四个大水库，除了官厅水库于
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1954
   </span>
   年
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    5
   </span>
   月竣工外，十三陵水库、怀柔水库和密云水库均同时建成于
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   大跃进
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   时期。四大水库的建成，基本解决了北京地区受困多年的缺水之苦。我作为国务院机关事务管理局的一名普通干部，参加了十三陵水库的建设工作。
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   为中央国家机关参加劳动做保障和联络工作
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   十三陵水库于
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1958
   </span>
   年
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1
   </span>
   月
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    21
   </span>
   日开始建设，正式动工前一天，即
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1
   </span>
   月
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    20
   </span>
   日，北京市政府办公厅向国务院反映，修建十三陵水库每天尚缺劳力约
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1
   </span>
   万人，希望中央国家机关能以参加义务劳动的方式给予支援。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    21
   </span>
   日，国务院副秘书长齐燕铭指示由国管局负责牵头承办此事。
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    接到任务后，国管局随即组织了一个临时机构
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ——
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    国家机关义务劳动办公室（以下简称
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    劳动办
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   ）。交际处副处长任民担任劳动办负责人，我为联络员，另一位是司机赵根繁，局里还为我们配备了一辆吉普车。
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   劳动办成立后，我们立刻开始前期准备工作。白天跑水库工地了解情况，领受任务，查看劳动场地，安排住房；晚上开会研究方案，排列各单位出工时间顺序。还要与财政部协商出工补贴问题，与粮食部门研究制定粮食供给问题，要考
   <span class="s3" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
    虑电话通讯、劳动工具、照明设备、医疗救护、广播网站、宣传报道等问题。由于时间紧、任务重，我每天都要工作到深夜，睡办公室是常事。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   此时，水库工程已经动工，参加建设的各路大军纷纷开赴工地。有北京各县组织的民工、市县党政机关干部、市商业企业系统的干部职工、各大院校的青年学生和驻京部队，等等。为了尽快保证中央国家机关各部委的劳动队伍进驻工地，齐燕铭副秘书长在中南海召开了各部委办主要负责人会议，亲自动员部署，并按照我们提供的报表下达任务、提出要求。会后，各单位立即行动起来。那几天，我每天守在电话机旁与各单位联系，了解情况，解答问题，协商出勤人数，安排出发时间，整整打了三天电话。
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    2
   </span>
   月
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    3
   </span>
   日，我们劳动办的三位同志带上简单的行李，坐上吉普车，正式进驻工地办公。当天率先开进工地的是水利部的
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    300
   </span>
   余名干部职工，他们自行解决食宿，由昌平县水利局给予安排。下午，任民带我去水利部驻地看望大家，一方面表示慰问，一方面征求意见，搞调查研究，我把了解到的情况一一做了记录。
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   晚上，任民召集大家开会，我在会上提出了一个方案：由于水库周边已被数万名民工的工棚、部队的帐篷围满，机关干部的劳动队伍只能驻扎在昌平县城。从前期情况看，如果由我们统一给大家安排食宿，困难会很大。根据水利部的经验，可要求后续各部委自行到昌平联系对口单位解决食宿，以减轻劳动办的工作压力。仍由劳动办负责安排的住所，规定每人占地长
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    2
   </span>
   米、宽为
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    0.9
   </span>
   米，有条件的可自备上下铺，没有床的一律睡地上。任民同意了我的方案。
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    会上，我们还研究确定了以下几点：（
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1
   </span>
   ）各单位自行解决炊具、餐具、炊事员，自己就地
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    埋锅造饭
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    。（
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    2
   </span>
   ）各单位组织人员参加义务劳动的时间，原则规定为两星期一轮换，每批出勤人数需提前报劳动办备案，由我负责统计审核。伙食标准定为每人每天
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    0.5
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
    元，粮食用量定期上报后由我负责核准报审。（
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    3
   </span>
   ）由工程总指挥部按照各单位参加劳动的人数，提供简单的劳动工具，如铁锹、洋镐、扁担、土筐等，其他技术工种所需特殊工具及其技术指导，由任民负责找十三陵水库工程总指挥部的工程部协商解决。当时的工程总指挥是罗文坊少将，政委由北京市副市长赵凡担任。
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   当时我们劳动办借用的是昌平县委机关大院的两间平房，只有一部电话。任民要我第二天开始向后续各单位打电话提要求，尽快落实劳动队伍入驻昌平的有关事宜。那天，我们又工作到深夜。
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   随后几天里，各部委按照规定时间陆续组织劳动队伍进入工地。我们将中央国家机关劳动队伍按系统分别组建了
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    13
   </span>
   个大队、
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    75
   </span>
   个中队、
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    329
   </span>
   个小队，一些局、处级领导担任了大队长或中队长，他们常驻工地，直到工程彻底完工才撤离。
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   从
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    2
   </span>
   月
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    3
   </span>
   日到
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    6
   </span>
   月
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    30
   </span>
   日，近五个月的时间里，由

   </span>
   的旗子时，红旗已经传到了周总理手中。看见这面红旗的旗杆，我心里一沉，手握工具愣在那里，心想：
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   小赵同志从哪儿找来这么根又粗又高的大旗杆呀？快有六米长了！
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   事过多年，再提起旗杆的事时，赵根繁还一脸懊悔地说：
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   当时谁想到周总理会亲自打旗呀？就这么根杆子，还是我跑了好几个地方，最后在一家山货铺的后院找到的。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    6
   </span>
   月的骄阳，晒得脚下的沙砾发烫。周总理神采奕奕地高举着这面红旗，带领队伍大步走上了沸腾的工地。到了工地上，闻讯赶来的总指挥罗文坊向总理敬礼，刚说了句
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   欢迎各位首长的到来
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s3" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
    ，周总理立即纠正说：
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   这里没有首长，大家都是普通劳动者，就请总指挥下达任务吧！
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   当天下午
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    6
   </span>
   点，周总理和第四支队的同志们一起在工地上吃晚饭。大家同吃一样的干粮、咸菜，同喝一桶菜汤，吃完继续参加劳动。工地上到处闪烁着灯火，有电灯、汽灯、马灯，部队甚至还打起了探照灯。周总理和第四支队的同志们一直劳动到深夜
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    11
   </span>
   点，才披星戴月地赶回昌平驻地。当晚，周总理就下榻在工程兵技术学校那间布置得十分简单的房间里。
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   十三陵水库胜利竣工
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1958
   </span>
   年
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    6
   </span>
   月
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    30
   </span>
   日，十三陵水库竣工。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    7
   </span>
   月
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1
   </span>
   日，召开了竣工庆祝大会。
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   工程收尾阶段，总指挥部布置了各单位的评功授奖活动。中央国家机关所属的
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    13
   </span>
   个大队中有
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    6
   </span>
   个荣获了
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   先进大队
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   荣誉称号，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    24
   </span>
   个中队和
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    329
   </span>
   个小队获得了
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   先进集体
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   的奖状。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    8
   </span>
   月
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    4
   </span>
   日，国管局召开大会，向参加十三陵水库义务劳动的各机关单位颁发了由北京市委、市政府和十三陵水库建设总指挥部授予的锦旗。我和赵根繁被评为
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   工地组织
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   劳动模范，每人还获得了一枚精致的劳动奖章。
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
   岁月如逝，斗转星移。告别那个火红的年代，已有
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    60
   </span>
   年了。今天，十三陵水库发生了巨大变化。原本光秃的蟒山、漢包山、豆角山等已经满是苍松翠柏，到处郁郁葱葱，被开辟为国家级森林公园。水库四周垂柳如帘，繁花似锦；坝内碧波粼粼，鱼翔浅底；坝外果树成行，春华秋实。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   昌平八景
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   之一的
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   天峰拔翠
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   遗址，已成为今天的九龙宫公园。矗立在公园内的一块纪念碑上，镌刻着周总理在水库劳动时的挥笔题词：鼓足干劲，力争上游，多快好省地建设社会主义。四周被鲜花簇拥，每天引来无数的中外游客驻足凝视。
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
