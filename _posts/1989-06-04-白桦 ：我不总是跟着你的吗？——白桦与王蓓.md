<p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">

</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1938
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，日本侵略军的铁蹄正在从华北向中原袭来，八岁的我跟着父母客居在武汉。音乐家冼星海正在江上发动救亡歌咏运动，大江两岸人山人海，齐声高唱同一首歌《中国不会亡》。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ——
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我相信！
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    秋天，故乡沦陷，父亲被日本侵略军活埋，这就宿命地决定了我后来的去路。悲情人生，激情澎湃，舍死忘生，苦苦追寻，认定
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    革命
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    是唯一高尚的追求，
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    革命理想
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    成为我的全部，成为我的未来。于是，千方百计，义无反顾，舍死忘生，投奔沙场。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1948
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年初冬的一天，在进军淮海平原的路上，络绎不绝的小车和我军大队人马并行。我问一位推车的农民大嫂：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    你们小车上推的是什么？
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”“
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    白面。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”“
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    你们家还有存粮吗？
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”“
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    有，不在窖里。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”“
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    在哪儿？
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”“
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    在地里。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”“
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    地里？什么庄稼？
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”“
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    麦子。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我环顾白雪覆盖的中原大地，麦苗还没出芽呢！我情不自禁地哭了。我想起一个战国时代越王勾践讨伐吴国的故事：在十年生聚，十年教训以后，越国复仇之师在河边誓师出征，越国一位老者把存了十年的一坛美酒献给国王勾践，勾践没有独自享用，当即倾入河中，下令全军迎流而饮。无论古今，这样的军队必胜。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ——
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我相信！
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    五十年代初，士兵们的枪膛渐渐冷却下来，战争的硝烟在绝大部分领土上消失，全中国各族人民正在欢欣鼓舞地走向
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    理想的天堂
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ——
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我相信！
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    对于我来说，
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1957
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年春天那场猛烈的反
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    R
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    派运动，如同晴天霹雳一般。结果是数以万计的知识分子被划为
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    R
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    派。因言获罪，因忠言获罪。在客观上，摧毁了大多数中国人心中的诚信。做梦也没想到，我这个
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    自己人
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    也被戴上了一顶
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    资产阶级
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    R
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    派分子
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    桂冠。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    在那个年月，
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    R
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    派桂冠可不是化装舞会的戏谑。一个理想主义者被
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    理想
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    抛弃。同时，被社会抛弃，被人群抛弃，思想库存里立刻一贫如洗，安身立命之本顿时塌陷。此情何堪？仅仅是带给亲人们的屈辱就能把人压死。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    由于难以忍受这种巨大的奇耻大辱，愤而自尽者、夫妻离异者大有人在。即便是往日生死之交的战友狭路相逢，也唯恐避之不及。就像傣族历史上的一种陋习，某人如果一旦被巫师或众人指认为
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    琵琶鬼
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s3" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ，他随即就变成了一头活在人群中的猪狗，直至死。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我们家是一个新婚燕尔的二人世界，妻子王蓓是年轻的电影演员，她会怎样看待我们所面临的奇耻大辱呢？她曾经是那样自珍自爱。从今以后她还能挽着
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    反革命
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    丈夫的臂膀上街吗？
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    等我回到家，她的第一道目光，仍然是往日的温情。当有些人为了羞辱我，组织一些小学生在我的窗前齐唱
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    社会主义好，社会主义好，
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    R
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    派分子夹着尾巴逃跑了
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的时候，她竟敢冒险走出去劝说小朋友们离开。更有甚者，她还多次到我劳动改造的场所陪我去加班干活。

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    如今，一双耄耋老人，相依为命，总应该平静下来了吧？不！五年前，妻子又罹患阿茨海默症，暮年的天空，立即一片阴霾。我原以为罹患阿茨海默症，就意味着在患者的意识里失去了过去，也失去了现在和未来。不会有欢乐，当然也就不会有痛苦。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    不久，我发现，并非那样简单。她似乎又回到了童年，重新开始认识这个
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    陌生
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的世界。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    什么是洗手间？
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”“
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    什么是橙汁？
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”“
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    什么是遥控器？
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    电视屏幕里在下雨，她会喊：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    收衣服！下雨了！下完雨，才会下太阳呢！
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    偶尔，她也会灵光一闪，小声责备自己：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    是我的错，一定是我的错。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    许多朋友都知道，她是一个少见的淡泊名利的演员。以往的影戏剧照早已散失殆尽。近来她竟会反常地把一些从书报上剪下来的图片，摆进玻璃书橱里，虽然有时候甚至她连图片中的自己都不认识。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    现在她对所有年轻人都叫大哥大姐，有时会把我误认为是她早年仙逝的父亲，准确地说，是父亲和丈夫的重合，管我叫
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    老爸
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s3" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ，或者
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    老爷子
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    据我所知，她自幼缺少父爱，父亲是个无暇顾家的人，生前总是在他那小小的钱庄里忙忙碌碌。自她十七岁从父亲手里接过一只小皮箱，随着电影导演孙瑜离家去上海以后，就再也没见到父亲了，在父亲辞世的时候，战乱阻隔了回乡之路，未能和父亲诀别。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    可以说，现在她已经生活在另一个陌生的空间了，几乎一无所有，但她依然保留着一个贫困年代的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    习惯
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s3" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ，但我不知道应不应该把它称为
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    习惯
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。那就是牙膏皮都可以换钱的年代，从地上拾起来的每一张破纸片，她都要抹平珍藏起来，她的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    保险柜
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    就是自己的床。要是不帮她清理，她的床很快就成了垃圾堆。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我发现，如果说她已经完全失智，是不对的。她有一根弦始终没有被割断，那就是我和她之间那根弦。我是她唯一认识的人，顿顿服药、吃饭、喝水，都要让我首肯。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    老爸！这能吃吗？这能喝吗？
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    每当我要外出的时候，问她：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    你在家休息吧？
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    她总是像孩子那样回答我：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我不总是跟着你的吗？
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我只好带着她，即使是开会，她静静地坐在一旁，微笑颔首，一言不发，谁都不会把她当做病人。当我一定要独自外出的时候，她会大喊：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    你想想，我能单独留下吗！
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    她隐隐约约地能意识到自己独处时没有安全感。是的，万一我不得不从她身边离去，那将不是我一个人的灾难。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ——
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我相信！
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    最近，我的八岁的小孙女聪慧，无意中听到爷爷在碟片里朗诵诗歌的声音。她立刻就安静下来了，坐在一张小板凳上，低着头，一动也不动了。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang TC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    等到她抬起头来的时候，我才发现她的脸上全都是泪水。她还那么小，就懂得爷爷了。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ——
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我相信！
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
