<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    流沙河
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    婴年趣事，多已遗忘，还记得母亲抱我跨坐在她的两腿上，握着我的双腕，一推一拉，一拉一推，随着推拉的节拍，曼声吟唱：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    扯一锯，还一锯，吃口奶奶又来锯。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    逗我欢笑。稍长，看见别家做母亲的也这样逗小孩，不过唱词更有趣些，如下：
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    扯一锯，还一锯，
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    家婆门口有本戏。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    请外孙，来看戏。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    看饿了，吃啥子？
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    牛肉包子夹狗屁！
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    光阴如白驹之过隙，一晃，人到中年，想不到我真的拉锯了，一拉就是六年。这活路够燥辣，全不似
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    吃口奶奶又来锯
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    那般有趣。如今事隔十多年了，友人见面，总要问我：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    看你这样瘦，也能拉锯吗？
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    言外之意，颇有怀疑。我笑一笑，只有一句很坦白的解释：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    人要吃饭，没有什么不能做的。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    一九六六年二月三日是我拉锯的第一天。联手罗绍和师傅，矮矮胖胖（我是高高瘦瘦），家在农村，比我大一岁，文盲，拙于言辞。他引我入门，只有两句话：一句是
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    双手端平，亮出腋窝
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，一句是
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    两腿跨开，前如弯弓后如箭
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。说起来倒简单，做起来就难了。我的四肢僵硬，姿态可笑，憨使蛮劲，弄得联手穷于应付。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    罗师傅，对不起，痛脚连累好脚了。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我频频地向他致歉。他只嘿嘿一笑，倒不生气。作坊里的木工师傅常常放下活路，走来旁观，或提醒我
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    亮出腋窝
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”“
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    后腿打伸
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，或替我拉几锯，做个示范动作教我，没有一个当面嘲笑我的。不过也有这样的情况，某个木工师傅突然
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ×
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    妈捣娘髙声叫骂，抱着一块木板跑来，冲着罗师傅吼道：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我说你狗
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ×
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的只有饿饭！你来看看，解些什么板子，坑坑包包，推刨子推死老子了！
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    原来木工也和解匠一样，都是做计件工资的，我们解的板子不平，害得他们多做背工活路，影响收入，所以他们要骂。我明白这是罗师傅替我挨骂了，赶快赔笑自责，声明是我手艺太瘟。罗师傅胆小怕事，挨了骂急得脸红，至多在喉咙里嘟哝一句
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    你自己也有妈
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，算是对
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “×
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    你妈
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    作了回答。两三个月以后，我渐渐上了路，这样的情况就很少发生过了。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    解匠活路极重。国家给的粮食定量四十五斤，月月被我吃光。有多少次，日暮收工，大锯一放，全身都瘫软了，不由自主地落坐在背后压马杆的大木料上，仿佛再也站不起来。必待一支烟抽完，才恢复了一丝气力，站起来披衣服，穿裤子，趿鞋子，然后拖着两腿走回家去。夜间入睡以后，梦中还在哎哟连天的呻唤着，而自己却不知道。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    计件工资制逼得人不敢偷懒，每一分钟都得计算着使用。我若偷懒，月底挣不够买米钱，就得饿饭。我忘记了星期日这个概念，一年做到头。腊月三十的黄昏还在做，正月初二的早晨又铮铮嚓嚓挣挣扎扎拉起大锯来了。我一生中从来没有这样勤奋过。天亮起床，烧火煮饭，胡乱吃了，出门疾走如风，赶去上班。从我家走到北街木器家具社，只需八分钟，路上决不耽误时间。走入木器家具社的店门，便忙着解纽扣，脱衣服。走到架木料的马杆旁，裤子已经脱了，只剩一条幺裤遮丑，裸体，赤脚，二话不说，便同联手拼命地拉起来。为了不浪费时间，上厕所小便总是和联手一道，跑去跑回。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    本社有解匠四人，自愿结合成两组：我和罗师傅一组，小邹和陈师傅一组。掌墨师姓黄，熟读四书五经，写得一手好字，教过私塾，开过棺材店。解匠们当面叫他黄老师，背后叫他耳聋。每天早晨，黄老师翻出一堆木料，一一弹好墨线，给解匠们作几句必要的指示，便袖手到店上闲坐去了。那一堆弹了墨的木料，有好解的，有不好解的，也就是说，有肥有瘦。谁吃肥的，谁吃瘦的，两组解匠之间，不免勾心斗角，常常争吵。我和罗师傅这一组，有了我这痛脚连累好脚，能力当然弱些，进度当然慢些，所以常常是失败者。看见块肥肉（又软又湿的大木料）已经弹好墨线放在那里，我和罗师傅垂涎欲滴，恨不得两锯三锯锯完架在马杆上的木料，好去抬那一块肥肉。同样地，小邹和陈师傅那一组也在拼命追赶，铮铮嚓嚓之声愈来愈急促。结果总是他们那一组领先锯完架在自己马杆上的木料，两人笑盈盈地将那一块肥肉抬起走了。这时候罗师傅伤心之至，黑起脸，嘟起嘴，气得狠敲抓钉。我呢，这时候就得一边拉锯一边讲两件奇闻怪事，给罗师傅疏导疏导，使他快乐。他像小孩一样，喜听凶宅闹鬼、猛兽吃人、猪生三足、牛长独角之类的怪事。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    常解的木料被分为正料和杂料。正料只有松、杉、柏、桐四种，般说来好解，解出一丈板面（以一尺宽计算面积），两人共得工值三角五分。正料以外，都算杂料，包括白桦、赤桦、青柄、洋槐、皂荚、菩提、麻柳、夜合、楠、樟、枫、榆、桉、柳等等，有的太硬，有的太绵，一般说来难解，解出一丈板面，两人共得工值六角。我在那六年内解的几乎都是正料。从早到晚，抓紧时间苦干，可解八丈板面，两人各得工值一元四角。解匠生活很苦，大多面黄肌瘦，穿着破旧，比木工低一等。苦中寻乐，最好的途径是幻想。解匠们都幻想世界上有一种又软又脆又疏松的木料如芭蕉树一样，堆积成山，供他们天天解。可是这个幻想又被解匠们自已否定了。有他们自嘲的谣词一首为证：
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    青枫硬。麻柳绵。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    芭蕉好解不给钱！
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    记得有一天下午，炎阳斜照，懒蝉长嘶，两把大锯铮铮嚓嚓之声催人昏昏欲睡。忽然听见小邹和陈师傅一边拉锯一边发起牢骚来。牢骚的主题是诉解匠的钱太少，骂人间的不公平。好在岳社长不在这里，掌墨的黄老师也歇凉去了，木工师傅们又都躲在各自的工房内忙活路，没有外人听见，不会被谁密告上去，夜间开会挨刮。罗师傅也有牢骚要发，便扭过头去伸嘴搭白。他供养着家中四个农村人口，自己顿顿吃泡菜，比我苦多了。我当然也有牢骚，但不敢发。要知道，公安局派出所布置的专门监督我的言行的某个小组共有二十个成员，遍布本镇，小邹就是其中之一。我若
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    乱说乱动
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，他会去告我的。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    铮嚓声中，牢骚发完，三个解匠转入发表幻想演说，你一句，我一句，好不热闹。我也伙到他们瞎说凑趣。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    小邹是文革前的中学毕业生，稍具见识，头脑灵醒。他的幻想富有情节性。说某日一辆小汽车开到本镇来，停在木器家具社的店门外。车门开了，走出来一位老干部。他是中华人民共和国国务院劳动部部长，后面跟着我省省委书记。他们是到民间来私察暗访的，就像包青天大老爷一样。他们步入我店，说家具做得好。听见内院铮铮嚓嚓之声，他们就走进来，看我们拉大锯。部长说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    这活路太苦了。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    问我们一个月挣多少钱。我们据实回答。部长吃惊，回头对省委书记说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    工资太低了吧，唔？
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    省委书记当即把岳社长叫来，一顿痛刮。部长回到北京以后，发了一个中央文件，专说提高解匠工资，加一个番。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    罗师傅不懂得什么叫
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    加一个番
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，小声问我。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    解一丈，拿两丈的钱。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我说
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    罗师傅憨笑了，仿佛文件已经下来。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    接着是陈师傅发表幻想演说。他说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    有一把神锯就好了。根本不用气力拉来拉去，两人只须抬平锯子，这头走到那头，一块板子就解下来了。那头走到这头，又一块板子解下来了。走过去，走过来，只消走几分钟，一天的饭钱就挣够了。什么中央文件，多事！
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    轮到我说。我说，我们锯的木屑，再经我们赤脚踩过，沾了我们的汗，便成了治癌的特效药。全国各大医院都请我们去解木料。我们只收药钱。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    轮到罗师傅说。他的幻想非常简单：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我唯愿天天都解腐朽了的木料，好比锯豆腐。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    做什么？
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我问。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    做棺材。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    他说。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    这倒是实话，本店出售的棺材都是用朽木拼凑的，专骗死人。大家一想，都哈哈大笑了。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    铮了又嚓嚓，嚓了又铮铮，这是能够被听见的寂寞。一锯一锯，锯锯锯的是光阴。有时疑心自己是在慢性自杀。为了排遣寂寞，我便搜索枯肠，找些有趣的话同联手说。联手显然也有这种要求。于是在我和罗师傅之间，互相交代历史，彼此流通见闻，无所不谈。当然，一不谈国事，二不谈文化。这两门他一窍不通，而我也不敢谈。我们的谈话总是先说夜梦，后说晨餐，再说今天走在街上又看见了些什么－－这方面的内容特别丰富，某个当官的戴了高帽子游街示众啦，本镇名人李疯子又在街上抓锅魁吃啦，某人喝醉了在街上同某人打架啦，某男和某女乱睡被捉住啦，某人骑自行车被汽车撞死了啦，谁家夫妻双双到镇革委打离婚案啦，造反派某司令轰闹派出所啦，东街某饭馆卖甜烧白啦，一位农二嫂卖猪的钱被扒窃了气得去跳水啦，等等等等，都在我们口头发表，一一加以评论。所见所闻流通之后，我们又说彼此的家务事。我家的巴白鸡生了个双黄蛋啦，他家的幺女子吃多了屙痢啦，等等等等，都能触发我们的联想，引绎出一大堆废话来，够我们咀嚼半天。罗师傅最感兴趣的是听我谈大城市的花花世界，吃的什么，玩的什么。年轻时候他到成都去过一次，尽管是住在金华街一带破烂的鸡茅店里，每日在北门大桥河滩地上拉锯。如今在穷困中偶然回首，在他，那已是最美丽的记忆了。罗师傅听我谈，听得又香又甜，拉得愈来愈慢。必待我猛还他几锯，他才清醒，想起
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    吃口奶奶
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    还得
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    又来锯
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    可怜的解匠，悲欢全不由己。碰着料好解，其乐也融融，眉开眼笑，一边拉一边找话说。碰着料难解，火冒八丈高，肚子里阴骂掌墨师的祖宗，用一个最难听的动词。阴骂继之以阳骂，骂木料，骂马杆，骂抓钉，骂撑钉，骂天气太热，都用那个动词，动词后面还要加上
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    死
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    字。联手之间因料难解彼此都不耐烦，你责怪我，我责怪你，一句话不对头便吵起来。在这方面，我的涵养并不比罗师傅好，也常用那个动词，不觉得有什么不好。有一次解硬料，罗师傅生闷气，一只牛虻飞来叮他的腿。本来一拍便可打死，但他不。他放下锯子，一扫手活捉了那只牛虻，细心撕掉双翅，然后放它到锯屑中去乱爬，骂道：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    让你狗
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    随即嘿嘿一笑，享受了报复的快乐。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    解匠最恨深藏在木料中的石质的或铁质的异物，哪怕丁丁一点，也会打坏一排锯齿。重新锉好锯齿，至少也得损失半小时的活路，少拿钱啊！如果接二连三打坏锯齿，那就惨了，这一天的买米钱都挣不回！解料六年，打坏过多少次锯齿，已经没法说清。我用手锤敲抓钉，从木料的锯缝内挖出过深藏的卵石、碎石、小砾石、角钉、大钉、寸钉、毛毛钉、断钉、铁丝、鸟枪霰弹、箭镞、砖块、瓦块煤块
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ……
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    有一次解一株大皂荚树，三次打坏锯齿，从树心内挖出三条像匕首一样的耙齿，每条长六寸。原来这株为人间洗濯污浊作过贡献的皂荚树，在它年轻的时候，曾经被愚昧的乡民指控为
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    树妖
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”“
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    木魅
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，说它作祟害人。谁家失火了，谁家死人了，谁家闹鬼了，都说是它在作祟，从而移罪于它。乡民讲阴阳五行，金是能克木的，所以那些失火死人闹鬼之家先后三次用铁锤敲钢质的耙齿，楔人树干，以便克它。几十年后，树干已经长得很粗，三条耙齿就被包藏在树心内了。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    树也有冤案呢。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我想。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    有一次解一株夜合树，实在有趣。唐诗有句云
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    夜合花开香满庭

<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    钱塘江上是儿家，郎若闲时来吃茶。黄土筑墙茅盖屋，门前一树马缨花。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    马缨花在这里暗示夜合，出自女性口吻，显系性的挑逗。此诗已经涉嫌精神污染了，是吧？
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我和罗师傅解的这一株夜合树很大，树干直径二尺，当然是百年古木。它生长在清朝的金堂县衙门里。后来这个县衙门又做了国民党的金堂县政府。解放后这个县政府又做了金堂县人民政府。如今这里是本镇的幼儿园。这株夜合树该是三朝元老了，它的年轮里记录着多少兴亡啊！
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    可是这位三朝元老传递给我的嗅觉信息却很不妙。原来夜合树的锯屑太难闻了，散发着一股股辛辣冲鼻的怪味，害得我一边拉锯一边打喷嚏，涕泗涟涟，什么思古之幽情都给冲散了。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    一张一张的寸板解出来了，锯片逐渐逼近树心。忽然眼前一闪，锯缝中飞出黄亮亮的金属锯屑来。与此同时，听见咕噜噜一声－－异物打坏锯齿的声音。赶快停锯，细看锯齿，果然锋棱有所损坏，但不厉害。这说明深藏在树心的异物是一种黄亮亮的软质金属，绝不可能是铁。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    罗师傅用食指蘸着锯片上的金属锯屑，看了许久，感到疑惑。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    这是啥呀？
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    他问。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    黄金。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我说。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    幻想成癖的我一瞬间看见了百年以前的一个明月之夜，地点就在县衙门的后院内这一株夜合树下。有一个人偷窃了衙门库存的黄金，投入夜合树的树洞。后来他死了，黄金未取出。这个秘密从此无人知晓。百年以后这块黄金碰着了我们的锯齿，遂有黄亮亮的金屑从锯缝中飞出来。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我的判断结论激动了罗师傅。我们顾不上锉锯齿，赶快用手锤敲抓钉挖树心。那里果然有一个洞，洞内沉积着腐黑的木渣。罗师傅从木渣中抠出一颗步枪子弹头来，弹头表层有黄亮亮的锯痕。真相大白，黄金梦醒，我们都觉得有些不好意思，脸颊微烧。锉锯齿的时候，我们愈想愈气，便痛骂国民党的丘八乱打枪。悲欢全不由己，可怜的解匠！
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我们也到乡下去给农家解料。时值文革两派内战，乡村路口常有派性武装人员放哨，盘查形迹可疑的人。哨兵若问：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    你是啥观点的？
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    你得先弄清楚这些背枪的家伙是哪一派的，再作回答。他们是跟着
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    跑的，你就回答
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    八二六观点
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ；他们是本镇
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    尖刀团
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的应声虫，你就回答
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    产业军观点
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。你若回答错了，轻则挨一顿臭骂，重则挨耳光，还要跪下向毛主席请罪。罗师傅胆小怕事，又不关心所谓国家大事，所以回答不出来，由我相机替他回答。轮到问我，我就作诚恳状，低声回答：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    再由罗师傅补充一句：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    他还在改造。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    在乡下给农家解料，主人都对我们很好，尊称我为
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    余师
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，拿烟倒茶，打酒割肉，盛情款待。黄昏时候，罗师傅总是大醉而归，一路偏偏倒倒。有一回过小桥，他醉了踩虚脚，跌落在水沟里，爬起来还在傻笑。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我们也常常在木器家具社的店门外临街解料。这是因为木料太大太重，不好抬进店内后院去解，所以就在街旁架马。临街拉锯，裸体，只穿一条幺裤，展览给满街的行人看，实在有点那个。可恼的是一群小孩，他们站得远远的，合着我们拉锯的来回节奏，齐声吼唱：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    解，匠，解。解，匠，解。解，匠的，东，西，两，边，甩。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    气得我们骂也不是，笑也不是。
   </span>
</p>
