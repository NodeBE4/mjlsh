<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    刘海鸥，笔名凌之，女，
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1988
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年赴澳大利亚马克立大学读博士研究生，后因病辍学。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1990
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年代初开始写作，主要作品有长篇纪实文学《半壁家园》《海鸥南飞》《刘海鸥作品集》三集、《夕出阳关》、姐妹合集《我们的田野》；曾在中国大陆、香港、台湾多家主要报刊杂志发表作品，另外多部作品收入国内外小说及散文选集。《她不属于这个世界》获
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    2003
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    世界华文文学奖
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。《半壁家园》《游必有信》（作品集之一）获得
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    2013
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    及
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    2017
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年海外华文著述奖散文佳作奖。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
<img alt="118.jpg" border="0" height="417" src="http://mjlsh.usc.cuhk.edu.hk/medias/contents/5689/118.jpg" width="320"/>
</span>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    这是阿巧留在世上唯一的一张照片。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    六十多年前她来到我家，曾有二十多年，她出出进进于我们的家庭，另外的四十多年她穿梭往返于我们家人的记忆和梦境中。她的地位并不重要，但是她的影子总是伴随着一家人对往昔的回忆。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    她叫何宝珠，我们家的保姆，大家叫她阿巧。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    她是南方人，不同于胡同里多见的脸宽眼细头发披散体态肥硕的北方妇人，她的头发油黑发亮，在脑后挽起一个发髻。眼睛不小，只是缺少灵气。人中挺长，包着微微突出的上牙，总像在抿着嘴笑。她身材瘦小，上穿一件月白色大襟褂，平平板板贴着身子，从腰际开始下摆逐渐增大，长过臀部。下穿一条黑色绸裤，裤腿肥大，在脚踝以上。脚登一双踩歪了鞋跟的五眼黑皮鞋。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    这是见第一面阿巧给我留下的印象，那是
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1955
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，我放学回家，她正在拖地。妈妈说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    这是新来的保姆阿巧，你们叫她阿姨。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我招呼了一声，想起了电影《一江春水向东流》里的老妈子，也是这身打扮，也叫阿巧。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    古猫灵，打铃，读须冇（读书了没有）？
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    她对我露出讨好的笑容，甜甜地冒出一串怪里怪气的话，我一个字也没听懂。很长时间，除了和她来自一个省份的妈妈能和她交流，没人知道她在讲些什么，后来习惯了，知道她讲的是以广东顺德话为本体，混杂着福建、上海、天津、北京话，还加上时不时蹦出来的洋泾浜英语，不过那英语在我们家很快就被禁止使用了，妈妈认为从阿巧嘴里讲出来的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    古猫灵
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    之类带有浓厚的殖民色彩。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿巧一来我家先发声明：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我喺
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ‘
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    梳起
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ’
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，十二岁就梳起咗。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    梳起
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    似乎和梳头有关，阿巧的头发永远是一丝不苟，一天要梳几次。先用油和水轻轻地拍过，再用篦子细细地拢几趟，双手背过去绕几绕挽上一个发髻，再从嘴里扯出发卡小心地一根一根别上。每次梳头都像在进行什么庄严的仪式，伏贴的头发像缎子一样闪闪发光。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    妈妈说在广东有一群女人叫
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    自梳女
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。她们称自己为
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    梳起
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，别人叫她们
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    妈姐
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。自梳女的历史有百年多了，一些村姑决意终身不嫁，年纪轻轻的就举行了梳头仪式，自行将长辫梳成发髻。女子自梳后不能再住娘家，于是她们就三五一伙，结拜金兰，离开家乡找活路，上到广州，下到香港，或者更远更远。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    自梳女们生活的全部希望和目的就是赚足够的钱，几个姐妹共同买一座房子租出去，等到年老体弱干不动时，就住在自己的房子里靠以前收来的租金互相扶持，养老待终。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    她们是最早的中国农村妇女独立自主的楷模。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    和现在的北京保姆有安徽帮、江苏帮一样，那时占据北京的保姆团体是广东帮，其中绝大多数是从顺德出来的。这批顺德女均为自梳女，阿巧就是她们中的一员。保姆们假期常常在八姑家聚会。八姑是北京顺德自梳女的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    大姐大
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，保姆们的头目。她给她们介绍东家，替她们排解纠纷，并及时为她们指明前进方向。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    八姑已经购置了自己的房地，一座大宅院旁边的一个独立小院。通往大院的月亮门已经用砖堵死，另外朝街开了一个小门。两间灰砖瓦房坐北朝南，一明一暗，原先是那个大宅院正北房东边的耳房，虽然没有正房那么高大，但小巧玲珑。正是晚春，院墙一角一棵大槐树的树荫撒满了小院，墙根一溜茉莉、绣球、指甲草、美人蕉把院子点缀得热热闹闹。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    保姆们手提点心盒子，怀揣红包，从四面八方赶来，个个打扮得平头正脸，油光水滑。月白的竹青的丝绸大襟褂、宝蓝的藏青的缎面宽腿裤熨得平平整整，穿得贴贴实实。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    正厅里靠北墙的红条长几上摆满了各色糕点，两张八仙桌拼成的长桌上已经摆满了十几样菜，几个保姆还在里里外外地忙着上菜。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    八姑坐在正席上，穿着雪白的丝绸褂，高高的额头闪着光亮，梳着发髻的头上没有一丝头发游离在外。她是一个中央部长级干部家里的保姆，也染上了一种高贵威严的气质：神情沉着、举止稳重，似乎她自己就是东家，令人敬畏。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    八姑是保姆的楷模，八姑的道路是每个保姆的必由之路。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿巧把我带到八姑跟前介绍道：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    这是我们事头（东家）的千金。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    她推推我让我说她预先教好的话
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    祝八姑长命百岁
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。我扭捏起来，无论如何都不开口，阿巧又尴尬又着急。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    八姑自己下台阶，把我拉过去，端详道：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    哏靓个女！
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
    <span class="Apple-converted-space">
</span>
</span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿巧高兴起来，赶紧接道：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    是呀，我最中意她了，蛾眉凤眼，鸭蛋脸，将来哽喺（一定）嫁个有钱女婿，一辈子荣华富贵。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我知道阿巧最喜欢我。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    八姑道：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    她将来出嫁，你跟着过去，也享享清福。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    八姑的话是金玉良言，阿巧看到了未来，笑得合不拢嘴，顺势亲了我一口，吐沫沾在脸上，我用手背擦了擦脸。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿巧自有她高兴的理由，她不用再愁那间养老的房子。阿巧走了一个捷径，到我们家干活的首要条件，就是包管她的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    生老病葬
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我家有自己的房子，爸爸用辛劳几年的稿费刚刚置办了一个漂亮的四合院。四面房屋一水青砖大瓦，鸟兽飞檐。高台阶抬起一溜北房，为客厅、饭厅和孩子们的卧室；月亮门通往西跨院的耳房，为妈妈的起居室；西厢房三间，是爸爸的书房和卧室；东厢房便给了阿巧。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    东房挨着大门，门洞占去了一间屋的面积，只有两间屋，给阿巧住。话说
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    有钱不住东南房
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，阿巧没有钱，一个人住东房两间已经很不错了，简直比八姑还要幸运。阿巧非常满意。屋子里一张床、一张桌子、两把椅子构成了阿巧的天地。她不必忧虑她的后半生，她的后半生与刘家休戚相联。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿巧确实是没有钱的，小时候父母早逝，跟哥嫂住，家里穷，七岁就被送到作坊里缫丝。十二岁上，在香港当佣人的乡里回家探亲，把她带进了观音庙。阿巧在菩萨面前拆开长辫，盘起发髻，穿上白衣黑裤
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ——
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    自梳女的装束，向菩萨叩拜起誓，完成了自梳的庄严仪式。然后自梳姐妹把她带进了城市，给大户人家当
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    住年妹
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    （十二岁以下只吃东家的饭，不拿钱，一年期满，再做良图），从此开始了佣人的生涯。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    至于后来漫长的时日，阿巧自己也说不清楚她是怎麽样从香港到福建，又上海、天津，最后来到北京的。她只知道，曾经攒下过几个钱，放在银行里，在几百圆一粒大米的通货膨胀时期，她所有的血汗钱变成了大便纸。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    来我家之前，阿巧在一个黄姓大官员家当老妈子，和这家人一起经历了他们的荣辱兴衰。后来这个大家子
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    主事的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    有的去台湾了，有的被镇压了，只留下几个孤儿寡母。被没收后剩下的一点点财产很快坐吃山空。没有收入，女主人还摆着大奶奶的款，起居饮食还要老妈子伺候着。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    从家道败落起，黄家再也没付过阿巧一分钱。阿巧是天生造就的仆人，具有中国传统故事中那些老家仆的最本质特征，在她脑子里只有一个牢固意识－－忠于主人。不论哪一任主人，不论他是飞黄腾达还是穷愁潦倒，只要是主人，她必是忠心耿耿，任劳任怨，有难同当。主人有难，阿巧倒贴，她心甘情愿地拿出了历届东家赏赐的金银首饰。黄家女人只说先欠着，将来一定给补上，也许她们坚信总有一天还会翻身做主人的。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    在黄家干了十几年，直到大家用尽了最后一分钱，再也没能力多摆一副碗筷时，阿巧才被打发走。临走时黄家女人给她写了一张债契，说欠何宝珠饷金两千万元（旧人民币，合现人民币两千元），却没说什么时候还。来我家时，阿巧的全部财产便是那一纸空文和几件随身的衣服。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿巧用这句话结束了她的奋斗史，这是阿巧的口头禅，阿巧说不出太完整的句子，只有这句话说得比较完整，使用得比较正确，发音比较接近普通话。

</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>

</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿巧不识字，来信是我代读，回信是我代写。那侄子在由乡下代写书信的老先生半文不白的信中，从打架坐班房、出狱盖房子、娶老婆生小孩，再到孩子上学、娶儿媳妇
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ……
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>

</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    老家苦哇
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。依旧是有信必回，每次回信只有那几句话：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    添衣加饭，出门戴帽。

<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    妈妈替阿巧打抱不平，她拿着黄家那张债契翻来复去地看，似乎要从字里行间找出那笔钱：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿巧哇，你多年的劳动就赚来这一张纸？
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿巧还替人家说话：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    他们现在没钱哪，冤公啊（可怜啊）。

<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    妈妈说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    常去催催，不催钱是回不来的。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    向主人逼债似乎有些大逆不道，但又放不下那两千万元的心思，到底她还是犹犹豫豫去了一趟旧主人家。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    呢嘟（这里），呢嘟！慢哋（慢点），慢哋！
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    旋即，阿巧开道，几个人尾随，吭哧吭哧抬着一口大缸挤进大门。不由分说，大缸便稳稳当当座落在四合院中央的葡萄架下。大缸茶叶沫釉，暗草绿色，四尺见方，还配有三块大基石，是明朝万历年间的宫廷珍宝。看那气势，就知道其主人家当年钟鸣鼎食之排场。黄家说就算是抵偿这些年的工钱。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    爸爸赞叹：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    好东西呀，好东西！
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿巧神采飞扬，笑容满面。她几十年的劳动结晶排排场场，显显赫赫地加入了主人的物业，为主人的四合院增光添色。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>

</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    大缸里放进十几尾金鱼，几丛郊外小河里捞的新鲜水草。于是登把椅子，趴在缸沿看鱼儿在水草中嘻戏，成了我们儿时的一大乐趣。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    一辈子当保姆的阿巧，也许命运本来不该如此。人生多变，不知在哪个坎节上就会出现转机，关键是有否眼光，能否抓住，一个失误，便是终生遗憾。阿巧心中确实有某些遗憾，在她的模糊记忆中，似乎放过了那么一两次改变命运的机会，突然想起来时，总是有一点不甘心。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    呷邦啦（吃饭啦）！
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿巧嘴里总是蹦出一些莫名其妙的非广东话喊大家开饭，她把菜从与饭厅和厨房相通的小窗口传进来，望着满桌亲手所做，又非自己享用的煎鱼烧肉（必须声明，父母给了她充分的权利和我们同桌吃饭，但是她绝不肯打破几十年保姆的规矩，不仅不和我们一起吃饭，而且把夹给她的菜留给我们，自己只吃剩菜，或把做菜时切下来的鸡头鱼尾菜帮子煮煮来吃，自觉地把自己摆在下人的位置），不知道脑子里哪一根弦又搭上了：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    佬上、汪上啊（刘先生，汪先生，阿巧对父母的称呼），那时我要是革了命，现在也像你一样是大嘎母了。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿巧总是喜欢在吃饭的这个当儿，东一榔头西一棒子地和父母聊一些国家或人生的大事。她说的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    嘎母
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    是
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    干部
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的滑稽发音。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    严格地说，父母不算
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    干部
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    干部
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    是一顶桂冠，只有那些流血流汗革了命，然后又排上一定级别的人才有资格佩戴。父母不过是
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    吃官饷
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    （阿巧语）的国家干部而已。妈妈望着她，想听听这个永远闹不清今昔是何年的阿巧怎么在想像中与主人在身分上平起平坐。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

</p>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">

<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>

</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">

<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

    阿巧讨厌男的，却为我还没有对象着急。那一年家里的人上干校的上干校，下乡的下乡，只有我在北京郊区工作，每星期日回家一天，身边最亲的人就是阿巧了。我已经二十三四岁了，婚恋史和阿巧一样，一片空白。那年头，家庭清白的未婚适龄男人稀罕得像沙漠里的水，所剩尽是些不清不楚者。阿巧四处张罗给我找对象，仿佛我是她嫁不出去的闺女。我不耐烦：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    你不是说男人臭吗。干嘛非要让我嫁人？
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我哋唔同啊，我哋唔同。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    一个星期六回家，阿巧兴冲冲跟进我的房间，左掏右掏，从最贴身处找出一个手绢布包，又从里面翻出一张照片。已经不止一次了，她给我介绍对象。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    你睇睇陈太个仔，几靓。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    她举着照片凑近我，急切地期待着我的反应。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我不屑地瞥了一眼，一个中年人，五观清秀，端端正正地望着镜头。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    读过大学，四十岁。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    都快成老头了，这年头男人金贵，如同沙漠里的水，还没有结婚的大龄男子必不简单，果然阿巧接着道，
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    他家以前是大官，他爸爸是陈ＸＸ。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    还是炫耀的口气。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我像听到炸弹爆炸，跳起来：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    陈ＸＸ？你发疯了，他是有名的大汉奸！连国民党都要枪毙他。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿巧有些尴尬地笑道：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我知道（她竟然也知道陈ＸＸ是汉奸），可是他早死了，有乜关系？再说，那个孩子极老实。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    再老实也不行，我不缺胳膊短腿，又不是地富反坏，凭什么找这么一个人。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我斩钉截铁，
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    绝对不行！
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我都同陈太讲好了，先见见再说啦。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿巧急得转圈。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    你告诉他，甭做这个美梦！
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我知道照片上那个人是无辜的，但我家父母已经有变节份子、反动学术权威一大堆帽子。我宁可一辈子不嫁，也不能再为父母为自己雪上加霜。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    男婚女嫁哪有那么复杂，不就是搬到一起，生儿育女吗？阿巧永远弄不懂这个世道。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我被心高气傲的爱情追求和世俗择偶标准弄得烦恼不堪，突然想到阿巧，觉得她确是不同凡俗，早早营造了一个感情真空的生活天地，无爱无欲，无忧无愁。从某种程度上说，她的一辈子是幸福的。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    那年寒秋，北京人被
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    一号文件
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    打发到全国各地，京城一下子萧条起来。我们一家人也呼隆一下子都离开了北京，自顾不暇更顾不上阿巧。阿巧的保姆帮会也指望不上了，八姑们都离开了主人家，不知道被打发到什么地方去了。没有人再雇佣阿巧，她只能靠拾破烂，卖废品度日。她还住在那个小厨房，从门外到屋里堆满了废纸、瓶子、铁丝、煤核儿，简直没法下脚。秋雨绵绵，屋里屋外充斥着被雨水浸泡过的纸张散发出来的霉味儿。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿巧头发变得干枯花白，参差不齐。面皮腊黄，目光呆滞，上牙更突出，人中更长，像个猩猩。她一早出门，推着一个捡来的小竹车去捡破烂，步履蹒跚，蓬头垢面，衣衫褴缕，五冬六夏总是用一块褪了色的，罩满灰尘的头巾包着头，用头巾的一角掩着鼻子。晚上，她坐在门口，把破烂分门别类，估算价值，废纸一斤两分钱，瓶子一个三分钱，冰棍棒二十个一分钱。一天好赖能挣上两三毛钱。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    最大的生意来自大字报，两分钱一斤，贱是贱，却有无尽的来源。阿巧很讲职业道德，大字报一定要让大家看完。哪天什么地方贴出了新的大字报，阿巧心里有一本明细账，一到第七天头上一早便匆匆奔赴现场。那些被太阳晒褪了色被风扯破了边角的大字报是阿巧的衣食父母。小车满载红红绿绿的纸张吱吱扭扭穿过大街小巷，阿巧的眼睛放着光芒。她会突如其来地向陌生人大声赞扬：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    文化大革命，嘿嘿，好！好！
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    人们以为她是疯子，不同她搭话。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿巧成为远近驰名的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    癫巧
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，其实她并不疯，我知道，只是脑子里没有一条线索把光怪陆离的世道和纷繁多变的人生串联起来。当她满足时，她很想向人们表达她零乱的思维中努力跟随时代潮流的那部分。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    周末回家，见阿巧瘦得皮包骨，我说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿姨，我们一起吃饭吧，包饺子。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿巧是南方人，可包饺子是一绝。虾肉和猪肉混合剁成碎丁，决不能用绞肉馅，用水打好，再加上冬菇碎末，味道鲜美极了。噢，我说的是五十年代。好多年没吃她的饺子了，在农村做梦都想吃一顿。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿巧兴冲冲买回两毛钱肉末，一大捆小白菜，正是小白菜臭街的时候，一分钱一捆。边走边对人说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我最爱吃大虾饺子。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    并不注意别人对她讪笑和侧目。其实阿巧说的不是水里游的虾，是
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    大馅
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    饺子。这么多小白菜，不
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    大虾
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    行么。我们两人切了所有的菜，皮擀得有碗口那么大，饺子包得像个大老鼠，一咬全是白菜，咯吱咯吱，像是在吃草。我满嘴白菜叶子，劝她道：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿姨，平时多吃点好的，补补身体。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    她满嘴白菜叶子，含糊道：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    冇钱那。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我问：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    卖废品的钱都干嘛啦？你还在给侄子寄钱？
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿巧顾左右而言他。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    大虾
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    饺子营养不够，我去买了鱼肉让阿巧做，她做菜没有了灵性，烧鱼炒肉随随便便扒拉几下就完事，缺油少盐，没有香味没有色彩。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我结婚了，搬出去住，不常见到阿巧。偶尔见时她已是脸色灰黄，头脚浮肿，心跳气短，看样子是心脏有病。不管别人怎么劝，她就是不去看医生。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    终于有一天阿巧倒下了，躺在那肮脏的小屋里，再也起不来了。那时我怀着七八个月的身孕，挺着个大肚子，上下跑腿，为她搞到一个
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    五保户
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的名额，每月国家提供十一元五角的生活费。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我生孩子了，一个儿子。出了月子我抱着孩子去厨房让阿巧看。门上挂着一把大锁，进不去。趴窗户往里面看，床上一堆破布棉絮，缓慢地、微微起伏，阿巧的身体埋在下边，苟延残喘。听邻居说，阿巧的身体越来越坏，什么也吃不下了，一天只喝一瓶牛奶。街道分配一些家庭妇女轮流照料她，她们每天只来一次，烧开水，热牛奶，倒尿盆，然后把门一锁，完事大吉。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我找到拿钥匙的人开锁进去，屋子里臭气烘烘。阿巧睁开惺忪的眼，含含混混地说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    你话佢哋总俾唔俾返我个屋（你说他们还还不还我的屋子了）？
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我安慰：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    大概政策快下来了。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    她的眼睛闪过一线亮光，枯瘦的指头碰碰儿子的小脸：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    等我起床了，给你带孩子。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1976
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    12
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    月
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    11
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    日，那天只有妹妹在家，她去看阿巧，只能隔着窗户往里看，门上永远挂着大锁，钥匙总不知道在谁手里。从窗户望进去，床上只有一堆零乱的铺衬，不见了阿巧。再往下看，阿巧脸朝下倒在门口的水池子旁，尿盆踢翻，屎尿一地，浸湿了她的裤腿。妹妹飞跑去找街道上那些管钥匙的老娘们儿，个个推说不知道谁拿着钥匙。好不容易找到人打开门，才发现她已经死了很久了，一天？两天？不知道，反正屋里的火炉已灭，暖瓶的水已凉，小锅里的牛奶没动。看样子阿巧死前大概渴极了，挣扎起来想到水池子接水喝，结果倒地不起。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    是妹妹给阿巧穿的老衣。阿巧消瘦的裸体第一次呈现在众人眼前。没有被男人触摸过的乳房干瘪地贴在肋条上，乳头小小的，粉红色，像少女的。曾经细心梳理的头发乱蓬蓬的，像一团烂草窝。阿巧的眼睛睁着，茫然空洞，她死也不明白，自梳女的道路怎么就走不通。阿巧的人中长长地包着上牙，相书上说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    人中长而深者，子孙满堂，年寿至高。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿巧死于六十三岁，无子无孙，无福无寿。妹妹给阿巧穿上了一件白色大襟褂，一条黑裤子，那是自梳女与男性世界隔离，终生守身如玉的庄严标志。妹妹说，她偷偷抹了一把泪，是世界上唯一一个为她的死流泪的人。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿巧去世时，我正带着儿子在贵阳躲地震，竟没有见她最后一面。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿巧的侄子闻讯赶来，一生一世第一次坐火车出乡下，把阿巧的房子折腾得天翻地覆。出乎意料的是，当侄子剥开阿巧的枕头套，一堆毛票散落下来，皱巴巴，脏兮兮，一毛两毛加起来竟有百十来块。捡垃圾的钱都藏在这里，想必是阿巧每晚枕着那堆钞票睡觉时，还在不屈不挠地作着拥有自己房子的梦。侄子欢天喜地地装起了钱，带走了阿巧所有的破衣烂衫和能拿在手上的东西。那些东西的价值，大概还不到火车票钱的十分之一。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿巧火化后，骨灰没人认领。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    居民委员会占据了阿巧的那间属于我家的小屋，卖掉了阿巧还来不及卖的废品，搬走了阿巧所有的劈柴、煤核。阿巧的家具也都充了公。那屋子那家具本来都是我家的，街道说何宝珠是五保户，吃了公家的，她家的一切都要归公。妈妈并不惋惜，说，都不要了，看着心酸。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    一两年后，
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    落实
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    房屋的政策终于出台了。政府对当初我们的私房的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    交公
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    做了补偿，给了两千元买走了四合院。像贡献清代大缸一样，我们的房子名正言顺地属于国家了。没有人去操心阿巧的私房，没人知道贵人关房子的门牌号码，更不知道另外两所房子在哪儿。活人的事都说不清，死了的就更搞不明白了。家里也没人再提起过阿巧借走的那三千元。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    夜深人静，阿巧经常入梦，入全家人的梦，在那个曾经属于我们和她的四合院里，忙忙碌碌地进进出出，语无伦次地大声嘈吵。四十多年了，顺德自梳女的骨灰早已不知飘散何方，无家可归的孤魂野鬼仍在北京上空游荡。四合院早已支离破碎，贵人关已经面目全非，十几层高的塔楼压在那长满蒿草的房子上。世道又变了，更让阿巧心乱神迷。只有那口明末茶叶沫釉大缸－－阿巧一生辛劳留下来的唯一见证－－依然稳坐皇宫内苑。或许阿巧会偶然兴发，漂游到那
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    194
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    号大缸边，坐在地上，一五一十数数今天又拣了多少冰棍小棒。但愿那时阿巧的灵魂暂得栖息。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    后记：今年，妹妹专程去了一趟广东顺德，通过公安局找到了阿巧侄子的后人，他们现在的生活都很富裕。他们非常热情地招待了妹妹。妹妹告诉他们，阿巧当年不仅以一生之力供养了他们的父母，还给他们留下了三所房屋的遗产。至于房子在哪，需要他们自己去寻找。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<b style="">
<font size="4">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
      延伸阅读：
     </span>
     阿巧菜谱
    </font>
</b>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阿巧离开我们多少年后，她做的菜一直是我们家人的精神食粮。甚至姐姐在病危时，什么东西也不能吃，还在一一回想阿巧的菜。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    冬瓜盅：把一个一公斤左右的冬瓜从蒂以下一寸横切一道，将籽瓤掏尽，里面装上冬菇虾米火腿蟹肉干贝，大火上屉蒸。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    炭火铜火锅：冬天用木炭点起红铜火锅。不似北方火锅把生肉带血端上桌，都是熟食，烫热来吃，便宜坊或浦五房买来的卤猪肘肉、酱牛肉、卤肝、鸡蛋皮包裹的肉糜卷等各种食物，切成薄片，还有自己做的肉丸鱼丸，加上笋片蘑菇白菜心冻豆腐。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    蝴蝶鱼：鳗鱼腹部横切，半寸厚段，酱油糖酒和花椒腌几小时，油煎，鱼段受热肉张开呈蝴蝶状。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    面包虾：面包片切成一寸见方，虾肉剁泥，加味料拌好，抹在面包上，蘸鸡蛋清，放在油里炸成金黄色。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    蟹卤面：螃蟹下来的季节，一买就是二三十只，吃剩一大堆螃蟹腿爪，把蟹肉剔出来，和鸡蛋木耳冬菇做成卤汁，拌面。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    清蒸鲥鱼：四寸长鲥鱼段，洗净，不去鳞片，稍加盐抹在表面撒上姜丝，大火蒸。蒸好后，撒葱丝，浇热油。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    烧鸭：半只鸭子，油煎后加酱油、酒、糖、五香粉烧，熟后切块。我去山西看插队的克阳时带了一只生鸭子，如法炮制，做得很好吃。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    罗汉斋：栗子、白菜、发菜、海米和豆干做的，鲜甜美味。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    八宝鸭：鸭子整只，内脏掏空，里面填上冬菇、海米、腊肉、香肠、莲子、糯米放在大碗里蒸若干小时。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    蒸冬菜肉：冬菜和肉末拌匀，大火蒸熟。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    咸蛋蒸肉：生鲜鸭蛋两个或三个，和肉末姜丝拌匀，大火蒸。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    白切鸡：嫩鸡大火煮开，换小火焖大约十几分钟。火候和时间非常重要，要刚刚断生，骨头里面还带血。切成块，就蘸水吃。蘸水是葱花、姜末、沙姜粉、盐和香油。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    茄盒：茄子去皮，切成斜边为一寸的正三角形，半寸厚。平切小口，塞入拌好的肉馅，蘸水淀粉油煎。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    炒茄皮：无论茄子怎样做都要去皮，但是茄子皮从来不丢弃，切细丝，与尖椒细丝一起炒。这种佐饭小菜比正菜更受欢迎，一上桌就被大家抢光。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    炸饹馇：饹馇是绿豆粉制作的大张饼，菜市场有卖的。切成条，大小和现在的薯条一样，煎炸，外脆里嫩。勾酸甜酱汁浇其上。最受孩子们欢迎。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    咖喱酥角：用大油和面一团，水和面一团，油面团放在水面团上，擀成片，卷起来再擀，如此三四遍，擀薄，切成两寸见方皮子，把咖哩粉洋葱丁将牛肉末细细炒过做馅，放在皮上对角捏合，捏成纽丝边，平锅放少许油，小火烤。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    腌曹白鱼：曹白鱼洗净，里外铺上大盐，用石头压上，几天后晾干，切块用油浸泡。吃的时候加姜丝蒸熟。妈妈最喜欢吃。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    肉粽：糯米、绿豆、海米分别泡半天以上，以肥肉为主的肉条子用五香粉和酱油腌半天到一天。三到五张粽叶并排铺在盘子上，铺一层糯米，一层绿豆，一层海米，放一条肉，一个生咸鸭蛋黄，再顺序盖以海米、绿豆、糯米，把粽叶包成五角状，小火煮一夜。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    猪肉包：不知道猪肉包是怎么做的，我从来和以后都没有吃过这么好吃的肉包，一口咬下去，饱和的油和汤汁顺嘴留下来，流了一手，一桌子。我琢磨了一辈子也没学会。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    腊肉饭：腊肉、香肠切丁，海米、冬菇切碎，和米一起焖饭。出锅拌半香葱酱油。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    燕皮馄饨：妈妈每早吃一碗，我眼巴巴地望着，妈妈就给我留一两个。不知道肉馅是怎么拌的，鲜香，有弹性。后来我也做过，做不出那个味道。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    萝卜糕：萝卜擦丝，虾干泡发，腊肉香肠切丁，和粳米粉加少许水拌在一起，蒸熟放凉，吃时切片油煎。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年糕：红豆煮熟。江米用小磨加水磨碎，加红枣红豆蒸熟放凉，吃时煎或蒸。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    八宝饭：红豆煮烂加糖，做成豆沙。糯米蒸熟，铺一层糯米铺一层豆沙，两层豆沙三层糯米饭，上面加核桃、枣、山楂糕条、红丝绿丝等，再蒸。这后来成了我的拿手活儿，每有聚会，必有此物，大伙儿一抢而空。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    芋头糕：芋头切成细丁，与香菇丁、腊肠丁、海米丁、五香粉、胡椒粉略炒，加米粉浆中拌匀，蒸一小时，放凉，吃时切片煎炸。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    煎堆：芝麻、核桃、花生仁、瓜子仁、杏仁等干果切碎，拌糖或蜜做馅，粘米粉做皮，包成圆球状，外面沾上芝麻，油煎。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    油角：大油鸡蛋和面，擀成薄皮。花生芝麻碾碎加糖做馅，包成饺子，边上捻成麻花状，油煎。用来招待客人和拜年时送朋友。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    蛋散：鸡蛋糖和面，擀片，切成两寸长、一寸寛条子，中间划一刀，一头从中间翻转。油炸。又酥又香。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    红果酪：冬天最喜爱的零食，红果从中间切开，挖核，煮烂加糖，放在外面冻冰凉。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    核桃酪：核桃仁泡水，用针挑去仁衣，和糯米加水细细地磨成浆水，红枣煮熟去皮剔核碾碎，熬至浓稠加少量糖。
   </span>
