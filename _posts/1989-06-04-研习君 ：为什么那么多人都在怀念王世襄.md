<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    为了捉兔捉獾，特意学着如何驯鹰驯狗，结果认识了两位爱鹰爱狗如命的朋友，其中一位，就是程砚秋的叔叔荣三爷。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS";'>

</span>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    架鹰的王世襄
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    翻开他的著作《锦灰堆》全三套，我们会发现并非想象中的那样，没有一件瓷器青铜器，除了明式家具外，全是一些不起眼、甚至很陌生的小玩意。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    一些所谓的收藏家对其嗤之以鼻，可王世襄对别人的评价也毫不在意，在他眼中，全世界就无一物不好玩，万物皆好玩。能够玩出这种境界，再找不到第二个人。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS";'>

</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    王世襄一生养鸽子，与众不同，别人玩的是新奇，他玩的是学问。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    为养一只鸽子，王世襄直接把养鸽的专家请到家里，同吃同住，天天泡在一起。最后把学到的经验，竟编了一本《明代鸽经清宫鸽谱》，成为养鸽者的必读之书。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    王世襄玩蟋蟀，别人玩的是赌博，是酒色财气，他玩的是真趣。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    为养好蟋蟀，他从全国各地图书馆和藏书家找来十七多种蟋蟀谱，逐段断句、改讹、勘误，还编成了堪称蟋蟀谱的百科全书：《蟋蟀谱集成》。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    王世襄玩葫芦，别人玩的是炫耀和显摆，他玩的却是等待。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    春天，他自己种下葫芦，然后就像冬天在等一场雪一样，他等一棵小苗长成葫芦满枝。最后还写了《读匏器》，在《故宫博物院刊》发表，这项濒临灭绝的传统技艺才得以传承。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS";'>

<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    王世襄的玩，不是玩世不恭，而是真的做学问。世间万物，只要到了王世襄手里，皆有情有义，皆栩栩如生，皆生机盎然。他读懂了物，物也读懂了他。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    启功先生说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    王世襄先生的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    玩物
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    是
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    研物
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，他不但不丧志，反而立志。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>

    马未都先生说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    王世襄的独特性就在于，他出身上层社会，却关注社会底层的乐趣，这在中国文人里是不多见的。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS";'>

</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    他能把一件起初并不为人重视的小东西、便宜的东西，经过自己的把玩和研究，重新赋予了更深文化的内涵，最终使小东西变成了重器。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    听着的确有点玄，因为能达到此境界的人，极少。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    王世襄是这方面的高人。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    王世襄自己说：人或称我收藏家，必起立正襟而对曰：
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>

   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    以我之家庭背景、个人经历，实不具备收藏家条件
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ……
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    大都掇拾于摊肆，访寻于旧家，自难有重器剧迹。在收藏家心目中，不过敝帚耳，而我珍之。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

</p>

</p>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    胡德生与王世襄先生
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    而我们当下的收藏风气，无不在追求藏品的升值与捡漏，固然，这都是收藏中的一部分乐趣。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我们的收藏起点可以是升值，可以是千千万万种理由，但是，如果终点还是赚钱、捡漏和一夜暴富，那就永远不会理解王世襄。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    他将玩的精神用自己的方式坚持对待，形成了自己的人生哲理，滋养并支撑着自己的生命。到最后，回首发现，处处充满着精彩。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS";'>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>

</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1939
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，最疼爱王世襄的母亲去世了，这给了他极大的震撼，直到这时，他才突然醒悟：玩了这么多年，我实在不该再愧对父母了。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    这个公子哥开始了人生的第一次转变，认认真真地坐在书桌前，发奋读书了。临毕业时，他写的毕业论文，题目是《中国画论研究》，获得了硕士学位。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    毕业后，北京已经沦陷，王世襄南下，来到中研院所在地，在这里他遇到了梁思成。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS";'>

</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    梁启超是王世襄的伯祖王仁堪的门生。梁思成便将他推荐给了研究所所长傅斯年先生，傅斯年只说了一句话：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    燕京毕业的不配到我们这里来。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    其实是傅斯年先生十分看不惯他身上的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    纨绔之气
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。知道他曾是少年玩主，一定难成大事。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    之后是梁思成收留了他，就是在中国营造学社，饱览了古建筑典籍，为日后从事髹漆，明式家具研究，打下了深厚的基础。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>

</p>
<p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS";'>

    《明式家具研究》英文本
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    真正让王世襄扬名的，是他那两本厚厚的巨著《明式家具珍赏》以及《明式家具研究》。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年，王世襄《明式家具珍赏》一书在香港出版，填补了中国人研究明式家具的空白。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    不久之后，《明式家具研究》问世，更是激起了收藏家研究明式家具的热潮。有的人甚至拿着《明式家具研究》为范本，到农村去收各式家具。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    王世襄先生几乎是以一己之力，推动了明式家具的研究与收藏。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

</p>
<p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS";'>

<p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS";'>

<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    除了
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    京城第一大玩家
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，王世襄在京城还有一个外号，叫
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    柜人
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，其一是因为
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    音同
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    贵人
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ；其二是因为王世襄家里堆的古代家具实在太多，晚上睡觉只能睡在柜子里。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1945
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年以后，王世襄骑着车，东拾西捡买家具。那段时间，邻居们总会看见王世襄骑着车出门，后面有个大架子。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS";'>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    他曾说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    那时候这些东西没有人要，当破烂，很便宜。并且贵的东西我买不起，我都是买便宜的东西。买回来，我请人修，恭恭敬敬请教，从中学到很多东西。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    王世襄认为他一生做的最有意义的事情有两件，第一件事情是日本投降后收回了几千件国宝，这些国宝现在都藏在故宫博物院。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    第二件事情是编写了文物研究著作《髹饰录解说》。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS";'>

</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    特殊经历
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，他竟然成了运动中要打的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    大老虎
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>

<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    轰炸
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”4
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    个月后，又被关到公安局看守所拘留审查
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    10
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    个月。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    在监狱遭受的非人折磨，让他精神和身体都受到了极大损伤，还染上了肺病，可他却说：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    不论我受到何种冲击，甚至是无中生有的污蔑，我决要求自己坚强、坚强、再坚强，只要活得长，一定能笑到最后。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS";'>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    审查进行了一年多，毫无证据，之后被取保释放，结果刚被释放回家，未曾想，这个曾经对故宫以终身相许的人，就收到原单位故宫的公函：故宫开除了他的公职。

</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

</span>

</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    王世襄的厄运还没有结束，

    1966
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年文革开始，本以为不问政治，就可以远离是非，没想到，他还是没能躲过那场浩劫。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

</span>

</p>
<p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS";'>

<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    王世襄先生在《明式家具珍赏》首发仪式上

<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>

   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    庙小妖风大，池浅王八多。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    为了保住自己手中的文物，王世襄

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

</p>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    马未都说：
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    王世襄对我最大的影响，仍然是他那种非常阳光的生活态度。他是一个对生活非常豁达的人，旧社会他可能是贵族身份，后来没落了，不断遭遇到打击，无论人生多么低谷，他一直都坚持一口气，坚持到他最后功成名就为止，这一点我佩服得五体投地。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    是啊，人生最可怕的事，是失去了对生活的热情和未来的信心。人真正的成熟，是经历了世态炎凉之后，知道了生活真相之后依然热爱生活。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的情景。

<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    生死永相匹
   </span>
<span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
