   痖弦，《痖弦回忆录》，江苏凤凰文艺出版社，。书令我们从人文的角度了解当代中国历史，了解台湾老兵。作者是台湾著名诗人。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    17
   </span>

   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">

   </span>
   我妈妈病危时，曾对她的好友四娘说：
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ‘
   </span>
   你如见到我娃明庭（我的小名），你告诉他，我是想他想死的！
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ’
   </span>
   硬是彻底的绝望！
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
   </span>
   历史上从来没有这么长、这么残忍的隔绝，绝对的隔绝，连书信都不通的。战争的惊心啊，小孩子不懂。到了中晚年，越想越伤心，越想越悲痛。尽管从军后的六十年在台湾日子过得还算平顺，但想到老家，想到亲人，那伤痛是永远无法痊愈的
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   。
  </p>

   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">

   </span>

  </p>

    李菁，《沙盘上的命运》
   </b>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   三联，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1917
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   太好看了。先在网上看到其中一篇
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   我的公公陈立夫
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
   </span>
   我们从小就熟知的，将中国人民的财产变为私产的
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   四大家族
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   之一，居然
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1950
   </span>
   年后在美国养鸡
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    20
   </span>
   年，太太做辣椒酱卖。文章根据陈立夫儿媳妇的口述写成。本书讲述了十多位民国时期风云人物的遭遇，其中最为精彩的篇章包括在台湾蒙冤坐牢
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    25
   </span>
   年的远征将军李鸿，跟随汪精卫而误入歧途陶希圣，另一位甚至丧命的曾仲鸣，西康王刘文辉等。由他们的后人讲述的故事自然只是一面之词，而他们大起大落的人生折射的历史令人扼腕。
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   作者的口述史写作堪称典范，看得出她在采访前做了很多功课，研读相关史料，成文后核对史实。李菁做编辑出身，克服了一般口述史拖泥带水的毛病。看过不少关于汪精卫的文章和书，有些谜团要靠推测才能明白七、八分。本书写
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1938
   </span>
   年的汪精卫，了了几页，非常有说服力。
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   抗战之后内战，同一批军队官兵八年之后，未能解甲归田，疲敝不堪地举起枪来，对另外一群中国人。作者自然无法去解读这段历史，书的第一章：一寸山河一寸血，分别描写抗战中三大战役、战场。讲述远征军的
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    60
   </span>
   页，是本书最长也最下功夫的一篇。对内战，则只讲了不同人的命运。那是我们可以想得到，而至今不能细细审视的一段历史。
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <b>
    刘齐，《我的串联生活》
   </b>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   深圳报业集团，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    2017.
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   文革开始时，作者是一名沈阳的初中学生。作者以当时的日记为依据，加以他对细节形象的描写，让我们从一个少年人的视角，看到中国那场
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   史无前例
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   的变故，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
   </span>
   留下被当今大多数过来人因不堪回首而渐渐遗忘的往事。这位
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   一张白纸
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   样的少年人，在封闭的空间里，在当时教化下形成的的观点、思想和情绪，今天看来匪夷所思。而同时又那么积极向上，充满激情。一切像假的，一切都是真的。悼念哥哥去世一周年的文章是这样结尾的：愿哥哥，黄泉敬先辈，再学马克思。
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   作者的父亲是沈阳日报副总编辑，文革开始就被打成黑帮。他这个黑崽子被同学打个半死，一点没有影响他从
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    13
   </span>
   岁到
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    23
   </span>
   岁对领袖的无限崇拜，并成为一颗超级合格的革命螺丝钉。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1975
   </span>
   海城地震，他被从工厂抽调去参加抗震救灾，日记中写道：
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   再一次决心：为抗震救灾斗争豁出自己全身肉，满腔血，生命不息，战斗不止！前几天，觉睡得太多了（平均六小时左右）从今天开始，要再减太一小时，抓紧工作，抓紧斗争
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   他的日记，当时的文章中充满这样的表白，今天读来好像滑稽小品。然而也有它的延续性，例如那些三什么，四什么，五什么
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    …
   </span>
   大概要等历史翻过一篇回头看，才会觉得啼笑皆非。
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   书中处处暗藏玄机，妙不可言。
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1969
   </span>
   年初中生刘齐到辽北山区插队，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   我最关心的战争区域有两个，一个是越南，一个是珍宝岛。越南太远，全国盼望打战的知青那么多，再需要人也轮不到我。珍宝岛就不同了，据说苏修
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    T62
   </span>
   坦克的速度极快，从边境开到我默默插队的辽北山区，也就是一阵风就到了。天将降大任于斯人也，去你妈的镰刀锄头
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   。
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   他常常当时严肃的事后面的荒谬巧妙地揭示出来：
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   那时的大寨，简直就不是个村子，是圣地！大寨农民种的好像也不是庄稼，是经验，是典型。印象中，除了毛主席太忙，林副主席机毁人亡之外，国家领导人几乎都到过大寨
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   。可圈可点。
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   作者后来上了大学，去了美国，再回到中国。他对历史，政治和社会的认识贯穿书中，耐人寻味，而没有居高临下的说教，因为他
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   批判
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   的对象，是青少年时代的自己，他带着理解和宽容，看待幼稚、虚荣等等人性的弱点，让读者看到民众如何被社会运动所利用。
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   他从骨子里的幽默，经历了土、洋插队，炼得炉火纯青，令人一边读一边笑。
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   书的开篇讲到四姐弟假扮成同学，参加串联的故事。像那时许多年轻人一样，哥哥将自己的名字改为刘卫东，即保卫毛泽东的意思。弟弟对他伪造的串联介绍信十分佩服：
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   这四点太妙了，刘卫东真不白给，又机智又英勇。毛主席啊毛主席，你往沈阳市和平区这边看一看，你真该也让刘卫东来卫一卫
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   书中不乏点睛之笔，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   文革
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   再厉害，终于压不垮人们对正常生活的渴望。魔鬼的巴掌捂得再严，总有漏光的所在。这个光，就是人性
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   。从未读过对文革经历如此精妙的描绘。
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
