</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">

<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    他死了。是累死的。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    才活了
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    48
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    个年头。他不该死。但只有他死后，周围的人们才开始认识他，理解他。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    在数学王国的无数分支里，他也是一路冠军呢。但在相当长的时间里，国内没有几个人认识理会他。这与中国女排在夺得世界冠军之后，那种万众鼓噪，举国欢腾的场面相比，太寒碜了。倒是西方数学家首先承认了他，为之折服倾倒。可他，不待金牌挂在胸前，就一声不吭地离开了人间！
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    陈景润，谁不知道，因为摘下数学王冠上的一颗明珠－－
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    歌德巴赫猜想
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    而名扬天下。他，也和陈景润一样，摘下了数学王冠上的另一颗明珠－－
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    斯坦纳系列
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。这是一道
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    130
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    多年攻之不下的著名数学难题。陆家羲耗尽毕生之力，巧妙地设计了一系列的递归构造，严谨地证明了互不相交的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    V
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    阶斯坦纳三元系的大集。除
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    6
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    个值外，对所有或，
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    都存在，从而宣告了这一问题的整体解决
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    (
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    对于
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    6
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    个例外值的处理，他已有的腹稿，因不幸去世，仅留下一份提纲和部分结果
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    )
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。国际组合数学界的一些权威人士认为，这是
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    20
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年来组合设计中的重大成就之一。这一成就将广泛地应用于计算机科学、空间技术、通讯网络、人工智能、概率统计、信息编码，以及物理、化学、生物学、遗传学等各门现代科学之中。而在世界数学史上，必将写上以下的文字：中华人民共和国，包头市九中，陆家羲博士。
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ……
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    1
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1961
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年夏天，陆家羲从东北师范大学物理系毕业了。他被分配到包头钢铁学院任教。这时，他
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    27
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    岁了，唯一的亲人母亲已故去，他伶仃一人。他穿着打补丁的衣服，鼻梁上架着深度近视镜，由于营养不良和用功过度，身架细高，端正的脸庞呈现出菜色。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    陆家羲来到兴建中的包头市，背着个黄布挎包去包头钢院报到。在同事的眼中，他近乎迂腐。平时不苟言笑，神情淡泊，行动安稳，是一个文弱寡言的书生罢了。谁又曾料到，在他那陈旧的挎包里，装着一座数学世界的高峰呢。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    还是在陆家羲考入大学前后，酷爱数学的他，买到一本孙振瀛先生著的《数学趣引》。在这本书中孙先生介绍说，西方大数学家寇克曼于
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1850
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年提出一个问题：
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    某寄宿学校有
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    15
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    名女生，她们经常每天
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    3
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    人一行散步，问怎样安排，才能使每个女生同其他一个女生同一行中散步恰好每周一次？
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    自然，这貌似浅显的问题的实质及其价值是非常深奥的。这就是著名的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    寇克曼女生问题
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，是
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    100
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    多年来世界无人能解的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    100
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    多个数学难题中的一个。此外，与之相联的还有个
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    斯坦纳系列
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    问题，也是数学世界未解的难题之一。刚刚走进大学的陆家羲被这道题深深地吸引住了，情不自禁地投入了它的怀抱。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    在大学里，他度过了如醉如痴的
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1400
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    天。他废寝忘食，埋头钻研，因而改变了他原本开朗活泼的性格。终于，在毕业前夕，他基本解决了
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    寇克曼女生问题
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    ”
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    现在，他走上了新的工作岗位，正准备向有关部门汇报他的科研成果。在他淡漠的外表下，内心正充满着激动和对未来的憧憬。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    他没料到，会遇到那么多莫名其妙的不幸。
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
</span>
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    1961
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    12
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    月
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    30
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    日，他把题名《寇克曼系列和斯坦纳系列制作方法》的论文寄给中国科学院数学研究所。一年过去后，回信来了，寄回论文和一些新资料，说
   </span>
<span class="s2" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; font-kerning: none;">
    “
   </span>
<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    如果结果是新的，可以投稿。
   </span>

<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">

   </span>

<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">

</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>

</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

</span>

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 19px;">
