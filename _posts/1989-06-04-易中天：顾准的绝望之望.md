  </p>
  <p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS";'>

  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    顾准（
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1915.7.1
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    －
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1974.12.3
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ）。中国当代学者，思想家，经济学家，会计学家，历史学家。第一次提出了在社会主义条件下实行市场经济。后来，他两次被打成
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    右派
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，仍然坚持理想和信念不动摇。在逆境中，他写下了《希腊城邦制度》和《从理想主义到经验主义》等著作，对革命胜利后所遇到的问题进一步进行了思考和探索。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1974
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    11
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    月
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    11
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    日，顾准被确诊为癌症晚期，癌肿大如鸡卵，卡在心脏与气管之间，并已扩散，实际上已无法医治。这时的顾准，可真如批判他的那些人所言，只有
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    死路一条
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    了。对于顾准这样的革命者来说，死原本不足畏。甚至，由于他多年来受尽苦难受尽折磨，死亡于他，可能还是一种解脱，至少不比生来得沉重。然而顾准却死不暝目。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    因为直到临终那一刻，他的五个子女没有一个来看他。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    顾准的子女和他正式断绝关系，大约是在
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1967
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年底。此前，同年
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    1
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    月
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    18
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    日阴历小年夜，妻子汪璧已提出离婚，在家的孩子也开始不再理他。这当然是因为顾准第二次被打成
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    右派
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，并且成了
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    极右派
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，而
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    文化大革命
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    已全面展开，政治形势变得更加严峻。在这个人人朝不保夕的日子里，顾准继续留在家中，只会给这个家庭带来更大的灾难。考虑到
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “1957
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    年以来我欠下这个家庭这么多债，以后不应该再害亲人
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，顾准同意了妻子和子女的要求。而且，说实在的，他不同意也得同意。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p3" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    <img alt="31.jpg" border="0" height="291" src="/medias/contents/5604/31.jpg" width="399"/>
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    但，藕虽断，丝相连；人还在，心不死。离开家庭孤身一人过着形影相吊生活的顾准，无时无刻不在思念着妻儿。他甚至痴情到这种程度：刚刚挨完批斗，擦一把脸，便抓紧时间搞翻译，还天真地幻想着今后能用这些稿费补贴子女。至于一次次的找寻，一次次的联络，一次次的托人传话，就更不在话下。现在，他已经病入膏肓，行将就木，就是想
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    害人
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    也害不了啦！在这人生的最后日子里，他多想看看自己的子女呀！哪怕只看一眼也好啊！
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    被老友陈易称为
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    英雄肝胆，儿女心肠
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的顾准，此刻几乎只剩下这唯一的一个心愿了。他的另一个心愿－－完成宏大的研究计划，已无法实现。但不能再写作，是没有法子的。再见子女一面，总是可以想办法的吧？这个念头如此的强烈，以至于顾准咬紧牙关，又做了一件违心的事。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    在顾准被确诊为癌症晚期后，在他朋友们的强烈呼吁下，经济研究所决定给他摘掉
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    右派
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    帽子，但前提条件或者说必须履行的手续，则是在一张预先写好
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    我承认，我犯了以下错误
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ……”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的认错书上签字。这对顾准，无异奇耻大辱，同样将死不瞑目。因此，尽管来人反复说明，他们完全出于好意，顾准仍倔强地表示，承认错误是万万不能接受的。他不需要、也不在乎摘什么帽子。但是，当他听朋友们说，
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    如果你摘了帽，子女们就会来看你
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，顾准忍痛含泪用颤抖的手签下了这个死都不肯签署的文件。他流着泪对骆耕漠、吴敬琏说：我签这个字，既是为了最后见见我的子女，也是想，这样也许多少能够改善一点子女的处境。这可真是
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    可怜天下父母心
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ！顾准的这份痴情实在感天动地，就连经济所
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    革委会
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的负责人也动了恻隐之心，去信给顾准的幼子，要他们来医院护理。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    然而得到的答复是：不来，不来，就是不来！顾准的幼子顾重之
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    (
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    一个才二十出头的年轻人
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    )
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    回信说：
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    在对党的事业的热爱和对顾准的憎恨之间是不可能存在什么一般的父子感情的。

   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，但他不能不说。因为他在商城县之所见，不是什么
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    形势大好
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，而是哀鸿遍野，人争相食。他亲眼看见老百姓一个个被活活饿死，生计无着，求告无门。如果他不说出来，天良何在？
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    不能说，又不能不说，这是矛盾所在，也是痛苦的根源。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    这种痛苦于知识分子尤甚。因为知识分子非他，乃是社会的良知与良心。如果知识分子发现了社会的错误，看见了社会的不公，也装聋作哑，视而不见，充耳不闻，甚至昧着良心说假话，那就愧称
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    知识分子
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，没脸在世上做人。但是，面对社会的错误和不公，知识分子又是最无能为力的。百无一用是书生。他一无权，二无势，三无财，四无力，肩不能挑手不能提的，又能干什么？唯一能做的，也就是把眼之所见耳之所闻心之所想说出来吧？叫他不说，哪里做得到？
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

   </span>

  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    因此，该说还得说。而且，还得说真话，不说假话。这里说的
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    真
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，不是真诚，而是真实。
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    文革
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    中，有多少人
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    真诚
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    假话
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    ，更为
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    真诚
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    。显然，真实才是更重要的。你可以不把所有的真实都说出来，但说出来的必须真实，这也是一条底线。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    文革

   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    2

   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    极右派

   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>

  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>

   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">

   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    的确，顾准是不幸的。直到含冤去世，都没能见上子女一面，也没能见到睽违十载的老母亲。那时，他的慈母就住在公安部大院，距顾准的住处只有一街之隔，却彼此望穿双眼不能一见。在那个不见天日的年月里，妻离子散家破人亡的顾准，只能孤身一人在无边的黑暗中蜷缩着身体舔食自己的鲜血、泪水和耻辱。顾准又是幸运的，他有那么多关心他爱护他的好人。在他两次落难之后，是中国科学院经济研究所两次收留了他。这实在是顾准不幸中之万幸。经济所是一个人才荟萃的地方。张闻天、孙冶方、骆耕漠、狄超白、林里夫、巫宝三，还有年轻的张纯音、吴敬琏，皆为一时之选。更重要的是，他们都是正直正派重感情的人。这就造成了一个奇迹：当人与人的关系变得比狼与狼的关系还不如时，顾准的周围却散发着人间的温暖。没有这温暖，顾准活不下来，也不可能留下那么丰富的思想遗产。
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    事实上，正如王元化先生所说：
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    人活着不仅需要使自己温饱，还需要精神养分，而友情就是其中的一种。
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    也正如王元化先生所说，顾准能获得如此之多的友情，
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    证明中国有些人纵使处在最恶劣的环境下，仍旧良心未泯，他们心中那朵正义的火焰始终在燃烧
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”[
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    《〈顾准全传〉序》］。是啊，没有良知、良心、正义感，就不会有什么真正的友情，更不会有知识者和思想者的友情。作为一个知识分子，知识固然重要，人品就更重要。顾准的幸运，就在于他遇到的人，不但学问好，而且人品好。在这些人看来，他们在顾准危难之时伸出的援手，不过是一个正派人该做的事，是应当应分的。正如事隔多年以后骆耕漠接受采访时淡淡地说的：
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    “
   </span>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    做人嘛，应该这样。
   </span>
   <span class="s2" style='font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; font-kerning: none;'>
    ”
   </span>
  </p>
  <p class="p1" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; min-height: 19px;'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
   </span>
   <br/>
  </p>
  <p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 16px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   <span class="s1" style="font-kerning: none;">
    做人，才是最根本的啊！
   </span>
