     不要把梅兰芳抬得太高

<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">

<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>

    －－作者：章诒和
   </b>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">

</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   中国新闻周刊：为什么在你的《伶人往事》里，从来没有试着写梅兰芳？
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   章诒和：梅
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    (
   </span>
   兰芳
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    )
   </span>
   是个有故事、无往事的人。怎么讲呢，由于梅的个性和位置，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1949
   </span>
   年以后，事实上他的时代已经结束了。所以他那之后的那些事，都是例行的故事，都是大家知道的，而不具备个人回忆的往事意味了。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   如今陈凯歌说梅，他不是要抬高梅，而是用梅来拯救他自己，他想打梅这张牌，但梅不需要他打，梅自己就是张立起来的牌。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   我和陈凯歌是两个方向的，他写虚，我写实，他追求美，我追求真。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   很多人都问我，写不写梅，我说我要看到档案，我知道这些
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    (
   </span>
   事
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    )
   </span>
   ，只是因为私人关系。但我觉得远远不够。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<img alt="100.jpg" border="0" height="446" src="https://i.imgur.com/LuwC0yI.jpg" width="360"/>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   写梅兰芳最主要要对梅先生有非常非常深的感情。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   艺人是非常神秘的一群人，他们和所有的群体都不一样。他们只给你看台上和场合中的一面，私下里的这一面，他是不给你看的。他不仅不给别人看，他也不说。除非像我已经扎到他们圈子里了，而且和他们特别好，然后还有一个父辈的这种关系，那么你可能知道一点点，但远不是他的全部。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   艺人只能告诉你一点点，再不然，告诉你的就是假的。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   中国新闻周刊：看梅孟
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    (
   </span>
   小冬
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    )
   </span>
   之间的感情，总觉得就像梅中正平和的舞台艺术，虚大于实，现实中梅真是这样吗？
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   章诒和：梅一辈子讲究中正平和，在舞台上、在现实中他知道什么是过犹不及。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   孟当初和梅碰头时，孟
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    18
   </span>
   岁，孟着实是好，但还未大红大紫，观众爱看他俩游龙戏凤。两人在王克敏的半百生日宴上认识，这时福芝芳
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    (
   </span>
   梅兰芳的二太太
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    )
   </span>
   已经是那么多孩子的妈了。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   婚姻是要撮合的。梅喜欢孟，孟也仰慕梅，但梅对孟，也仅仅是喜欢而已，梅从没主动追求过谁。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   所以要把梅写成情种，那也错了。渐渐地两人就有了摩擦，孟抽大烟，梅家是不能有这种嗜好的；做了太太，必须要做家眷，孟还是要抛头露面；不能登台，孟背着梅去唱戏。不停地事出来。所以孟对于梅既是新爱又是个负担。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   事情发展到后来，梅非常清楚，必须要了断，要不全完！
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   分手前，梅在武汉打了一个双台，完了和身边人说，今这台是白打了，这四万两白银是给那边
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    (
   </span>
   孟
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    )
   </span>
   准备的。也好，这事就这么了了吧。这是梅当时的心情，有些许失落，更有如释重负。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
<img alt="101.jpg" border="0" height="368" src="https://i.imgur.com/9yU6EZg.jpg" width="550"/>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   梅一生和谁都很和气，只负过孟，但那也是世事中的不得已。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   一分手，孟就拜了余叔岩
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    (
   </span>
   当年京剧老生最高水平
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    )
   </span>
   ，开始大红大紫，成了冬皇。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   大家都喜欢福芝芳，她是真为梅好，人又大气，爽快，能干，也能生。艺人是必须要靠血缘来传承的。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   这一点，梅怎能不懂？
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   中国新闻周刊：你怎么评价梅兰芳？
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   章诒和：首先不要把梅
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    (
   </span>
   兰芳
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    )
   </span>
   抬得太高，他不是一个英雄，他就是个艺人，而且绝顶的聪明。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   同样是艺人，我们把梅和程
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    (
   </span>
   砚秋
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    )
   </span>
   放在一起看，就能多少看到梅的实了。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   程和梅不一样，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1949
   </span>
   年解放时，程觉得高官真捧他，真把他当人看，给了他很高的地位，因为以前唱戏的地位是很低的。他很感动，特积极，让他去哪儿从不含糊。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   这些梅可没有。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   但程马上就傻了，戏改后发现最后只能唱
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    7
   </span>
   出戏。戏子就是戏子，以戏为生，以戏为命。这个打击是致命的，所以程一生都生活在悲情之中。程那种悲凉，觉得人生非常没意思，不想唱戏，绝对不让自己的孩子唱戏。这贯穿他的一生。你要说脱俗，程先生比梅先生脱俗多了。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   所以
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    30
   </span>
   年代他到德国不想回来，他把银子都搬过去，就想在那边居家度日了。但这边一个大角儿养的一群人
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ——
   </span>
   养他的和他养的，全急了，首先就是他太太。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   而梅呢，他在大问题上，非常听话，因为他知道周围都是高人，都比自己强，他要靠他们来抬和帮。这一圈子的人还互相掐架，但梅先生都能摆平。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   这些梅党就是爱他，爱艺术，所以别再过多地投入到同性恋的狭隘想法中。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   梅党不是要求和梅做什么、或是捏捏他的脸蛋，那都不对。不是没有玩男旦的，但
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   梅、程、尚
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   ，全都没有。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   梅通过梅党，他艺术上集中了多少人的智慧，而且他能体现出来，化成自己的。一出戏下来，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   走，咱们吃饭去
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
   。席间您就得给畹华讲讲戏唱得怎样，给他挑刺。所以你看梅多聪明，第二天人家就改。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   梅先生是这么个人，不分党派，不分左右，不分穷富，只要您真有本事，真为我好，我就掏心掏肺。梅党这些人，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1949
   </span>
   年后基本都倒了，但梅先生依然如故。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   中国新闻周刊：你怎么看梅兰芳与程砚秋对戏改的不同态度？这可以看出他们哪些不同的性格？
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   章诒和：梅对于形势的把握又是聪明至极，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1949
   </span>
   年后，梅兰芳不同意戏曲改革的那一套，但这件事让他碰了钉子，差点被组织批判。当时，他在家里说，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   敢情
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    49
   </span>
   年后咱还是不能随便说话
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”——
   </span>
   这他立刻就总结出来了。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   我们这些人都知道他那份聪明，我父亲就在家里说，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    “
   </span>
   罗隆基还不如一个畹华！
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    ”
   </span>
</p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   所以虽然
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1949
   </span>
   年后梅被抬到一个很高的位置，但做不了太多的事情。这些他心里非常清楚，所以之后就没再做什么事了。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   但一些政治讲话，梅是必须要发言的。那个处境，他是个头靶，是个旗子，他得亮相，所有的稿子都是别人写的。只要他登台亮相，他就是个大青衣！
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   其实梅对戏改是大有看法的，
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1949
   </span>
   年以后，用运动的方式推行文化政策，一种是文改另一种是戏改。而首当其冲的就是他们，这叫旧艺人，梅清楚极了。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   而程的悲情就在于看不透，他的戏改建议绝对正确。要知道梅程都唱过好几百出戏，他们不断地唱不断地丢，为什么丢，因为觉得不好。最后留下很多很精致的，各种改良的成就都在保留剧目里。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   中国这些顶级艺人的智慧不是一般人能比的，每天都是三教九流，政治的、经济的、文人墨客、得意的、失意的。他把中国上上下下，左左右右都看得明白着呢。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   所以
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    1949
   </span>
   年后，梅是看透的。这也是解梅这个人的关键，为什么他入了党，却放着每月
   <span class="s1" style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica;">
    7
   </span>
   千的工资不拿呢，这叫了得。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   但梅确实比程胆小。程多豪迈，大胸怀，那工夫，太漂亮了。要是你，爱梅还是程？我是爱程。
  </p>
<p class="p1" style="margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: Helvetica; min-height: 20px;">
<br/>
</p>
<p class="p2" style='margin: 0px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 17px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";'>
   到后来程完全放开了，大碗酒大碗肉，那酒都是白酒。梅心情也不是很好。他俩一走，家班剧社都不复存在了，从此以后就没有角儿了。
  </p>
